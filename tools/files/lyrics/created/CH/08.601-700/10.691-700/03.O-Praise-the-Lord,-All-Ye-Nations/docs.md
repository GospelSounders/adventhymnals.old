## 693. O PRAISE THE LORD, ALL YE NATIONS

```txt
1.
O praise the Lord, all ye nations: 
Praise Him, all ye people. 
For His wonderful kindness is great toward us: 
And the truth of the Lord endureth forever. 
Praise ye the Lord. 
Amen.
```
