## 697. WITH PRAISE, O GOD, WE WORSHIP THEE

```txt
1.
With praise, O God, we worship Thee; 
With song and prayer we bow the knee, 
Accept our love, receive our praise; 
Direct our ways, throughout our days. 
Amen.
```
