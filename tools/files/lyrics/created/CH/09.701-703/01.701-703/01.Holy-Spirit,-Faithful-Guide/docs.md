## 701. HOLY SPIRIT, FAITHFUL GUIDE

```txt
1.
Holy Spirit, 
Faithful Guide, 
Lead us heavenward 
And abide; 
How we need 
Thy guiding ray! 
Lead us home, 
We pray.
```
