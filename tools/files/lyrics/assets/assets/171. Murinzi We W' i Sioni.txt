171. Murinzi We W' i Sioni

1.
Murinzi we, W' i Sioni, Ijoro rigeze he? 
Mbes'umusek' uri hafi? Ntiburatandukana?
Ntacy' ushishoz' iburasirazuba? 
Ntacy' ushishoz' i burasirazuba?

2.
Nyamuneka tumenyeshe, K' urugendo rushize; 
Mbesetugeze ku cyambu, Tugiye kugerayo?
Mbese koko, ubwami burihafi?
Mbese koko, ubwami buri hafi?

3.
Burakey' umuns' uraje! Tubyamamaze hose;
Dor' izubarirarashe, Mwese nimurirebe! 
Mwa bera mwe, Mugiyegucungurwa!
Mwa bera mwe, Mugiye gucungurwa!

4.
Tugeze ku rugabano, Rw' igihugu tujyamo,
Nimukabuzetwihute, Icyambu kiri hafi. 
Nimurangurure ya majwi yanyu!
Nimurangurure ya majwi yanyu!
