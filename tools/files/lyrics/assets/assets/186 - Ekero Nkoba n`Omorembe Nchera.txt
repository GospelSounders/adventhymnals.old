186 - EKERO NKOBA N 'OMOREMBE NCHERA
"When Peace Like A River"


1
Ekero nkoba n`omorembe nchera,
`Bororo bun` emerindo `Se kende gionsi aye kwanyorokirie,
Goteba nga Mbuy` as` enkoro.

Chorus
Imbuya. . . `S`enkoro;
Imbuya Imbuy` as` enkoro.

2
Nonya Saitani naisa gontema,
Ninyeremie ekiagera Kristo ororir` obwororo bwane,
Okure as` ebibe biane.

3
`Bibe biane bionsi gose tai bike,
Biabambirw` Omosalaba Tinkoragererigwa as`ebibe, Motogi` Omonen` Omoy`one.

4
Omonen` ayereri` ogocha kwao,
`Goro rioba ndimanoke, `Konu nebug` ogocha Kw` Omonene,
Nonyaigo mbuya as`enkoro.
