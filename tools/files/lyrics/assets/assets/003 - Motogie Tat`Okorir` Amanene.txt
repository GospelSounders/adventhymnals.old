003 - Motogie Tat`Okorir` Amanene


1
Motogie Tat`okorir` amanene,
Agancha`ens agato` Omwan`oye,
Akerua gokwa atosibi` ebibe,
Oyoranche anyore Obogima.

Chorus
Mobake `Monene, Ense emoigwe,
Mobake `Monene, Abanto bagoke:
Inchwo ase Tata Yes` abarete,
Momotogie okorir` amanene.

2
Okoborigwa ogoikeranu,
Oyobwanchire nare na eira
Mosari`onde bwensi oramwegene,
Kegim`ere nanyore okwaberwa.

3
Okorire na kwegeri`amanene,
Twagokire ime y`Omwana Yeso,
Nkob`ogokumia `bochenu, `bonene,
Tomoroche gator`as` orogendo.
