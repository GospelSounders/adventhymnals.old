244 - TOIROKA ABABISA
  "Be not Dismayed"

1
Toiroka ababisa, Nyasae nakorende;
Ins`ase chimbaba chiaye, Nyasae nakorende.

Chorus
Nyasae nakorende, Kero gionsi nchera chionsi, Igo nakorende, Nyasae nakorende (nakorende).

2
Kero ki` amatuk`amabe, Nyasae nakorende; Nony` as` obororo bwonsi, Nyasae nakorende.

3
Nako` okogania kwao, Nyasae nakorende; Takwang` ogosaba kwao, Nyasae nakorende.

4
As` amatemw` ande onsi, Nyasae nakorende; `Morosu eegek` asare, Nyasae nakorende.
