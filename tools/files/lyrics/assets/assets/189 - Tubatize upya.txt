189 - Tubatize upya
"Baptize Us A New"

Mimina upya nguvu toka juu; 
Tupe pendo lako, ewe Mwokozi.

Chorus

Tubatize upya, kwa Roho leo.

3
Kipaji cha juu, kitume kwetu, 
Tubariki sasa, utufariji.

4
Isikilize, kwa moyo wazi, 
Sauti ya Roho; ubarikiwe.
