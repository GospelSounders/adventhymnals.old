137 - IGWA RING'ANA RIA NYASAE OITO
"Hear The Words Of Scripture"


1
Igwa ring`ana ria Nyasae oito, Reta chitaisi as`emonga; 
Karue chinkoro chiano chionsi bori, mwegen`eira y`ebisio.

Chorus
"Morete chitaisi as`emonga, Montem" Otebir `Omenene;
"Nimbatwererie ebisi`ebinge, mono goetani`ebi moganetie.

2
Norigeti` ornanye `nguru chi`omoika, Reta chitaisi as`emonga;
Menya n`Ere buya chingaki chionsi, Gakoru`eira y`ebisio.

3
Kwamocheir` Omonene oo gose? Reta chitaisi as`emonga;
Reta bun`erikir`ime y`ebuku, `re nagoiterer`ebisio.

4.
Mosabe ner`Omonen` Omorwoti, Kamokoreta chitaisi asare; 
Egen`eir`okobaka koigweke, Korw`enkoro y`omwegeni.

5
`Mater`aigweke `goro as`amare, Kamwaretire chitaisi emonga; 
Amatera y`omogok`aigwekane, Ntore neira Y`ebisio.
