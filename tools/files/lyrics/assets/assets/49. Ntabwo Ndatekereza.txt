49. Ntabwo Ndatekereza

1.
Ntabwo ndatekereza, Kw' iwac' ari bugufi. 
Uko bukeyenegereza, Gusohora mw' ijuru.

Gusubiramo:
Hafi y' iwacu, Hafi y' aho; 
Ubu ngezehafi y' iwacu, 
Ngiye gusohorayo.

2.
Ku rurembo rwa data, Hariy' amazu menshi;
Yateguriy' aberabose, Ngo babane na Yesu.

3.
Ni njya kuger' iwacu, Nzururuts' umutwaro; 
Nzoroherwa n'umubabaro, Nambar' ikamba ryiza.

4.
Ningera ku nkengero, Nzaba ngeze bugufi; 
Nzaba negerey' i muhira, Ngiye kuger' iwacu.
