122. Nyigiza Hafi

1.
Nyigiza hafi, Mbane nawe; Nta cyandutira kubana nawe. 
Ngumisha mu gituza cyawe; Mpisha nibere mu buhungiro;
Mpisha nibere mu buhungiro.

2.
Nyigiza hafi, Nta cyo mfite; Ntacyo nzanye hw' ituro ry'Umwami, 
Kerets' umutim' ushavuye; Mpa kuhagizw' amaraso yawe;
Mpa kuhagizw' amaraso yawe.

3.
Nyigiza hafi, Mwami wanjye; Nishimiye gucika ku byaha. 
Ndekekwikuza n' ubwibone; Umpe Yes' Umwami wambambiwe;
Umpe Yes' Umwami wambambiwe.

4.
Nyigiza hafi, ngihumeka; Kugez' ubwo ngazer' i Budapfa; 
Mazemu bihe bidashira, Nzibere hafi y' Umwami wanjye;
Nzibere hafi y' Umwami wanjye.
