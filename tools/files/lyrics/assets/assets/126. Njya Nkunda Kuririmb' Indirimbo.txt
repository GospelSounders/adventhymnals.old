126. Njya Nkunda Kuririmb' Indirimbo

1.
Njya nkunda kuririmb' indirimbo, Ngo Yaranshunguye. 
Niwewaciy' ingoyi z' Umwanzi, Maz' arambatura.

Gusubiramo:
Sop:
Kuk' ubwe yanshunguye; Kuk' ubweyanshunguye,
Nzahimbaz' izina rye. Kuk' ubwe yanshunguye,
Nzaririmbir' uwandokoye.
A.T.B.:
Kuk' ubwe yanshunguye, Kuk' ubweyanshunguye; 
Kuk' ubwe yanshunguye, Nzahimbaz' izina rye. 
Kuk'ubwe yanshunguye, Kuk' ubwe yanshunguye; 
Nzaririmbir'uwandokoye.

2.
Mfit' umukiz' unogeye rwose, Niwe wanshunguye. 
Mpimbazwano gukor' iby' ashaka, Kuko yanshunguye.

3.
Nzahora mpamya nta cyo nikenga, Yuko yanshunguye.
Sinzashidikanya ngo nshogore, Kuko yanshunguye.

4.
Mfit' aho yanteguriye heza, Kuko yanshunguye. 
Nihonzibanira naw' iteka, Kuko yanshunguye.
