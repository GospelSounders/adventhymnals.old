52. Ngwino Soko Y' Umugisha

1.
Ngwino soko y' umugisha, Mp' umutim' ugushima. 
Migez'idakama myiza, Tugusingize cyane. 
Unyigishe kukuramy' iteka, Kandimeny' ubwiza bwawe. 
Kwiringir' ubwami bwawe, Kunyuzuz' urukundo.

2.
Nshyir' ahw' icyibutso cyanjye, Niho ngeze kubwawe. 
Ndizeyeyuk' uzanjyana, Nger' iwaw' amahoro. 
Yesu yantaruye nazimiye, Narindi kure y' Imana; 
Ashaka kunkiz' urupfu, Yatanz' ubugingo bwe.

3.
Ndimw' ubwishyu bwawe Mwami, Bw' imbabaz' ungiriye. 
Nyigizabugufi bwawe, Meny' urukundo rwawe. 
Ngoswe n' ibingerageza byinshi,Ngo ndek' iyo Mana nkunda. 
Akir' umutima wanjye Undinde nkire rwose.
