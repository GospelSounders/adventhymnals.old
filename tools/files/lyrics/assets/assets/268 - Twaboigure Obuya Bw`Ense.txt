268 - TWABOIGURE OBUYA BW'ENSE
"We Speak of the Realms"


1
Twaboigure obuya bw`ense, Engiya echabeire, Obuya bwaye bw`ogokumia. Ng`aki_erabe kobao! Chinchera nechia edhahabu, `Magacho ye chilulu, Tibogotebeka obuya, Ng`aki_erabe kobao.

2
Ntosiboke korw` as`ebibe, `Matemwa, n`obororo; Mechando ende tekobao, Ng`aki_erabe kobao; Obwanchani Obonge mono, Tobek`eang` endabu, `Kanisa y`Omotangi o Igoro, Ng`aki_erabe kobao.

3
Mechando yaito yonsi_eere, Rituko ri `omogoko; Torore omochi `ogoika, Ori`ochabeire mono; Buya buna omoriakari, Koumer` Omonene; Goichana tigokobao, Mosunte tokobao.

4
Ninchandeka ime as`ense, Nindengererie_Igoro; Bwango nainche nimanye onsi, Ng`aki_erabe kobao; Ntororane aase agare, As` obonen` obonge, Abachenu bonsi mbabeo, Yeso nere nabeo.
