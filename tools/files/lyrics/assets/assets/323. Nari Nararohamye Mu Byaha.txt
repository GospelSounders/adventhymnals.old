323. Nari Nararohamye Mu Byaha

1.
Nari nararohamye mu nyanja y' ibyaha, 
Nta byiringiro narimfite byo kuvamo, 
Maz' Umukiza yumva gutabaza kwanjye, 
Ab' ariw' unkuramo none ndiho.
 
Gusubiramo:
Urukundo rwankuyemo, 
ubwo nari nabuze uwamfasha, 
Urukundo rwankuyemo, 
Nari nabuz' umfasha, Mbona Yesu.
 
2.
Muhay' umutima wanjye mushikimyeho, 
Nzajya ndirimb' iteka icyubahiro cye, 
Urukundo nyakuri nzajya nduririmba, 
Niwe nkesha kwizera n' urukundo. 

3.
Mutim' ubabaye reba Yes' agukize, 
Niw' uzakurokora, Mu murab' ukaze, 
Ategek' inyanja zose zikamwumvira, 
Arashaka kugukiz' uyu munsi.
