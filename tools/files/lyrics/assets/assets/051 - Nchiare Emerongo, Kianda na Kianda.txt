051 - NCHIARE EMERONGO KIANDA NA KIANDA
"The Ninety And Nine"


1
Nchiare emerongo, kianda na kianda Chiaraire egora ime,
Eyemo egasirer` ebitunwa Are are n`egeita gi`enka
Are ase ebitunwa ebibe Teri ase omorisia omuya 
Teriase Omorisia omuya.

2
"Monen` emerongo kianda na kianda Echio tichigoisaini?"
`Morisi` akairania: Eyemo yane Yasirire korwa ase nde- 
Nonya enchera erembe mono Ning`ende as`eroro koyerigia 
Ning`ende as`eroro koyerigia.

3
`S` ababorigwa onde tamanyete Chindiba echi ambogete
Gos` omosunte Yeso aetete Ataranyor` engondi yaye
Ar` as` eroro akaigwa ekwana `Ndwair`etari kwenyar`er`ang`e gokwa 
`Ndwair`etari kwenyar`er`ange gokwa.

4
"Monene ng`ai amanying`aya arwa, `Nyororoget` egetunwa?" 
`Nigo aitekeret` omosiru, `Morisi` ataramoirania` 
Ninki giasionoir` amabok`ao" Nigo asionorwa n`amagw` amange 
Nigo asionorwa n`amagw` amange`.

5
As `ebitunwa chinkoba chigoaka, Korwa egetare `goro
`Riogi rikaigweka gochia igoro, `Goka nanyorire eng`ondi! 
Malaik` agater` as` ekerogo, "Monen` oiranirie egiasirete 
Monen` oiranirie egiasierete.
