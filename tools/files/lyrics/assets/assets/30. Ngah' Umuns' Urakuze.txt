30. Ngah' Umuns' Urakuze

1.
Ngah' umuns' urakuze, Burije turuhuke; 
Kand' ubw' imuri z'ijoro zaka turamy' Uwiteka,
Twebwe twese.
Gusubiramo:
Wera, Wera, Wera, Mwam' Imana. 
Wigwij'ahantu hose, 
Nta kitaguh' ishimwe, Mwam' Imana.

2.
Mwami nyir' ubugingo, Terany' abagushaka; 
Buzuze bos'urukundo, Ubagir' ubwoko bwawe,
Bakuramye.

3.
Umwijima n' ugwira, Dukingir' imbabazi; 
Tukwegerez' imitima,Hirya y' ubwiza bw' ijuru,
Ngo duhirwe.

4.
Duhe kukwiringira, Ng' ubwo tuzapfa mwami, 
Twongere kubon'umucyo, Ijoro rishir' iteka,
Tugushime.
