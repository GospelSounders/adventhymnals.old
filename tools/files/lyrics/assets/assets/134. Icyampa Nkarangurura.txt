134. Icyampa Nkarangurura

1.
Icyampa nkarangurura, Ngahimbaz' Umwami wanjye, 
Ariko ni ndewabasha kuririmb' ishimwe rya Yesu?
Gusubiramo:
Urukundo ruhebuje! Urukundo rwa Yesu!
Urukundo ruhebuje! Urukundo rwa Yesu!

2.
Niwe munezero wanjye, Ni naw' umpesh' amahoro. 
Mu makubano mu byago, Yesu niw' umber' ihumure.

3.
Niwe byiringiro byanjye, Byo kuzahabw' ubugingo. 
Mu mwijim'amber' umucyo, Yesu ni w' umber' ihumure.
