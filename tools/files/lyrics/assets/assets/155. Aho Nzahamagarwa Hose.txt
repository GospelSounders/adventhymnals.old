155. Aho Nzahamagarwa Hose

1.
Aho nzahamagarwa hose, Ari no mu misozi, 
Cyangwa ku rugambarukaze; Mubyeyi, n' unkenera, 
Ukambwiz' ijwi ryawe ritoya, Ukannyuz'inzira ntazi, 
Nzakwishyira mu maboko, mvuge nti: Nzajy' ah'unshakira hose.

Gusubiramo:
Nzajy' ah' unshakira hose, Mwami; 
Mumisozi no mu bibaya. 
Nzavug' iby' ushaka ko mvuga Mwami,
Kandinzaba nk' uk' ushaka.

2.
Ahari Yes' ashaka yuko mmuvugir' uyu munsi; 
Ahari babonek'impabe, Nkwiriye guhabura; 
Murokozi wanjye, N' unyobora, N' ahar'ibihanamanga; 
Sinzareka kogez' inkuru yawe; Nzajy' ah' unshakirahose.

3.
Ntabwo nshidikanya, Har' aho Mwam' ushaka ko nkora. 
Mukubaho kwanjye kugufi, Nkorer' Uwambambiwe. 
Mwami, Nkubikij'ibyanjye byose, Nzi k' unkunda bitangaje. 
Nzakor' iby' ushaka,mbikunze cyane; Nzajy' ah' unshakira hose.
