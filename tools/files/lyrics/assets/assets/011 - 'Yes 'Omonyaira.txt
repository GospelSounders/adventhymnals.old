011 - YES 'OMONYAIRA
"Jesus Thou Hast Promised"

1
Yes` omonyaira, nonya nabake
Bachire gosaba, nobe aroro;
Twanchire ring`ana togosasime,
Yeso tosesenie, natokorore.

Chorus
Yeso tosesenie, nao toraa;
Inchuo tosesenie, mono botambe.

2
Nkwabete na intwe korw`agwo kare,
Ko ntokoganetie goika oche;
Inchuo Omoboria, toe obuya,
Toigwe otwanche, otosesenie.

3
Yeso otokonye, natogotogie,
Totere as`ore, igw` ogosaba;
Buna togosaba, erio twegene,
Erio tosemerie, twanche kobua.
