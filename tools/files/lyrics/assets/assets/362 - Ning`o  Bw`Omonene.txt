362 - NING'O BWA' OMONENE
"Who Is On The Lord's Side"


1
Ning`o  bw`Omonene,  ning`ore  ang`e?
Abanto  barigetie  kogend`esegi;
Ning`o origeti`aret`abamwabo,
Bache  gokonyeka tibacha gosira? 

Chorus
Ogokonya  nokwao  n`obuya  bwonsi,
Ogokonya  nokwao  n`obuya  bwonsi;
Ntob`ensemo yao `Monene botambe.

2
Tindigeti`obonene  gos`ogosikwa;
Gatwasoir`egonde  ya  Yes`Omuya;
Intwe  n`obwanchani  bwatoretire!
Oyorangerigwe Oyio noyoye.

3
Agatogora  tari  n`edhahabu,
Rigori`ri`amanyinga  eri`ogokumia;
Yes`otoichoiri`Omoik`Omochenu,
Twatigir`ebibe, Nabaye tore.

4
Esegi  nendoro  tiga  torore,
Oyorayebu`egonde  y`Erwoti;
Nse  as`ebendera  twatenenire,
Nao  torabuere na`omorembe.
