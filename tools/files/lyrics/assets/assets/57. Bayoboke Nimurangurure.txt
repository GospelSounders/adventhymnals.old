57. Bayoboke Nimurangurure

1.
Bayoboke nimurangurure, Mwamamaz' ubutumwa mw' isi yose.
Nimugwiz' ayo makuru mu bantu! Abashaka nibaze.

Gusubiramo:
Uwemey' aze! Uwemey' aze! 
Nimubimenyesh'abatuye hasi! 
Umubyey' aratarur' abana be,
Abashaka nibaze.

2.
Ushaka kuza yitindiganya, Niyinjir' irembo riruguruye.
Umwami ni we nzira y' ubugingo, Abashaka nibaze.

3.
Ushaka wese n' agire bwangu! Kand' agir' ubutwari yihangane.
Ushaka wes' azahabw' ubugingo, Abashaka nibaze.
