131. Ndusheho Gusa Nawe

1.
Ndusheho gusa nawe, Yesu mukiza wanjye;
Nyuzuz' ubugwaneza,Mp' urukundo ruhebuje. 
Ndusheho kumera nkawe, Aho ngenda hose,Mwami; 
Ndiyoroheje rwose, Mwami njye ngukurikiza.

2.
Nib' irind' icyanira, Ikamenya n' igishwi; 
Sinshidikanyarwose, Nanjy' ihor' inyitayeho. 
Izanyigisha kwitonda, Imbabarir'amafuti; 
Ndiyoroheje rwose, Mwami njye ngukurikiza.

3.
Ndusheho gusa nawe, Ndusheh' uko bukeye; 
Nyigiza hafi yawe,Ahw' amahor' adashira.
Uhor' umpindura mushya, Kubw' urukund'ungirira; 
Ndiyoroheje rwose, Mwami njye ngukurikiza.
