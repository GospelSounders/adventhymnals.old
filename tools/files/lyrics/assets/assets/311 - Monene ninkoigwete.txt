311 - MONENE NINKOIGWETE
	"Master Speak"


1
Monene ninkoigwete, Nganyet` eng`ana yao, Nyigwet` okwane ondemie; `Monene kwana bono; Ningotegererete, As` ari` onsi orantebie.

2
Monen` onyator` erieta, Eri` omonye nase nde, Kwana nainche ntunye bwango, Ndemete nsibogete; `S` Omorisi` ararae, `Kiriri Ki` egetare.

3
Nonya nd` omoke, ochayire, Tonseri `otanyigweti, Monene nomanyet` onsi, Ayar` enkoro yane; Nomanyet` okogania, Kwana eri` onsesenie.

4
Monene Kwan` ombek` ang`e, Riogi riao riigweke, `S` okoigwera n` omogoko, Nimbwati` eng`ana yao; Monene ninkoigwete, Koranche kwana nainche.
