129 - IKA ANG'OMOKUBIO
"Welcome, Welcome, Day Of Rest"


1
Ika ang` omokubio, Boitongo ase ense; Kun` enkor` enyororo. N`amasaria Igoro.

2
Rituko gotimoka, Ngaki yao ngoet` ere; Neriogo `S` obororo, Goik` emeremo eere.

3
E rituko richenu, Gotogia na gosaba; Gosemia abororo, `Bisio biao ebinge.

4
Ika ang` omokubio, N`omong`us` Omochenu;  Sesenia chinsa chiao, Nkoro yan` eganyete.
