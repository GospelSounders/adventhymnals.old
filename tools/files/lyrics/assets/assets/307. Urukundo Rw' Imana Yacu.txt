307. Urukundo Rw' Imana Yacu

1.
Urukundo rw' Imana yacu, Nta muntu warurondora, 
Rusaguts' ikirere cyose, Rugera no munsi y' isi. 
Abantu bose bacumuye, Bacunguwe na Yesu, 
Umwana we wese wahabye, Yaramwicunguriye.
 
Gusubiramo:
Mbeg' urukundo rutangaje, Rutagir' urugero, 
Abera bazaruririmba, Ibihe bidashira. 

2.
Ubw' ibihe bizah' ibindi, N' ubwami bw' isi bwashize, 
Abantu bakanga gusenga, Amabuy' azaririmba. 
Urukundo rw' Imana yacu, Ntabwo rurondoreka, 
Rwacunguy' abo mw' isi bose, Ruzaririmbw' iteka. 

3.
Inyanja zose zaba wino, N' ijuru ryab' impapuro, 
Tukandikisha agati kose, Umuntu wes' akandika, 
Ntabwo rwabon' aho rukwirwa, Rusaguts' ikirere.
