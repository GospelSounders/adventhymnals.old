237. Ntabwo ndamenya

1.
Ntabwo ndamenya nez' icyatumye Iman' ingirir' ubuntu, 
Bigatum' ingir' uwo mu muryango w' abana bayikunda.
 
Gusubiramo:
Kuko nzi nez' uwo nizeye, 
Kand' ariw' ushobora byose, 
Niwe undindir' ubugingo, 
Kugeza urya munsi. 

2.
Ntabwo nzi nez' uburyo yampaye, Uko kwizera gukiza, 
N' ubury' Ijambo rye ryampaye amahoro yo mu mutima!

3.
Sinzi ubury' Umwuka yihanisha abanyabyaha nkanjye, 
Kumenyesh' abandi ibya Yesu, Bidutera kumwizera.
 
4.
Ntabwo nz' icyo nateganirijwe, Ar' icyiza cyangw' ikibi, 
Imibabaro n' iminsi myiza, Byose bir' imbere yawe. 

5.
Sinzi igih' umwan' azazira, Ndetse n' ahw' azaturuka, 
Niba mbana na Yesu mur' iki gihe, Nzamusanganira.
