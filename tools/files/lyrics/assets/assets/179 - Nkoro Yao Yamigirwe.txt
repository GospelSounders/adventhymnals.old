179 - NKORO YAO YAMIGIRWE
"Crowded Is Your Heart"

1
Nkoro yao yamigirwe, `baga ndiy` ase Yeso?
Gwasibirwe n`ebi` ense, `baga ndiy` ase Yeso?
Ngesieri ateneine, Gotur` omoigorere,
Igwa ngosoror` are, `baga ndiy` ase Yeso?

2
Gwasiria nsa chingiya, chiogokor` emeremo?
Nakoe chinguru, chiogokor` emeremo,
Takobugwa nebibe, bakony` abasaririe,
Barigie abasiru, Kae Yeso ribaga.

3
Ay` okorigi` ebi` ense, Kae Yeso ribaga;
Tori na_ibaga rinde, Komokorera Yeso,
Emegoko n` enibo, Ebinto bi` ense mbosa,
Gwechand` as` okwegokia, kae Yeso ribaga.

4
Ekero totameti, chimbusuro chia Yeso;
`Maboko are bosa, Rituko rie rigesa,
Ntori na gento kende, `meremo yaito mbosa;
Ntokonyora kogoka, Gatoramoror` Ere.
