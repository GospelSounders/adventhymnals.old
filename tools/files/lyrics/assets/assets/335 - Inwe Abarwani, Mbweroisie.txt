335 - INWE ABARWANI MWEROISIE
"Soldiers Of Christ, Arise"


1
Intwe abarwani, Mbweroisie kegima,
Mobe noboremu, Mogende nomokia.

2
Nonya bono segi, Mobe nokwegena,
Oyo `barangerie, Nare amo ninwe.

3
Gaki motenene, Mobe nokwegena.
Moyetache ense, Omosunte bwonsi.

4
Gaki motenene, Mobe nokwegena
Moyetache ense; Omosunte bwonsi.

5
Nabae chiguru; mogese kegima,
Timoba `batamu; Ase `segi ime.

6
Rigori mokobua; Ne chinguru chiaye,
Igoro mogende, Nobonene mono.
