285(2) - GOTAGOCHICHA AMO
"Come Unto Me Ye Weary Now As We Gather"

1
Gatogochicha amo, As`okoinyora Yeso; Toinyore Yeso Kristo, Tomwanche as` omoyo, Bono toinyore Yeso, `Y`onkwerete Kalwari

2
Gatokori` omogati, Na konyw` egekomb` eke Toinyor` omober` oye, Obambetw` as` omote, Toinyor` amanying` aye, `Ri` aitegete Kalwari

3
Tata_iter` Omoik`oo, Aso`ase chinkoro; Koinyora omwan` oo, Bun` akweret` abanto, `Manying` ay`agaiteka, Agakw`aria Kalwari.
