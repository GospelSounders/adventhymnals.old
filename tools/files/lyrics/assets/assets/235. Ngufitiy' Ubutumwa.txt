235. Ngufitiy' Ubutumwa

1.
Ngufitiy' ubutumwa, Haleluya, N' ubutumwa bw' Umukiza, 
Bwanditswe mw' ijambo rye, Haleluya, Muhugukire urakizwa. 

Gusubiramo:
Tyo reba (tyo reba) maz' ukire (maz' ukire), 
Reba Yes' Urakizwa. Byanditswe mw' Ijambo rye, 
Haleluya, Muhugukire urakizwa. 

2.
Ngufitiy' ubutumwa bw' urukundo, Mwene Data, Haleluya, 
Buturutse mw' ijuru, Haleluya, Bwatanzwe n' Umukiza Yesu. 

3.
Weguriw' ubugingo, Haleluya, Buhorahw' iteka ryose, 
N' uhanga Yes' amaso, Haleluya, Niwe wenyine ugukiza.
