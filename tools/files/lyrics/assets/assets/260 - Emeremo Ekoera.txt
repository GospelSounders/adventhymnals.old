260 EMEREMO EKOERA
"When My Life Work Is Ended"


1
Emeremo ekoera twamboke nyancha ng`umbu, Nindor` emambi` engiya y`obonene; Nimomany` Omotoria ko ndaike aria_igoro, Nankwanie n`omogoko Omonene

Chorus
Nimomanye, nimomanye,
Ko ndatenene ang`e as`are;
Nimomanye, Nimomanye,
Kondarore amabok` aye.

2
Naki ndagoke mono ko ndarore Nyasae, Kondaror` obosio bway` obwororo; Nimobake as` obwanchani n`obuya bwonsi, Bwandoseiri` obomenyo aria_igoro.

3
Abanchani baito bonsi_abakure as` ens` eye, Bakwete n`okwegena kwa Nyasae; Natogoke mono koumeran` aria_igoro, Ko ninganie korora Yeso kubua.

4
Kondasoe omochie nechianga chindabu, Nandae as`emechando etari;
Nintere n`omogoko n`ababuir` ebibe, Ko ninganie korora Yeso kobua.
