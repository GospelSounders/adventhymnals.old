106 - MORENDIOBONENE MBOCHE
    "Watchman Tell Me"


1 
`Morendiobonene mboche, bwa Zayoni as`emambia? 
`Bimanyererio bi`ogocha biakorabeiri` enchera? 
Omotoria Oramokie, `Masase nkobar` are,
Eboyie ase enyangi, Bwakire boka! boka!

2
Omorendi, amasaria `Mentekire kobua,
`Bimanyererio as`ense, Biorokirie ogocha 
`Getureri gekobuga, geture abakure, 
Abachenu nababoke beboyeti ogotakwa.

3
`Morendi rora bwakire, Rituko ri`Omonene;
Natoake amariogi, Obonda ng`ang`e bore,
Omotari ndora ng`umbu Kanani ekoorokia,
Yerusalemu omuya boig` ororekanire.

4
`Morendi nka yedhahabu` Karanset` Omogambi;
Ochabeire buya mono, `S` omoremb` agamb`onsi
`Borabu as`ebitunwa Amasaria akomesa 
Chinsoko chingiya mono, Ebisicha ebiya.

5
`Morendi, ror` ens`egocha, Yamete buya mono;
Ere ng`umbu ang`e mono! omogond`o Edeni
Igwa amater` amaya,  Agoterwa Igoro,
Ror` abange bagotera, Omotari nabeo.
