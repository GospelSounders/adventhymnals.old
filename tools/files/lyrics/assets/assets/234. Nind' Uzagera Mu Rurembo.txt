234. Nind' Uzagera Mu Rurembo?

1
Nind' uzagera mu rurembo, Ruhorahw' ibihe byose, 
Akabon' ibyiza bihari, Mbese nijye, Cyangwa Wowe?
Akahabon' inzira zose zisizemw' izahabu nziza, 
Agahora mu masezerano, Mbese nijye, Cyangwa Wowe?

2
Nind' uzatur' umusaraba, Ngw' aruhuk' iteka ryose, 
Umwizer' azahabw' ikamba, Mbese nijye, Cyangwa Wowe?
Azareb' Umwami mu maso, Akiz' imibabaro yose, 
Abane naw' iteka ryose, Mbese nijye, Cyangwa Wowe?

3
Kandi har' uzakingiranwa, Ubutazakingurirwa, 
Azabwirwa ngo ntabwo nkuzi, Mbese nijye, Cyangwa Wowe?
Azahamagarir' ubusa, Urugi ruzaba rwakinzwe, 
Bazagororerw' asigare, Mbese nijye, Cyangwa Wowe?

4
Harih' uzishimir' intsinzi, Anesheje, Anesheje, 
Ari hamwe n' abacunguwe, Mbese nijye, Cyangwa Wowe?
Har' uzaramukanya n' abe, Batandukanijwe n' ibyago, 
Be kongera gutandukana, Mbese nijye, Cyangwa Wowe?
