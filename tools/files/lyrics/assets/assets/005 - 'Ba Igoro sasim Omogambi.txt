005 - 'Ba Igoro sasim Omogambi


1
Aba Igoro sasim Omogambi Tera
Obwanchani bw`ogokumia; Enguba
Embingwa nere botambe;
Y oichire nobuya n`ogotogigwa.

2
Ter`obobui n`obuya bwaye. 
Ere bwebisetie Oborabu: 
`Ndamwamu yaye yareta rirumbe,
Nabuet` obotindi bwemerindo.

3
Borendi bwao mbogokwaneka,
Bwarosi` embeo bwarabi omobaso 
Bwaika korw` ebitunwa gochi` omweya,
Bwatoerer`ase rime n`embura.

4
Ntw`ab`eriroba ntor`abareu,
Twagosemeri`aye n`omobui; 
`Maber`ao y`obworor`a botambe,
`Motongi, omoboria,
n`Omosani oito.
