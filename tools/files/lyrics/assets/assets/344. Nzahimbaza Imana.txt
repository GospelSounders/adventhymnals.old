344. Nzahimbaza Imana

1.
Nzahimbaz' Imana mfite umutima unyuzwe, 
Kubw' urukundo rwawe n' imbabazi wangiriye, 
Mfasha mu ntege nke zanjye, unsindire icyaha. 
Murikir' umutima wanjye, Nguhimbaze Mwami.
 
2.
Mutima wanjye umvira, Wubahe Uwiteka, 
Ungarure hafi yawe, Ne kuyoba Mukiza, 
Narazimiye, Mvana mu nzira y' urupfu Mwami, 
Wampaye ibyiringiro igihe wapfiraga.
 
3.
Mfukamye imbere yawe, ngushima Mwami wanjye, 
Ndakwinginze Mwami mpa umugisha uyu munsi, 
Nkeney' urukundo n' ubuntu biva kuri wowe, 
Maz' imibereho yanjye ijy' iguhesh' ikuzo.
