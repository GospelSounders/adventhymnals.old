211 - YES' OMOSALABA ONE
"Jesus I My Cross Have Taken"


1
Yes` omosalaba one, Bono naywetwekire; Ngotunyan` Omonen` one, Natigir` ebiens` eye.  Tindengererete_enibo, Enibo y` ense eye: Inche nomonguru` as`ore, Monyamwando o Nyasae.

2
Nonya ngochandek` ens` eye Ningaramere Yeso; `Mechando neba emenge, Nindemereri` as`ore.  Emechando teri gento, Ninde n` obuya bwao, Nairaneirie mbuya mono, Ninyore bions` as`ore.

3
`Nkoro ira obogima, `Konyek` amatuk` onsi; Obue ebibe n`obwoba, Gendereria emeremo,  `Moika nore as`ore, N` Obwanchani bwa Tata, Omotoria nere oyoo, Ogokweret` omote.

4
Oyominto ayerera, Bweboe okwegena, Bwebo` ebinto bi`esegi, Toraika ense enyia;  Yeso nomotang`ani oo, Sab` asare b`Ker` oraike Igoro, Nomogok` omonene.
