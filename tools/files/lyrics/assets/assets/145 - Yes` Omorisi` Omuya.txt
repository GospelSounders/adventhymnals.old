145 - YES'OMORISI'OMUYA
"Jesus The Loving Shepherd"

1
Yes` Omorisi` omuya, Okorangeirie, 
Gocha ase riicho, Otimoke nyomba; 
Nchwo bori nechinguru, kore omosae, 
Soa ase riicho, Enchera endonge.

Chorus
Nkorangeria are, nobwanchani;
Omotari inchwo ase Yeso, Nateneine
akoganyete, Namororire Ere.

2
Yes` Omorisi` omuya, Akerua as`ore,
Bono nkorangeri` are, Omotari inchwo; 
Karue as` emechando, Yeso otebire, 
Soa ase riicho, Soa motimoke.

3
Koganya noboriri, Chisese nchireo,
Gochirigia ching`ondi, Chinsiru chichirie,
Yeso Morisi` Omuya, Otebire oche,
Soa riicho riaye, Erio otimoke.
