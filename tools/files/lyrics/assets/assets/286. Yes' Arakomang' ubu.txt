286. Yes' Arakomang' ubu

1.s' arakomang' ubu, Mukingurire?
Aringing' umutima wanjye, Se mukingurire?
Muri wo harimw' ibyaha, N' umwijima n' agahinda. 
Kristo ashaka ko musanga, Se mukingurire?

2.
Se mubwir' urukundo, Mukingurire?
Nakir' uwo mwami w' ubuntu, Se mukingurire?
Ntantererana na hato, Ambabarir' ubugome. 
Mbese namwimura burundu, Cyangwa munambeho?

3.
Umutima w' ubwibone, Ndawukinguye. 
Mwakirany' ibyishimo byinshi, Ndamukinguriye. 
Mukiza mwiza tubane, Undind' ibishuko byose, 
Mb' amahor' iyo turi kumwe, Ngwino Mwami Yesu.
