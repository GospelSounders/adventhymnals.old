103. Ubyarw' Ubwa Kabiri

1.
Harih' uwasanze Yesu n' ijoro, Amubaz' iby' inzira y' agakiza.
Yes' amusubiz' amweruriy' ati: N' ukuri nkubwire:

Gusubiramo:
Ubyarw' ubwa kabiri (rwose);
Ubyarw' ubwa kabiri (rwose); 
N' ukuri, N' ukuri, Ndakubwir' ubu 
Kw' aribw' uzarama (rwose).

2.
Namwe nimwite kur' iryo jambo rye, Kuko Yesu yarivuz' aryeruye; 
Nibyo bizabagirir' umumaro Dor' aravug' ati:

3.
Abashak' uburuhukiro bose, No kuririmbana n' abacunguwe, 
No guhabw' ubugingo buboraho, Mwite kur' ibi ngo:
