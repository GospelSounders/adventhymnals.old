271. Mbese Ngend' Imbokoboko

1.
Mbese ngend' imbokoboko, Nsanganiy' Umukiza wanjye?
Nta cyiza namukoreye, Mbese ngend' imbokoboko?

Gusubiramo:
Mbese ngende ntacyo mfite, 
Nsanganir' uwo Mwami wanjye, 
Nta n' umwe namukirije, 
Mbese ngende ntacyo mfite?

2.
Sinterw' ubwoba n' urupfu, Umukiza yarankijije, 
Ku musanga ntacyo mfite, Numva binkojej' isoni.
 
3.
Napfushij' igih' ubusa, Nakoz' ibyangwa n' Umukiza, 
Ubu ndamutakambira, Musaba kumbabarira.
 
4.
Mwebw' abera mushishikare, Mukore hakiri kare, 
Dor' ijoro riraguye, Mutarakiz' imitima.
