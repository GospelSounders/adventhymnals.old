159 - NCHWO ABAMWAROSIRE
"Come Unto Me Ye Weary"


1
`Nchwo abamwarosire, Nche nimbatimokie" Eriogi ria Yeso riacher` abachandeku Rioroki _amaber` aye, Bun` atwaberete Mogoko otaere, `bwanchani botambe.

2
"Nchwo inw abatari, Nimba` oborabu" Riogo `giya ria Yeso Korabi` omosunte Ntware nogoichana, ntwasirete enchera Twanyor` omogoko n`amater` as` emambia.

3
"Nchwo inwe` abamogokwa, Nimba` obogima" Eriogi riokoremia, Tobue esegi `Mobisa nomokong`u, Esegi nendoro Ko gwatoeire chinguru mono kobua.

4
Onde bwensi orache, Tinkomotuguta Titogotiting`ar` S` eriogi ria Yeso `Torangeiri` abamochi, Totagwenereti
S` obwanchani obonge, Twachicha as`ore.
