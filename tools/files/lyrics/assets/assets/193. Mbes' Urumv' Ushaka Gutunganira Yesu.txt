193. Mbes' Urumv' Ushaka Gutunganira Yesu?

1.
Mbes' urumv' ushaka gutunganira Yesu? 
Ng' ujy' ujyana na wemu nzira ntoya? 
Akwikorerer' imitwaro yawe yore? 
Emer' ugengwe nawe.

Gusubiramo:
Azagutungany' akuboneze,
Amaraso y'akwezehw' ibyaha, 
Akuzuz' urukundo rwe rwinshi; 
Niwemera kujy'ugengwa nawe.

2.
Mbes' urashaka kw' akubatura mu byaha? 
Ngw' aguh' amahoro yomu mutima?
Ajy' akuramira, we guhor' ugwaguza?
Emer' ugengwe nawe.

3.
Mbes' urashaka kugabana ku bugingo?
Ngo wibere mu bwami bwomw' ijuru?
Urashaka kumaramaza muri byose?
Emer' ugengwe nawe.
