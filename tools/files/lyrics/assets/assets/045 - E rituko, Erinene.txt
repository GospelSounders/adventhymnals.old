045 (2) - ERITUKO ERINENE
"O How Great Day"


1
E rituko, erinene. Ri`ogotogia Omotoria!
Omoy`one noichorete, Koba buna omwan`oye.

Chorus
Rituko, rituko, Ri`ogosibia `bibe biane!
Okorenda n`ogosaba, Ogotunya,
bun`omwana, Rituko, rituko, Ri`ogosibia,
`bibe biane.

2
Amang`an`aya namaya, Nind`oyoye,
ner`oyone; Akang`usa komotunya.
Namwanchire n`omoy`one.

3
Gatorabe n`omoy`oyo, Natobe na omogoko;
Encher`eyemo tobwatie,
Na totare na Nyasae.

4
Eira yane ya kare, Ninyegache `miaka,
yonsi; Goik`achich` Omonene,
Natobe n`obuya bwonsi.
