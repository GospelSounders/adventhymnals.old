110. Wumv' Amagambo Y' Umukiza

1
Wumv' amagambo y' Umukiza; Ni y' aguhesh' ubugingo.
Wamunyabyaha we, menya ko hahirw' abakomez' amategeko.

Gusubiramo:
Hahirw' abayakomeza yose,
Nibo bahirwa,Barahirwa;
Hahirw' abayakomeza yose, 
Barahirwa, Barahirwa.

2
Kuyumva gusa nta kamaro, N' ukutugwabiza rwose; 
Kuyumvadutinya, ntibyadukiza; Hahirw' abayumvira yose.

3
Bazinjiran' umunezero mu mudugudu w' Imana,
Ahatab' ishavun' umubabaro, Beger' igiti cy' ubugingo.
