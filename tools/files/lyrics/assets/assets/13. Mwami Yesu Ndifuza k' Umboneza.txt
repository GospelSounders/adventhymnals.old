13. Mwami Yesu Ndifuza k' Umboneza

1.
Mwami Yesu ndifuza k' umboneza; Ndashaka k' umba mu mutim'iteka. 
Noye kujya nita ku bigirwamana. Umboneze rwose nere ndush' ingwa.

Gusubiramo:
Ngonere de ndush' urubura! 
Mpa kwer' ubu ngutunganire rwose!

2.
Mwami jy' undebera ku ntebe yawe! Mpa gutamb' igitambokiboneye! 
Ndeke gushidikanya nitange rwose. Umboneze rwose nere ndush' ingwa.

3.
Yesu ndagutakambira nkomeje, Ngo mbon' uko nkwitega Mwamimwiza! 
Ntunganish' umuvu w' amaraso yawe! Umboneze rwose nere ndush' ingwa.

4.
Mwami ngutegereje nihanganye, Yesu ngwin' ump' umutim'utunganye!
Ntiwari wirengagiz' abagushaka. Umboneze rwose nere ndush' ingwa.
