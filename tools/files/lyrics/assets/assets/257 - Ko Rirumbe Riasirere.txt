257 - KO RIRUMBE RIASIRERE
"When The Mists Have Rolled"


1
Ko rirumbe riasirire korwa as`ebitunwa, Omobaso okobara ogotongia emambia; Natosome rirube riaye ri`obwanchani bwaye, Tomanyane twensi buya, rirumbe rigosira.

(Rirumbe rigosira)

2
Tokomocha koinyora buna intwe namaroba, Togosaria amachiko na gochandeka tweka; Chimbaba chiaye chindabu chitub` ebibe biaito, Okorenda gokoera, rirumbe rigosira.

3
Rirumbe rigototuba, Tata natomanyete, `Bosi` as`obosio tomanyane, na baria twanchete; Korwa ng`umbu yabarire buya, yakiamokire, Ekiriri kegoeta, rirumbe rigosira.
