187 - GOTARA NA YESO
"When We Walk With THe Lord"

1
Gotara na Yeso, as` eriarakwane,
Enchera yaito morekire; Gokor` ayanchete,
Namenye naintwe, Na baria, bonsi baregene.

Chorus
Okwegena, encher` ende teiyo;
Y`omogok` ase Yeso: Egene oigwere.

2
Ntori n`omusunte, Nony` amare taiyo,
Ere bweka obirusirie; `Tang`utang`u nchiyo,
Nonya n`amariga, Gatoregene na Koigwera.

3
Ntori n`egurube, n`obororo bwonsi,
`Meremo yait` atoakane; Emechando teiyo,
n`ogweitia nkoiyo, Nogosesenia `S` okoigwera.

4
Titokoenekia, obwanchani bwaye,
Goika twerure Kegima; As`ogwanchereria,
n`omogok` arure, Nebiab` abaramoigwere.

5
As` okobwatana, toikarans` as`are,
Gose totare amo nere; Kor`ekiaratebe,
Gend`as` arachike, Toirok` egene, n`oigwere.
