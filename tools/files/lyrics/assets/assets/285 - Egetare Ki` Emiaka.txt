285 - EGETARE KI EMIAKA
"Rock Of Ages"

1
Egetare ki`emiaka, Nyebise im` as` ore; Amache n`amanyinga, Bikorw` omober` oo; Bib` eriog` as`ebibe, Nkonye korwa ebibe.

2
Meremo y`amaboko, Tegonkorera gento; Nonya n`omokia bwonsi, `Mariga naiteka; Ase nde `bibe mbosa, Naye bwek` orankonye.

3
Tindi koreta gento, `Mosalaba ndaimokie; Mbosa nde aye mboyie, Nakorigereire; Ay` ensoko `bogima, Onsibie tinkwa ense.

4
Kangokor` orogendo, Mobui bw` ababisa; Ng`iruruke gochi` are, nkoror`As`ekerogo; Egetare ki`emiaka, Nyebise ime as` ore.
