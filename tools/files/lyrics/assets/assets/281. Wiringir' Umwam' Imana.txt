281. Wiringir' Umwam' Imana

1.
Wiringir' Umwam' Imana, Kukw' ari y' izakuyobora, 
Izere Nyir' imigisha, Niw' uzagukenura. 

Gusubiramo:
Izer' uko yakubwiye, 
Yihang' amas' iteka ryose, 
Uyiringire ibihe byose, 
Niyo Mwungeri wawe. 

2.
Wiringir' Umwam' Imana, Umushukanyi nakwegera, 
Wiringir' Umwam' Imana, Ujy' uyihungiraho. 

3.
Wiringir' Umwam' Imana, Mu birushya no mu makuba, 
Wiringir' Umwam' Imana, yih' umutwaro wawe. 

4.
Wiringir' Umwam' Imana, Mu mibereho yawe yose, 
Wiringir' Umwam' Imana, Kugez' ahw' azazira.
