214 - BONO NGOCHA NDE
"Dear Lord, I 'm Coming Home"


1
Natayayire ar`Omonene, Bono ngocha nde nka;  Nganetie ng`irane as`obuya, Bono ngocha nde nka.

Chorus
Ee ngocha nde, Tata ngocha nde, Bono ngocha nde nka; Ee ngochande, Tata ngocha nde, Bono ngocha nde nka.

2
Narosire n`ens`eye y`ebibe, Bono ngocha nde nka;  Omotoria nang`ariganie nka, Bono ngocha nde nka.

3
`Manyir` Omotoria nantimokie, Bono ngocha nde nka; Ko nganetie gotimok`as`are, Bono ngocha nde nka.

4
Nasabire nonya nobosomba, Bono ngocha nde nka;  Gicha nyor`obuya bw`Omotoria, Bono ngocha nde nka.

5
Togi`Omonene Omotori`one, Bono ngocha nde nka; `Ganyet`ang`ariganie nch`omobe, Bono ngocha nde nka.
