079 - TOTAMERA KENDE
"Ask Not To Be Excused"


1
Totamera kende, `Meremo nereo,
Kabe ang`ogende, Ase oratomwe
Okorangeirire, Botamb` as` obuya,
Tibwang` emeremo, `Miya ya Igoro.

Chorus
Aye nchwo (bono) Totamera kende,
Aye nchwo (bono) Kabe ang` ogende
Totamera kende, Ande notebigwe,
"Aye gwanchayete, Togochia Igoro".

2
Totamera kende, `Monen` orangeirie,
Kwangir` aye mono, igw` eriogi bwango
Rigesa riamire, `Bagesi nabake, 
Kabe n`omogoko, Korer` Omonene.

3
Totamera kende, Bono totebana,
Tomochaya Ere, Ande nagotige
Ribaga korire, Orore Igoro, 
Ngosororwa ore Inchuo nabwaberwe.
