324. Mbega Igitambo

1.
Mbega igitambo cy' amaraso Y' Umukiza, 
Wapfuy' arinjy' azira! Yambambiwe ku musaraba, 
Abitewe n' ukw' ankundacyane, 
N' urukundo rutangaje Mwami wanjye, 
Kugirango wemere kubabara no kumfira. 

Gusubiramo:
S.:
Urukundo ruhebuje, 
Nirworwamuteye kumfira, 
Mbeg' urukundo rutangaje, 
Ngo ruratum' ancungura!
A. , T. na B.:
Urukundo ruhebuje, 
Nirworwamute… kumfira, 
Mbeg' uruku… rutangaje, 
Ngo ruratum' ancungura!

2.
Yasiz' intebe y' ubwami ya Se, 
Yemera kunyitangirango nkire, 
Nari mbuz' epfo na ruguru, 
Imbabazi zezirangoboka, 
Maz' Urukundo rwe runter' umunezero, 
Mperako mererwa neza mu mutima wanjye. 

3.
Nari maz' igihe kirekire, 
Mboshywe n' umwijima w'ibyaha byanjye, 
Maze kumeny' ineza yawe, 
Ngukurikiramfit' ibyishimo, 
Kuko nari mbohowe ingoyi z' ibyaha, 
Mperako ndahaguruka, Ndagukurikira.
 
4.
Nta teka ncirwaho kuko ndi muri Yesu, 
Naw' akab' arimuri jye, 
Niwe nkesha kubaho kwanjye, 
Nkeger' intebeye ntacyo ntinya, 
Nkambikw' ikamba mbikesha uwancunguye, 
Ntabwo nzahwema kumusingiz' iteka ryose.
