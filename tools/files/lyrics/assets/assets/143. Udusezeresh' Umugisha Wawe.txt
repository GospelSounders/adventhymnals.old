143. Udusezeresh' Umugisha Wawe

1.
Udusezeresh' umugisha wawe; Mwam' utwuzuz' amahoro; 
Urukundorwawe rudusabemo, Tubone kunesha rwose. 
Ngwin' uduhembure, Ngwin'uduhembure;
Tukiri mur' iyi si y' igomero.

2.
Tuguhay' ishimwe no kukuramya, Kubw' ubutumwa bw' imvaho,
Bwagukomotseho bukadukiza, Bunyur' imitima yacu; 
Tubushikamemo,Tubushikamemo;
Tugundir' ukuri tudacogora.

3.
Mbeshwaho n' imbabazi zawe nyinshi, None ndakwiyeguriye.
Ngwin' uduhumurish' impuhwe zawe, Duhore turwan' ishyaka; 
Tuzager' iwawe, Tuzager' iwawe;
Tukubone nta kidukingirije.
