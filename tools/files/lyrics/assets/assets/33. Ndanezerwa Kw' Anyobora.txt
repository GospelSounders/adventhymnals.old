33. Ndanezerwa Kw' Anyobora

1.
Ndanezerwa kw anyobora, Arampumurish' ijambo rye. 
Mubyonkora, N' aho ngenda, N' ukuboko kwa Yesu kunyobora.

Gusubiramo:
Aranyobora nkanezerwa,
N' ukw anyoboresh'ukuboko kwe. 
Nshaka kumukorera neza, 
Yuk' ukuboko kwe kunyobora.

2.
Niba ngir' ishavu ryinshi, Cyangwa nubwo nezerwa cyane. 
Mumahoro n' amakuba, N' ukuboko kwa Yesu kunyobora.

3.
Mwami nshaka ko tubana; Ntabwo nzinuba nitotomba;
Nzagumyanishime cyane, K' ukuboko kwa Yesu kunyobora.

4.
Ndangij' imirimo yanjye, Nesheje kubw' imbabazi ze;
Ntacyonzatinya n' urupfu, K' ukuboko kwa Yesu kunyobora.
