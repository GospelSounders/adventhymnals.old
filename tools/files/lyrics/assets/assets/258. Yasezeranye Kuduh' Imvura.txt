258. Yasezeranye Kuduh' Imvura

1.
Yasezeranye kuduha, Imvura y' umugisha, 
Iri sezerano ryayo, ryaturutse mw' ijuru. 

Gusubiramo:
Duh' umugisha, turifuz' umugisha, 
Uvuy' iwawe mw' ijuru, Nyabuneka wuduhe.

2.
Yasezeranye kuduha, Imvura y' umugisha, 
Yitugoborer' uyu muns' ikwir' impanda zose. 

3.
Yasezeranye kuduha, Imvura y' umugisha, 
Turayifuz' uy' umunsi, Sohoz' ibyo wavuze. 

4.
Yasezeranye kuduha, Imvura y' umugisha, 
Icyaduh' uyu munsi, Mana tubabarire.
