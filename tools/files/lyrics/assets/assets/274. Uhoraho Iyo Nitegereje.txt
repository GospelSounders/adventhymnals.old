274. Uhoraho Iyo Nitegereje

1.
Uhoraho, Iyo nitegereje, Ibitangaza by' ibyo wakoze, 
Ar' izuba, N' ukwezi n' inyenyeri, N' ibindi bintu byose waremye.
 
Gusubiramo:
Nishimira kukuririmba, 
Mana nkuru y' urukundo, 
Nishimira kukuririmba, 
Mana nkuru, Y' urukundo. 

2.
Mana nkunda, Iyo nitegereje, Igiti cyose mu byo waremye, 
Binyibutsa igiti cy' i Kalvari, Umwana wawe yabambiweho. 

3.
Iyo numva inyoni ziririmba mu biti byiza yaturemeye, 
Urusobe rw' amajwi yazo meza n' urw' amasumo biranezeza.
 
4.
Igih' ibyago bizaba bishize, Abera bose bagukikije, 
Tuzagusingiza iteka ryose, Kandi turamy' Uwatwitangiye.
