10. Duhurire Ku Mugezi

1.
Duhurire ku mugezi, Dusange bamarayika; 
Amazi yaw'atunganye, Atemb' av' imbere y' Imana.

Gusubiramo:
Nuko tujye ku mugezi!
Duhurire n'abera ku mugezi! 
Ku mugez' utunganye w' Imana,
Utemb' uv' imbere y' intebe.

2.
Ku nkombe y' uwo mugezi, Ni ho tuzatambagira,
Turamy'Umwami w' iteka, Mu gihe cyiza kidashira.

3.
Tutageze ku mugezi, Dutur' imitwaro yacu;
Tuzakirishw'imbabazi, Duhabw' umwambaro n' ikamba.

4.
Ub' umugez' uri hafi, Urugendo ruzashira;
Tuzishima mumitima, N' indirimbo z' umunezero.
