66. Nshim' Umunsi Nakwemeye

1.
Nshim' umunsi nakwemeye, Mwami Yesu mukiza we!
Nshima cyanen' umutima, Ndabwiriz' ibinnezeza.

Gusubiramo:
Mpimbaze, Mpimbaze; Yesu wankijij'ibyaha. 
Anyigisha kumuramya, Ngo nezerw' iminsi yose;
Mpimbaze,Mpimbaze Yesu wankijij' ibyaha.

2.
Biramaze, napfiriwe, Nd' uwa Yesu wandokoye; 
Yangiz' uwe,Nkurikire, Nshima yuko ntowe nawe.

3.
Mutima wanjye ruhuka; Reba mw' ijur' ahejejwe, 
Ntugasig'Umwami wawe, Bana nawe mwumvikane.

4.
Iteka nzagukorera, Iby' isi ndabizinutswe.
Nkugarutseho noneho,Sinzarorera kwishima.

5.
Ubw' umucyo wo mw' ijuru uzamurikir' iyi si.
Nsanganir' Umwami wanjye, Tuzibanire mw' ijuru.
