133 - KABE NAINCHE
"Abide With Me"

1
Kabe nainche mbwango bokoira,
Mosunte oichoire kabe nainche;
Abande basinyirwe kondemia,
Mokonyi bw`aboro, Kabe nainche.

2
Bogima bwane bwaeta bwango,
`Mogoko bw`ense nig`ogoeta,
Ebinto bionsi-biaonchokire, 
`Yotagoonchoka, menya nainche.

3
Ninkoganetie ekero gionsi,
Buya bwao mbobu` omobisa;
Ng`o bun` ay` oranyare kondaa,
S` obuya n`obobe, menya nainche.

4
Amo naye tindi n`okoiroka,
Marwair` obororo bionsi mbosa
Nonya nogokwa tikori kende,
Nabuire onye kore nainche.
