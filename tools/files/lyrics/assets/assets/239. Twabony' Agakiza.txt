239. Twabony' Agakiza

1.
Twabony' agakiza, Muri Yesu kristo, Yadupfiriye i Kalvari, 
Mu rukundo rwe rutagir' iherezo, Twatsindishirijwe na Yesu. 

Gusubiramo:
Yesu yabay' incungu y' ibyaha byacu, 
Yaducunguj' amaraso ye, 
Nzahora ndirimb' urukundo rwinshi, 
Yesu yankunze kera. 

2.
Igitambo cy' amaraso y' Umukiza, Nicyo cyadukijij' ibyaha, 
Cyadukuyehw' inenge twari dufite, Ubu tur' abana b' Imana. 

3.
Urukundo rw' Imana yakunze umuntu, N' ukuri ruhebuje byose, 
Nta yindi mpano dufite twayitura, Tumuhe imitima yacu.
