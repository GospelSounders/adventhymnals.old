089 - GOSIMEKER' AMARIGA
"He That Goeth Forth"


1
Gosimeker` amariga,
`Mbusur`as`obwanchani; Takorosa na
korara, Nonyor` obuya bwaye.

Chorus
Rigereria ense yonsi,
Rigesa ndire ang`e;
Ror` endagera yamire,
Rigesa ng`ang`e rire.

2
Ngotw` ere korwa Igoro, `Borabu
bw`omobaso; Emete yonsi nayame,
Ne chinguru chia Nyasae.

3
Toros` as`ogosimeka Tobaisa koiroka;
Nonyor` emechand` emenge,
Nogese n`omochengo.
