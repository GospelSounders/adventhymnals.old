193 - IGWA AMANG'ANA A YESO
"Hear THe Words Of Our Saviour"


1
Igwa amang`ana a Yeso,
`Mang`an` obogima bori
Otari gosaba, igwa inyora,
Basesenigw` abagokora.

Chorus
Ababwatete amachik` Aye,
Mbare negesio negesio
Ababwetete amachik` Aye,
Mbuya, mbuya, mbuya bare.

2
Koigwa bosa nkori kende,
Chiira nachibe bosa
Kobigwa, kwoboa, mbigototoria,
Basesenigw` abagokora.

3
Baso` omochie n`omogoko,
Abachenu abasera
Basibekire bare n`obonene,
Barie Omote bw`obogima.
