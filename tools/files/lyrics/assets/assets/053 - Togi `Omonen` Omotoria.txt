053 - TOGI'OMONEN'OMOTORIA
"All Praise To Our Redeeming Lord"


1
Togi `Omonen` Omotoria, `Moik` atobwatanie,
Gatobwatanirie `go, Tobe n`omoy`oyomo.

2
`Tochikire tober`aa, Tob`abam` as`are,
Erio totere gator`amo, Gochi` enchera `goro.

3
Abanchet`erieta riay` Obaeir `ebiegwa;
Natworoki `obuy`obonge, Gatwanchaine buya.

4
Toigwanaine buy`as`ense, Togok`ase Yeso;
Mogoko ki oyi`orabe, Gatwaikireigoro.
