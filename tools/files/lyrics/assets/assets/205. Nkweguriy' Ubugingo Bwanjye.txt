205. Nkweguriy' Ubugingo Bwanjye

1.
Nkweguriy' ubugingo bwanjye, Ng' ubugenz' uk' ushaka Mukiza.
Nweguriy' amaboko yanjye, Ng' uyakoresh' uk' ushaka Mwami,
Ng' uyakoresh' uk'ushaka.

2.
Nkweguriy' ibirenge byanjye, Bijye bijyan' ubutumwa bwiza, 
Nkweguriy' ijwi ryanjye, Ngo rijye rigusingiz' iteka,
Ngo rijyerigusingiza.

3.
Nkweguriy' akanwa kanjye, Kuzuz' ubutumwa bwawe Mwami, 
Nkweguriy' ubutunzi bwnjye, Ntacyo nshaka kwisigaranira,
Ntabwo nagir' icyo nkwima.

4.
Nkweguriy' imigambi yanjye, Yikoresh' uk' ushaka Mukiza, 
Nkweguriy' umutima wanjye, Maz' uwimike m' Ubwami bwawe,
Wimike m' Ubwami bwawe.

5.
Nkweguriy' urukundo rwanjye, Sinshaka kugir' icyo nguhisha,
Nguhay' impagarike yanjye, Nkweguriy' ibyanjye byose Mwami,
Nkweguriy' ibyanjye byose.
