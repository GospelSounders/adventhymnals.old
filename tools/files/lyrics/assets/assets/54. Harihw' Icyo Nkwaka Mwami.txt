54. Harihw' Icyo Nkwaka Mwami

1.
Harihw' icyo nkwaka Mwami, 
Nubwo mpora ngucumuraho,
Unyejeshe, Unyejeshe! 
Amazi cyangw' umuriro.

Gusubiramo:
Mpanagurwe hose Mwami!
N' umuriro nib'ushaka. 
Nkir' ibyaha, Birimburwe. 
Nkir' ibyaha, Birimburwe.

2.
N' untunganiriz' ubwenge, 
Nzanezerwa nzagusingiza;
Arik'umutima wera,
Urutaho, Urutaho.

3.
Ninezw' umutima wanjye,
Niho nzunguk' ubwenge bwose,
Mbubwirijwe nawe Mwami, 
Bwo mw' ijuru, bwo mw' ijuru.

4.
Njya nirind' ibicumuro,
Nkitonder' ibyo ntekereza. 
Mwami n'ibyo nkora byose,
Ntabwo nera, Ntabwo nera.
