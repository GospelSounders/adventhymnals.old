82. Mu Gitondo Njya Ndirimba

1.
Mu gitondo njya ndirimba,
Nguhimbaza Mwami;
Nguh'amashengesho yanjye, 
Kuko nkwiringiye.

2.
Ku misozi niho Yesu,
Yajyag' asabira;
No gutakambira Data, 
Ngw' adukiz' ibyago.

3.
Mwam' unyoboresh' Umwuka,
Mu nzir' itunganye. 
Untunganiriz'ubwenge, 
Meny' ibikwiriye.

4.
Abubah' izina ryawe,
Urajy' ubumvira.
Ubakingir' imbabazi,
Bakir' amakuba.
