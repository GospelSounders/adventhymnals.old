138 - NKI KERANSIBI' EBIBE?
"What Can Wash Away My Sins?"


1
Nki keransibi` ebibe? Namanyinga aye Yeso;
Nki keranchene ime? Namanyinga aye Yeso.

Chorus
Ogoitek` okuya, Kogonchena bori;
Nsoko ende teiyo, Tatig` amanying`a Yeso.

2
Kegochena tikeiyo, Tatig` amanying`a Yeso;
`S `okwaberwa ninsabe, Amanyinga aye Yeso.

3
Kende ngekombwatania Tatig` amanying`a Yeso; 
Tindakora ekiya Tatig` amanyinga` Yeso.

4
Tindi n`ogosemeria Tatig` amanying`a Yeso;
`Buya gos`oboronge Tatig` amanying`a Yeso.

5
Obonene! nintere, Amanyinga aye Yeso;
Nintere na kobaka, Amanyinga aye Yeso.
