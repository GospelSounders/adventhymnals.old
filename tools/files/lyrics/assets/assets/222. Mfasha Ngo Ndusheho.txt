222. Mfasha Ngo Ndusheho

1.
Mfasha ngo ndusheho kugukunda, 
Icyo nicyo ngusaba mpfukamye, 
Mbigusabye cyane mfasha Mwami Yesu, 
Ngo ndusheho kugukunda.
 
2.
Nashats' iby' iyi su ngo nezerwe, 
Ubu ndabiretse, Ng' ump' ibyiza. 
Icyo nsaba gusa, Mfasha Mwami Yesu, 
Ngo ndusheho kugukunda.
 
3.
Mfit' umubabaro n' agahinda, 
Nzishimira cyan' intumwa zawe. 
Iyo ndirimbana nazo biramfasha, 
Nkarushaho kugukunda. 

4.
M marembera y' ubu bugingo, 
Nzagushima cyane ngusingize. 
Nzakuririmba maze mvuge nsenga, 
Ngo ndusheho kugukunda.
