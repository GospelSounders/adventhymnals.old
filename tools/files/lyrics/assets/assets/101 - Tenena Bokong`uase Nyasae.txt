Tenena bokong`uase Nyasae, 
Komwaga omonge okogusa; 
Ner` egetare, nere chinguru,
`Bibe kobire ang`e.

Obonge bw` obosiru.

3
Tenena bokong`u s`ekeene,
Nonya kegotebana nkebue;

merindo keraete
