152 - YESO NGOER'ARE
"Jesus Is Passing"

1
Yeso ngoet` are, Yeso ngoet` are,
`Meuko nchwo monyor` amaiso;
Yeso bwanchire nabagwenie inwe,
`Maiso ain` aramokie, morore;
Inchwo Yeso aba` okorora.

2
Yeso ngoet` are, Yeso ngoet` are,
`Nchw` ebirem` as` omogweni` o bonsi;
Manying` agaiteka abatorie,
Naigw` okorangeria gw` ekerema;
Bono ngoet` are ase bonsi.

3
Yeso ngoet` are, Yeso ngoet` are,
`Nchw` abataka namoigotigwe;
Nabarae inwe, nabaragerie,
Yes` obachikire moigote;
`Nchwo Igor` as` ebiage bichire.

4
Yeso ngoet` are, Yeso ngoet` are,
Inw` abamochi, moche asare;
Twabasoroire, mwanche rero iga,
Moche bwango Gakorangeria;
Inchw` abamochi, momwanche Yeso.
