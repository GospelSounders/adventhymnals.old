026 - CHIMEME CHIONSI CHITERE
"O For A Thousand Tongue"

1
Chimeme chionsi chitere; Kobak`Omoboria! Kobak`Omoboria!
`Buya bwa Nyasa`Erwoti; 
(Bobui bw`obuya) Bobui bw`obuya
Bobui bw`obuya, Bobui bw`obuya.

2
Omonene Nyasa`one; Onkony`erio ndarie! Onkony`erio ndarie!
Ndari`Erieta riao ense; 
(`Bonene bw`erieta,) bonene bw`Erieta
Bonene bw`Erieta, Bonene bw`Erieta.

3
Yeso n`Erieta ri`obobui; Riseria obwoba Riseri`Omoichano!
Goter` as`Omonyabibe; 
(`Bogim`omorembe) `Bogim`omorembe
`Bogim`Omorembe `Bogim`omorembe.

4
Abuna `nguru chi`ebibe; `Sibor`omosibwa! Sibor`omosibwa
Manying` ay`asibi` ababe; 
(Nainche nansibie bo) Nainche nansibie bo,
Na nche nansibie bo, Nainche nansibie bo.
