237 - ABACHENU BONO RENDA
    "Watch Ye Saints"


1
Abachenu bono renda, Igoro yanyegerigwe; Mochiokie chitaya chiaino, Chibe ang`e kagoch`Ere.

Chorus
Ngoch` are, Ere Yeso; Ig` agocha n` obonene; Erio agambe botambe, Ngoch` are, Ere Yeso.

2
Eira ya Omotoria, Nwe mwabeirwe kegima pi; Chianga chiasibigwe bo, Tebi` aband` okoborigwa.

3
Amagambi agure pi, Yeso mbwang` agocha bono; Morandie obuya bwaye, Amatuk` ataraera.

4
Ebisaku nabisire, Yeso nachiche bwango bo; Onsi ay`ense naete, Tera Yeso ngocha are.

5
Abamochi, inchwo bwango, Amatuko ng`ang`e are; Ayerer` atarasieka, Monyore obuya bwaye.
