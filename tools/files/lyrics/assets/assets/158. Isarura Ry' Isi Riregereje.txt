158. Isarura Ry' Isi Riregereje

1,
Isarura ry' isi riregereje, Yes' arahamagar' abasaruzi. 
Dor'imirim' irez' ahantu hose, Nimuzan' imihoro musarure!

Gusubiramo:
S.:
Mugire bwangu, muhunik' imiba; 
Mugirebwangu, nimuze.
A.T.B.:
Mugire bwangu mwese;
Nimuzemuhunik' imiba ye.
Nimuze mugire bwangu mwese;
Nimuze mwese nimuze.

2.
Uwo muns' utazabamw amarira, Uhora wegerej' uko bukeye.
Imyaka yararitswe n' umuyaga, Mugire bwangw imvur' itarahanguka.

3.
Urukundo ni nk' isokw' idakama, Ituruka mw' ijuru kumuremyi; 
Nirwo ruturarikira gukora, Rukadukomeza ntitugw'isari.
