198 - YES'OMORISI 'OITO
"Jesus Is Our Shepherd"


1
Yes` Omorisi` oito, Tinyi` amariga;
Gatore as`are, Nki toroboe?
Tomotunye rioka, `S` aratorae;
Gochia as` eroro, Gos` oborisia.

2
Yes` Omorisi` oito, Twamany`eriogi;
Okomanya kwaye, Gwatogokirie
Nig` akogorora, As` obwororo;
Ner` oratorae, Nabaye tore.

3
Yes` Omorisi` oito, Otokwerete,
Gaiter` amanyinga, `Se ker` eng`ondi
Ker` eyem` abeka, Kemanyererio;
`Tebir```Ab` Omoika, Abwo nabane".

4
Yes` Omorisi` oito, Otorendire;
`Bibengi nabicha, Tibigotoria
`Mwenya bw`amakweri, Ogotwoboyia;
Ntokwobo` obobe, `Babui b`embera.
