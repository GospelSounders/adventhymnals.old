197. Yesu N'Az' Umwijim' Uzahunga

1.
Yesu n' az' umwijim' uzahunga, Yesu n' az' umwijim'uzahunga, 
Impundu zizavuga Yes' aje, Gukorany' intore ze.

Gusubiramo:
Gukorany' abakunzi be (bose);
Gukorany'abakunzi be (bose);
Impundu zizavuga Yes' aje, 
Gukorany' intoreze.

2.
Imibabaro yos' izashira, Imibabaro yos' izashira; 
Ubw'Umwami Yes' azagaruka, Gukorany' intore ze.

3.
Nta wuzongera kurir' ukundi, Nta wuzongera kurir' ukundi;
Umwami Yesu n' aza ku bicu, Gukorany' intore ze.

4.
Hazab' indirimbo z' indamutso, Hazab' indirimbo z'indamutso; 
Z' abakijijwe Yes' agarutse, Gukorany' intore ze.
