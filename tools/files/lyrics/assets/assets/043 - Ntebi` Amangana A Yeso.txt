043 - NTEBI'AMANG'ANA A YESO
"Tell Me The Story Of Jesus"


1
Ntebi` amangana a Yeso, Mbeke onsi omoyo
Nyorokereria boronge, Maya abuete onsi
Ntebie bun` Amalaika, Baterete kaiborwa.
"Obonen` ase Nyasae,`Moremb` obuy`as`ense."

Chorus
Ntebi` emeremo ya Yeso,
Mbeke onsi enkoro
Nyorokereria boronge,
Maya abuete onsi.

2
`Keng`ata bweka eroro, Ntebi`engak` akorete
`S`ebibe biait` agatemwa, Omoeri`akabua
Ntebi` engaki y`emeremo, N`oboror`anyorete
Achayetwe nagochandwa, Motak` otari n`enka.

3
Ntebie ase abambetwe, Akaba n`obororo
Ntebi` ember` atindegetwe, Ntebie un`are moyo
`Bwanchani `S`mang`an` ayio, Bore maiso marore
Karakira ndararera, `Bwanchani bwamboririe.
