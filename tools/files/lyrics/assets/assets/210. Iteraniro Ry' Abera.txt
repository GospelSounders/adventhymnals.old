210. Iteraniro Ry' Abera

1.
Iteraniro ry' abera, Inzovu n' ibihumbi, 
ingabo z' abacunguwe, Barabagirana nk' umucyo, 
Barwany' uko babasha, None baratsinze, 
Mwagurir' amarembo, kugira ngo binjire. 

Gusubiramo:
Haleluya, Haleluya
Dusingiz' Umwana w' Intama, 
Haleluya, Haleluya, 
Nimusingiz' uwazutse.
 
2.
Ikobe ry' indirimbo, Rirangira mu kirere, 
Urusobe rw' amajwi y' inanga, Zizacurangw' urwunge, 
Mbeg' umuns' utangaje, Ni nk' uwo kurema, 
Mbega ng' umubabar' uratsindwa bitavugwa. 

3.
Hanezerew' abari mu mirenge y' i Kanani, 
Igitey' ubwuzu kandi, N' uko batazatandukana, 
Ya maso yariraga uzasang' akeye
Nta mpfubyi nta n' impfusha bizarangw' i Kanani. 

4.
Iteraniro ry' abera, Batinze wa mubisha, 
Nimwumv' izo ndirimbo nziza Ni nk' iz' abamarayika
Hehe n' imibabaro, Bayisezeyeho, 
Ntibazongera usuuz' imitim' ukundi.
