159 - NKORO YAN'EYASIBOIRWE!
"Oh For A Heart To Praise My God"


1
Nkoro yan` eyasiboirw` omotongie Nyasae;
Nyigwe omoy`on` amanying` ari` aitegete.

2
Ekweru`as` obokanyeku Asor` Omoboria,
`Nkoro ekoigw` eriogi ria Krist`agambe ime.

3
Nkoro eyakorirw`enyia yaichoirw` obwanchani,
`Ng` ikeranu _endonge, `nchenu, `ngiya buna ore.

4
Chimbwa chiao aye ong`e `Monene nchwo bwango,
Rik` erieta ri` enkoro yane ri`obwanchani.
