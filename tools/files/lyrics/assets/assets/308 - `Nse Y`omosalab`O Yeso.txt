308 - 'NSE Y'OMOSALAB'O YESO
"Beneath THe Cross Of Jesus"


1
`Nse y`omosalab`o Yeso Ninteneneo, Ekiriri ki`egetare `S`ense yarosire
Enka ere im`as`eroro Gotimok`enchera, Korwa as`omobaso ogwoka Nemechando rero.

2
`Boigamo bw`omogoko, Botamer` obuya, Ase obwanchani bore Boumer` oboronge; `Bagaka batari nenka, Bayenyor` enk`eyio, Nabe omosalaba as`ende, Engasi ya Igoro.

3
`Nse yekiriri kiaye Ensemo moino, Nere embera y`obwoba Er`entamb` engare; Gati yait` omosalaba, `Mabok` ay`amboirie, Buna omorendi as` enchera, Korw` embera eyio.

4
`Gor` asomosalaba Amais` an` arora, Ekieni ki`Omotori`one Onchandekerete; Nkor` eichire namariga `Makumia abere, `Makumia y`obwanchani bwaye, N`ogotaisana kwane.
