236. Ndi Mu Murima

1
Ndi mu murima jyenyine, Ikime gitonze ku byatsi, 
Numv' ijwi rivuga riti: Umwana w' Iman' aratungutse. 
Gusubiramo:
Turakomeza turagendana, 
Maz' ambwira ko yantoye, 
Mbeg' ibyishimo twari dufite, 
Bitigeze bibaho!
2
Aravuga maz' ijwi rye ryiza ryikirizwa n' inyoni, 
Indirimbo nziza namwumvanye ntabw' izamva ku mutima. 
3
Tugumana mu murima, Kuk' ubwire bwari bukubye, 
Maze anyoherez' ankangara, Arikw' ar' ukumpwitura.
