333 - MOBAKE NYASAE
"The God Of Abraham"


1
Mobake Nyasae, Mogambi ore Igoro; Oyore` emiaka yonsi, Omwanchanii;
`Monene Yehova, Ense na Igoro; Twaribak` erieta riao, Ribakwe bo.

2
Mobake Nyasae, `s` ogochika kwaye; Ndw` as`ense ndigi` Omogoko, As` ar`ere; Ntige bionsi ebiense, `Bonene, chisemii; Mokor` ere bweka koba, `Nguba yane.

3
Omoganda bwensi, Mobake Nyasae; Tata, Omwana n`Omoika, `Kero gionsi; Motogie Nyasae, Mbwatisi`aba Igoro; `Bonene bwensi n`obwao, N`ebako bo.
