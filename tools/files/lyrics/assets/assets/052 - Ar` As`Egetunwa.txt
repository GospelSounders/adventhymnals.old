052 - AR'AS'EGETUNWA
"On A Hill Far Away"


1
Ar` as`egetunwa nor` omosalaba,
Bw` emechando n`obosoku;
Naywanchir` Omote, S `omuy` Omosera,
Gatokwer` abanyabibe.

Chorus
Ngoker` omosalaba oria,
bobui bwane mbeke inse
Nintarer` omosalab` oria,
Kaa nyoonchoreri` egutwa.

2
Omosalab` oria, ochayire n`ense,
Nore nomong`us` ase nde;
Ng`ondi ya Nyasae. Gatig`obonene,
Koyoira aria kalwari.

3
`S` Omosalab` oria, Nimbe Omwegenwa,
Nyimoki` obosoku bwaye,
Erio nandangerie, gochia nka igoro,
`Nsange obonene bwaye.
