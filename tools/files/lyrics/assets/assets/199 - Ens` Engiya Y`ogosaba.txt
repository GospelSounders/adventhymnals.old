199 - ENS'ENGIYA Y'OGOSABA
"Sweet Hour Of Prayer"

1
Ens` engiya y`ogosaba,
Kondusi` ense y`emechando
Nakong`ira ase Tata, Motebie kogania kwane
Ekero nde n`obororo, Moy`on`onyor` okoremia
Erio mbua omotemi, Ens` engiya y`ogosaba.

2
Ense engiya y`ogosaba,
Ira gosorora kwane Gochi` as` Ere omwegenwa,
Oyokoru` ogosesenia
`Ntebirie morigererie, Nyegene `Ng`ana n`obuya
Nimoe ker` ogwechanda, Nganyet` Ensa y`ogosaba.

3
Ens` engiya y`ogosaba,
Nyore okoremia kwao Korw` egetunwa gia Pisga,
Ndor`enka yane ndagende `S` obogima nintenene,
Mbwat` ekeegwa gia kare Ng`ak` eriogi as` embeo, "Mbuya Ensa y`ogosaba".
