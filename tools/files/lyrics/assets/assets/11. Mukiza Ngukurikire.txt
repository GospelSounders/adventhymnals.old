11. Mukiza Ngukurikire

1.
Mukiza ngukurikire, Ah' unshakira hose; 
Ah' ujya ngukurikire, Mwami ngukurikire.

Gusubiramo:
Mukiza ngukurikire, Wamviriy' amaraso;
Nib' utagir' ukwitaho, Jye nkwikurikirire.

2.
Nahw' inzira yazitirwa, Naho yakukumuka, 
Ko wayinyuzemo mbere, Nyicamo mfit' ishimwe.

3.
Naho naterwa n' ibyago, Nkageragezwa cyane, 
Warageragejwe nawe, Mwami ngukurikize.

4.
Nib' unyobora mw ishavu, Niba nkena nkanenwa, 
Wigeze kuba ruvumwa, Nanjye ngukurikize.
