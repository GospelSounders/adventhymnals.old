339 - ENSE ENGIYA
"The Summer Land"


1
Gochi` as`ens`engiya ng`umbu, Malaika bagotera, Haleluy` as` Omotoria, Hosan` as`Omogambi; Togotara noboremu, Tokoragwa na Tata, Korw` ense y`ebibe nobororo goch`ens` engiya.

Chorus
Omogoko togoika. Natobeke chigutwa; As` ens`eria engiya, As` ens` eyi` engiya ng`umbu.

2
`Bachenu bande mbachit`as`en`eyio riria kare, `Ngaki bono yaerire, Erio naintwe tosoe; Ntogende bosio rioka, Tobe nabwo batorigwe, Ntotogi` Erieta riaye botamb` as`ens`eyi` engiya.

3
As`ens`eyio obomenyo bwarosigw`ase bonsi, `Ba begenet` Omotoria na koigw`eriogi riaye; `Ngaki y`omogoko ngoch`er`Omonen` arachike, Toyetig` ense ey`embe, Togend` as`ense` eri` engiya.
