156. Amagambo Y' Umujinya

1.
Amagambo y' umujinya, Ntakabave mu kanwa! 
Ahubw'umutim' uyacuby' atararushy' asohoka.

Gusubiramo:
S. na A:
Mukundane rwose; Yes' aravug' ati: 
Ngir'itegeko ryanjye mbahaye: 
Mukundane rwose, Yes' aravug'ati: 
Bana banjye, Mukundane.
T. na B:
Mukundane, Mukundane ; 
Ngir'itegeko ryanjye: 
Mukundane, Mukundane; n' itegeko rye.

2.
Urukundo n' imbonera, N' ubucuti, birera; 
Byab'ishyano kubyicisha, Ubupfu bw' akanya gato.

3.
Amagambo y' intonganya, Ntaregama mu kanwa, 
Ijamborimwe ry' intonganya, Ritandukany' inshuti.
