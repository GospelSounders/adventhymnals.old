188. Nubwo Nsazwe N' Ibyaha

1.
N' ubwo nsazwe n' ibyaha, Yesu yarankijije,
Yamviriy' amaraso,Angura n' urupf' atyo. 
Tuzab' akaramata, Ak' ishami n' giti.

2.
Urukundo rwa Yesu, Rusaguts' ikirere. 
Ntacyo twarugeraho,Ruzagumahw iteka; 
Niwe warumbwirije, Jye ntabwo nari nduzi.

3.
N' ubwo nsazwe n' ibyaha, Yes' andutira byose; 
Az' ibyonkennye byose, Ankiz' umubabaro. 
Namuberey' imbata, Nta cyagocyampangara.
