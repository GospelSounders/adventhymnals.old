16. Mbonez' Intoke Zanjye

1.
Mbonez' intoke zanjye, Zibonere rwose; 
Ko nazihaye Yesu, Ngozimukorere.

Gusubiramo:
Nitondesh' intambwe, 
Aho njya hose.
Nekuyobok' undi,
Keretse Yesu.

2.
Nteg' amatwi ngo numve, Nez' umunsi wose. 
Nkor' ibikwivenumva, Ntagir' icyo mpuga.

3.
Mpang' amabokw' amaso, Ngo ndeb' iby' akora; 
Nyabuzekwenderanya, Akorere Yesu.
