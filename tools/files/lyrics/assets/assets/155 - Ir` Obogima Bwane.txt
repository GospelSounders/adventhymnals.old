155 - IR'OBOGIMA BWANE
    "Take My Life"


1
Ir` obogima bwane, Bochene bob` obwao; 
Ir` amaboko naro, `Kore bun` origetie; 
`Kore bun` origetie.

2
Ir` amagor` ane, `B` amang` as` emeremo; 
Ira n` eriogi riane, Nter` ase Nyasae oka; 
Nter` ase Nyasae oka.

3
Ir` ebikoba biane, Bikwan` ay` ar` ayao; 
Ir` amo n`echinibo, Tindigetie kobisa; 
Tindigetie kobisa.

4
Ira chingaki chiane, Chikobake botambe; 
Ir` obong`aini bwane, Korer` ay` origetie; 
Korer` ay` origetie.

5
Ira ogwancha kwane, Gokore kob` okwao; 
Ir` enkoro yane, Eb` obomenyo bwao; 
Eb` obomenyo bwao.

6
Ir` obwanchani bwane, Nib` as` amagor` ao; 
Ir` omobere bwensi, noyoo bono bori, 
Noyoo bono bori.
