220. Mbese Yesu Ajy' Anyitaho?

1.
Mbese Yesu ajy' anyitaho, Mu byago no mu mahoro, 
Mu mibabaro no mu miruho, No mu rugendo rurushya?

Gusubiramo:
Anyitaho, Arandinda, Mu mibabaro yanjye
Mu bihe merewe nabi, Umukiza wanjy' anyitaho. 

2.
Mbese Yesu ajy' anyitaho, Iyo ngenda mu mwijima
N' iy' umwijima w' akag' ungose, Mbese Yes' amba buufi?

3.
Mbese Yesu ajy' anyitaho, Iyo nguye mu bishuko, 
Iyo mbabaye N' iyo mbogoza Mbese Yes' arantabara?

4.
Mbese Yesu ajy' anyitaho, Iyo nsezeye ku nshuti, 
Iyo mbabazwa, Simpumurizwe, Mbese Yes' ab' abireba?
