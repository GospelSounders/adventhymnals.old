350. Mugenzi Komera

1.
Mugenzi komera Yes' ari hafi, Twamutegereje kera, 
Tugiye kwitahir' iwacu heza, Turirimbe ko dutsinze, 
Twihangane twe kumuhemukaho, Kandi w' ataduhemukiye, 
Yatwerets' urukundo ruhebuje, Nirwo rwatumy' adupfira.
 
2.
Mugenzi komera, Utegerez' umudugudu w' izahabu, 
wateguriwe abakiranutsi, Niho tuzaruhukira, 
Ihangane ntabwo tuzagum' aha, Mu buhanya no mu miruho, 
Harubw' umwijim' uzatamuruka, Igitondo gitangaze. 

3.
Mugenzi komera dore araje, Uwo muremyi wa byose, 
Azanye n' abamarayika benshi, Azanye n' ingororano, 
Ihangan' ugez' igih' Umukiza, Azakubwir' aya magambo, 
Ngo mugaragu mwiz' ukiranuka, Igire mu munezero.
 
4.
Mugenzi jy' uhora wihanganye, Komeza kwizera kwawe, 
Amarira yawe ahanagurwe maz' uzahabwe n' ikamba, 
Ihangane mwene Data ni koko, Isezerano rye n' impamo, 
Uzibanirayo n' abakijijwe, Na Yes' Umukiza wacu.
