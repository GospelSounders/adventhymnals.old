071 - NG'A AY'EBUKU
"Give Me The Bible"


1
Ng`a ay` Ebuku etaya engiya,
Gokony`abanto bachandekire; 
Kende Tikegotangaokwegena, 
Buna Yes` achete gototoria.

Chorus
Ng`a ay` Ebuku amang`an` amaya,
Amasas` aye nanyare kondaa; 
Gochika kwaye eir` obwanchani, 
Goik` omoerio bigenderere.

2
Ng`a ay` Ebuku konde nogoichana, 
Ker"omoyo oichoirwe n`ebibe;
Ng`a ring`ana riakwanetwe na Yeso,
Nyegene morore Yeso ang`e.

3
Ng`a ay` Ebuku inyare komanya, 
Mechando yonsi ere ens`eye;
Amasas` aye aseri` omoichano, 
Ayerabi` enchera y`omorembe.

4
Ng`a ay` Ebuku `taya y`obogima,
Gokoiran` achiche komboria;
Onyorokie etaya ya Igoro,
Nindore obonene bwa Yeso.
