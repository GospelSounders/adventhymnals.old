245 - GOSE MAMBIA ERIOBA RIOKOOROKA
"It May Be At Morn"

1
Gose mambia erioba rikooroka, `Mobas`oseri` omosunte n`ekiriri;
Yeso narache n`obonene bwaye, Kwoyia abanto baye.

Chorus
O! Yeso Kristo ndiriri, Natoak`eriogi? Yes`ochire, haleluya, haleluya nabo Haleluya Amen.

2
Gose mobaso gati gose ko bwaira, Gose nebe obotuko bw`ekeigoro;
Neb`omobaso korw`obonene bwaye, `Kero Yes` Akwoyi` abaye.

3
`Buya n`omogoko titogokwa rinde, `Borwair` obororo n`ebirero mbiyo,
Kobogorigwa as`obonene bwaye, `Kero Yes` Akwoyi` abaye.

4
Emeganda yaye etere Hosana, Amalaika n`abachenu mbabeo;
Obuya nabobe as` obosio bwaye, `Kero Yes` Akwoyi` abaye.
