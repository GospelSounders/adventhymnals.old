248 - KO RIRANDI RIKOBUGIGW'AS'OMOERIO
"When The Trumpet Of The Lord"


1
Ko rirandi rikobugigw` as `omoerio, Natorore omobas` obarire;
N` abatorigwe korw`ense baumeran`aria ng`umbu, Gakorangeri` amarieta nimbeo.

Chorus
Gakorangeri`amarieta, Gakorangeri` amarieta, Gakorangeri` amarieta, Gakorangeri` amarieta nimbeo.

2
Rituk`erio n`omogok` abachenu bakoboka, Nabakwete kare nababoke pi;
Abachore nabagende na Nyasae igoro, Gakorangeri` amarieta nimbeo.

3
Natokore emeremo ya Nyasae eere, Tokwan`obwachani_obo bw`ogokumia; Eri` emerem` ekoera natogende Igoro, Gakorangeri` amarieta nimbeo.
