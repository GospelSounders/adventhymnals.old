125. Nk' Ukw' Izuba Rituvira

1.
Nkukw' izuba rituvira, Rikarabagirana,
Nik' umucyo wo mw'ijuru wansabye mu mutima. 
Wawukuyemw iby' amanjwe, Utangaz'ibihishwe; 
Kuko mu mutima wanjye, Nahatuj' umukiza.

Gusubiramo:
Mbeg' umucyo w' agatangaza,
Wansabye mumutima! 
Ineza y' Umwami Yesu,
Yambereye nk' umucyo.

2.
Natay' agahinda kose; Ntabwo nkikitayeho; 
Kuk' unyambik'amahoro, Ameze nk' umwitero.
Kwerekejeh' umutima, Ndakwishimiyecyane. 
Nzagezw' i wawe mw' ijuru, Na rwa rukundo rwawe.

3.
Mukiza waranshunguye; Nd' uwaw' uko ndi kose.
Nyemereranguhimbaze, Nkuber' umucyo waka. 
Nkwerekejeh' umutima, Ndakwishimiyecyane. 
Nzagezw' i wawe mw' ijuru, Na rwa rukundo rwawe.
