236 - AMANG'AN 'AMAY'AS 'OMOGENDI
"How Sweet Are The Tidings"

1
Amang`an `amay` as` omogendi, `Yotayayete korwa nka; Omotoria mbwang`arairan`ense, N`obonda bwaye boche.

Chorus
Ngoch` are, Mbwang` arairan` ens` eye; Namanya nairane bwango, N`abagendi bonsi, baegw`obonene, `Ker` Omotori`aragambe.

2
Chimber`as` abachenu baraire, Nachimanorwe kegima; Baria bonsi baraire mbaboke, `Rio naende bamenye.

3
Ntoumeran` as`enka y`omogoko, Toter` ogotoreka; Korwa chinsinyo chionsi chi`ense`eye, Tomosasim` Omogambi.

4
Haleluya amen Haleluya nabo, Gatoregene ntoike nka; Tosabe mono torendete, Ntobek` egutw` enyia.
