37. Nkuyoboke Mwami

1.
Nkuyoboke Mwami, Nkuyoboke.
N' aho natendekwa ku musaraba,
Naba nkiririmba: nkuyoboke Mwami, 
Nkuyoboke Mwami, Nkube bugufi.

2.
Nkuyoboke Mwami, Nkuyoboke.
Mu makuba yose, Mpagarikira! 
Ninterwa n' ibyago, Umpe kwihangana,
Nkuyoboke Mwami, Nkube bugufi.

3.
Nkuyoboke Mwami, Nkuyoboke. 
Jy' untabar' iteka ngumyenkwizere! 
Umwanzi n' antera, Ujy' undwanirira!
Nkuyoboke Mwami,Nkube bugufi.

4.
Nkuyoboke Mwami, Nkuyoboke. 
Wakinguy' ijuru, Nyobor' ah'uri. 
Mugenzi komera, Urenda gukira! 
Nkuyoboke Mwami, Nkube bugufi.
