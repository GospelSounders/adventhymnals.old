001 - `MOCHENU OMOCHENU
"073 - Holy, Holy, Holy"

1
`Mochenu_Omochenu or` Omonguru,
Ekero bogokia tokobake Aye;
`Mochenu `Monyamabera bw`echinguru,
Nay`omogambi kare na kare.

2
`Mochenu malaika bagosasima,
Babek` egutw`as`enyancha y`ekerore;
`Megand` emeganda bakogosasima,
Naye oreo kare na kare.

3
`Mochenu nony`omosunt`ogontuba,
Nonya titokoror`obonene bwao;
Nay`or` omochenu kobua bonsi,
`S`obwanchani, `nguru n`obochenu.
