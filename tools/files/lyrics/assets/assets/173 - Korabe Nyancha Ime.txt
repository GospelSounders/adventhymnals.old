173 - KORABE NYANCHA IME
"When Upon Life's Billows

1
Korabe nyancha ime as`omwaga,
Aroro okage gwaisire gokwa;
Chaka kobara ebisio obwate,
Nokumie mon` obuya bwa Nyasae.

Chorus
Bar` ebisio, `kemo ekemo,
Many` eki Nyasa` atomire,
Bar` ebisio, `kemo ekemo,
Nokumie `buya bwa Nyasae mono.

2 
Korabe kwarosire n`egurube,
Gokwarengereirie kwabuirwe,
Chaka kobara ebiso obwate,
`Mogoko ay` ogotera nonyore.

3
Ker` okorora enibo y`abande,
Inyor` eira y`enibo ya Kristo,
Bar`ebisi` otagore ne chibesa,
Ng`eria yao nenka ere Igoro.

4
Igo as`esegi tagoichana,
Nyasa` ere bwanchire gotokonya,
Bar`ebisio `malaika mbabeo, 
Mbagokonye goika omoerio.
