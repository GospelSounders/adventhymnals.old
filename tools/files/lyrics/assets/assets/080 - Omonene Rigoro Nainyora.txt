080 - OMONENE RIGORO NAINYORA
"How Great Thou Art"


1
Monen`one `S`ogokumia nkoinyora;
Bint`amabok`ao akorete,
Ndora ng`enang`eni nyigwa chinkoba: 
Bioroki`ense okobua kwao.

Chorus
Nkoro yan`eterer` Omotoria;
Monen` Aye Bw`ogokumia!
Nkoro yan`eterer` Omotoria;
Monen` Aye Bw`ogokumia.

2
Kongoeta chinsana ime ng`ichana;
Nyigwa chinyoni chikogotogia
Gankorigereria korw`ebitunwa;
Ndoche chioga mbeo engiy`egusa.

3
Nkoinyora buna kwaruet`Omwan` oo;
Kamotom`ache gokwa ng`ichana,
Kabogori`ebib`as` Omosalaba;
Manying`agaitek`eri`agansibia.

4
Kristo karache n`eriogi rinene;
Kong`ira nka ng`ichorw`omogoko,
Ntung`ame mosasime n`oboitongo;
Ndari`agwo Nyasae Bwogokumia.
