033 - TATA TOSIBORE BONO
"Lord Dismiss Us"


1
Tata tosibore bono, N`ogwancherab okuya
Toe omogoko oo, Torusie ebibe pi 
Toe gwancha, Toe gwancha
Gatokogend` ens` eye.

2
Twagokire rero mono, As` amang`ana ao; 
Togwanch` ogochika kwao, Natogoke botambe
Natoteme natoteme Kogotokonya aye.

3
Gatokogenda ens`eye, mbuya togokorere 
Gatogotunya enchera, Totokona tatiga, 
Obe ang`e, obe ang`e Goika Oirane.
