111 - TWAGOSABIRE CHINGURU
"O For That Flame!"


1
Twagosabire chinguru,
Chiria chiare riria kare
Chiagerete bakerua pi,
Bakabua emechando.

2
Ng`ai Omoika oria ore, 
`Koret` Abramu oyoo? 
`Kanyete Paul` enkoro,
`Kaba na nguru chia Igoro.

3
Omoik` oria orandetie, 
`Bwanchani chinchera chiao 
`Karabi` amarik` a Isaya,
N`amatera a Daudi.

4
Buya bwao mbori nguru,
`Bun` ekero kia Elija 
Musa akaror` obonene,
Ayub` akaremereria?

5
Inyor` amatuko aria,
Keror` emeremo
yao Gatwakoeire chinkoro, 
Toiterere omoik` oo.
