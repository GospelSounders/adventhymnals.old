370 - OROGENDO RWA YESO
"O Happy Band Of Pilgrims"


1
Orogendo rwa Yeso, Mogoko noyoino, 
Motunyane Yeso Oino Imanweli;
Koborab`obororo, Tari ban`obwaye,
Enchera mogoeta, Nero aetete.

2
`Mosalaba o Yeso, Nainwe moimokie,
N`engata abegetwe, Nainwe negwenerete;
Ogwanch`okwo okwaye, Mobe nakorokwo,
Na Obwanchani bwaye, Boikaranse nainwe.

3
Chingencho mwakorerwe mwanche igo nomokia, 
Mwensi morek` enchera igo bun` etaya;
`Bororo n`emekono, N`emechando Yaino;
Ogotemwa ao ao, Goika ogokwa kwaino.

4
Yonsi nero eng`eria, Bun`Ere anyorete,
Nebe buna obororo, Igoro korw`ense;
Orogendo rwa Yeso, Rigereria Igoro;
`Mechando Y`ense`eye, Nyebue as`are.
