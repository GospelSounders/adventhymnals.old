211. Nimwuname Imbere Ya Yehova

1.
Nimwunam' imbere ya Yehova
Yemwe maana yo mw' isi
Mumenye yuk' Uhorahw' ari we Mana, 
Niwe soko y' ubugingo; 

2.
Kand' imbaraga ze zitangaje, 
Zaturemye mu butaka, 
Ndets' igihe twazimiraga nk' intama, 
Yatugaruye mu bushyo. 

3.
Tumusingirize mu ndirimbo, 
Duhanik' amajwi yacu, 
Kand' indim' ibihumbi zo mw' isi yose, 
Zizasingiz' Uhoraho.
 
4.
Amateka y' ageng' isi yose, 
Nta wager' urukundo rwe, 
Ukuri kwe gushikamye nk' urutare, 
Ukw' ibihe bih' ibindi.
