164. Nicay' Imbere Ya Yesu

1.
Nicay' imbere ya Yesu, Mpaboner' ihumure;
N' ahantu h'igikundiro; Icyampa nkahibera. 
Iyo nicay' imbere ye, Nimenyah'ubugoryi; 
Kumuhang' amaso ni byo, Bimpa gukiranuka.

2.
Kwicar' imbere ya Yesu, Nta mahirw' arut' ayo; 
Niho nsig'ibimbabaza, Nkumva nduhutse rwose. 
Iyo nicay' imbere ye, Nkundakumuganyira; 
Naw' ababarana nanjye, Maz' akampumuriza.

3.
Yesu mp' umugisha wawe; Dore, Ndategereje;
Ndeban' imbabazizawe, Menye k' unyitayeho.
Mp' umutima wawe, Yesu; Nyezankubonerere; 
Bigaragarir' abandi, Yuko mporana nawe.
