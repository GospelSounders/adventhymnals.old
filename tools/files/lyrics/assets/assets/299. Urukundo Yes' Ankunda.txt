299. Urukundo Yes' Ankunda

1.
Urukundo Yes' ankunda, Rwatumy' abambwa ku giti, 
Kugira ngo nkomeze kubaho, Ntawarurondora. 

Gusubiramo:
Yankunz' urukundo rwinshi (urukundo rwinshi), 
Yankunz' urukundo rwinshi (urukundo rwinshi), 
Urukundo Umukiz' ankunda, Nta warurondora. 

2.
Uburyo yambabarijwe, Yambitsw' ikamba ry' amahwa, 
Kugira ngo nkomeze kubaho, Nta wabirondora.

3.
Mfit' amahoro muri we, Kukw' ansabira kuri se, 
Uko yankirishij' amaraso, Nta wabirondora. 

4.
Iyo ndi kumwe na Yesu, Amber' uburuhukiro, 
Ibyiringiro mfite muri We, Nta wabirondora.
