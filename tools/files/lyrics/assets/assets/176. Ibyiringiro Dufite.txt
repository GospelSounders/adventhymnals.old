176. Ibyiringiro Dufite

1.
Ibyiringiro dufite bigir' agaciro cyane; 
Kurut' iby'iyi si by' imburamumaro,
Bijya bishimishabenshi.

2.
Twabony' umucy' utangaje, Utamurur' umwijima, 
Wazanywen' uk' urupfu rwiganzuy' isi,
None twaretsekwiheba.

3.
Twumvis' ijwi ry' ihumure, Rinyur' imitima yacu; 
Ridutinyurarwose, rikatwizeza,
Yukw' Iman' ar'urukundo.

4.
Iryo jwi n' irya Rurema, Uwo mucyo n' Ijambo rye; 
Ibyobyiringiro n' ukuragw' ijuru,
Ntitwongere gupf'ukundi.
