"Sweet The Time"

Engaki_ engiya mono, Bachenu bacha amo;
K`Omotori` ar` eganga, `Kero bakomotera.

2
Totere obwanchani, bwamosugetie Tata
Aroch` ens` etaraba, Akamoru` Omwan` oye.

Ter` ogwancha kw` Omwana, bun` atigete igoro;
Akaba buna tore, Agakwa atotorie.

4
Obwanchani bw` Omoika, kwororobia chinkoro;
Kero tware nobwoba, Gatoa Omotoria.

5
Engaki engiya mono, `Bachenu baumerane;
Yeso nabe eganga, Bamotere botambe.
