187. Mwam' Ubwo Tugushengereye

1.
Mwam' ubwo tukigushengereye,
Tukuririmbirir' icya rimwe 
Iyindirimbo yac' iheruka,
Twicishije bugufi, duhire.

2.
Mwami dusezerer' amahoro; 
Duherekeze, tujyane nawe;
Turindir' iminwa n' imitima,
Ngo yoye kujy' igukoz' isoni.

3.
Amahoro yaw' abane natwe; 
Ab' ari y' ajy' aduh' ihumure.
Maze n' uturuhur' imiruho, 
Tuzongere kuyabon' i wawe.
