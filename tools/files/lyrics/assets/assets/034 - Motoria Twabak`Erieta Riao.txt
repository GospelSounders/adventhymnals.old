034 - MOTORIA TWABAK 'ERIETA RIAO
"Saviour Again To Thy Dear Name"

1
Motoria twabak`erieta riao,
As`ogotera kw`ogotigana;
Gatwakorire ogosasima,
Twatung`ama kogany`omorembe.

2
Toe `morembe gatokogenda,
Twachaka twakora ituko naye;
Rend`emenwa n`enchinkoro chiaito,
Abwo bagwatoir`as`enyomb`eye

3
Toe` morembe gator`ens` eye,
Riog` as `obororo n`amatemwa;
Ker`orachik`esegi eere,
Toe `moremb` okare na kare.
