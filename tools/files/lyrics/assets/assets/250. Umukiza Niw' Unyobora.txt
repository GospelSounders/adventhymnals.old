250. Umukiza Niw' Unyobora

1.
Umukiza niw' unyobor' iyo ndi mu bishuko, 
N' iy' amakub' angose, amber' ubuhungiro, 
Niw' unkingir' ibishuko, Yesu nyiyoborere.

2.
Uhosh' umuraba nk' umubyey' uhoz' umwana we, 
Ndets' uranawutegek' ubwaw' ukakumvira, 
Mwami wanjye Yesu nkunda, Ujy' Unyiyoborera.
 
3.
Iyo negerey' inkengero ngashy' ubwoba, Nkakwihishamo, 
Ndaruhuka, Nkumv' umpumuriz' uti: 
Witinya ntacy' uteze kuba, Nzajya nkuyobora.
