130 - KER 'EMAMBIA
"New Every Morning"


1
Ker`emambia nobwanchani, Torenda rituko rionsi. Gatwarendirw`obotuko, Meber` ere nechinguru.

2
Kabogokia n`amabera, Se tore gatogosaba;
Mechand`eete twaberwe, Tosemeretie igoro.

3
Emeremo ye rituko, Toisa koyerengereria;
Bond`oboyia, nib` enene, Nyasae nerue ekengw`anso.

4
Bimoremo bisinini, Mbitotange titosaba; 
Na kwang` enchera endonge, `Gotoiki` ase Nyasae.

5
S`obwanchani bwao Tata, Tobwenere gotimoka;
Tokonye rero botambe, Tobe buna togosaba.
