172. Ngabo, Muhaguruke!

1.
Ngabo, Muhaguruke! Murwanire Yesu; 
Murind' ingabeyanyu, Mutazayinyagwa.
Mu nzira yo kunesha, Tuyoborwa naYesu, 
Tujyane naw' iteka, Niho tuzanesha.

2.
Impanda ziravuze, Muze mwebwe mwese. 
Ikamba ry'ubugingo, N' ibihembo byanyu. 
Nimwangwa n' ababisha,Mukomere, murwane;
Mwonger' umwete mwinshi, Muganze Satani.

3.
Mubyuke, Mutabare, Mudashidikanya. 
He kugir' umw'usiba, murebe kwa Yesu. 
Kandi mujye mutwara, Intwaro zokwizera. 
Muhabwe ukunesha, N' ibyishimo byinshi.

4.
Mubyuke vuba vuba, Murwane iteka, 
Mwizer' izinaryera, Niho muzanesha; 
Maz' izo ntwaro zose, Muzih' UmwamiYesu,
Muzabon' amahoro y' iteka mw ijuru.
