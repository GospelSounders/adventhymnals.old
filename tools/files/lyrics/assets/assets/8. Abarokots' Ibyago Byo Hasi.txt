8. Abarokots' Ibyago Byo Hasi

1.
Abarokots' ibyago byo hasi hazakoranywa, Ab' azatumir'azajyana na bo. 
Umwami n' agaruk' ikondera rye rizavuga, Amatek'azab' agiye gucibwa.

Gusubiramo:
Azaba yabararitse, Ubw' azabahamagara (hamagara).
Azaba yabararitse, Ab' azatumir' azajyana na bo.

2.
Uwo muns' abapfuye muri Kristo bazazurwa, Bazava mu bituronka we rwose.
Abo yitoreye bazimana na we mw' ijuru, Ubw' azagenda,azajyana na bo.

3.
Ni ho tuzajya dukorer' Umwami tudahwema; Tuzajya dusingiz'urukundo rwe.
Maz' ubwo tuzarangiz' imirimo yacu mw' isi,Tuzateranywa tujyanwe mw' ijuru.
