021 - NGWEKANE BUNA YESO
"More Like Jesus I Would Be"

1
Ngwekane buna Yeso; Amenye ime yane,
Ng`ichorie obwanchani, Nindabe omwororo,
Ngwekane buna Yeso, kangotara as`ense, 
Mbe n`omoika oye, `Monene nchwo ase nde.

2
Gakoigwa chinyoni, Nagochirenda buya,
Nanyigwe ekerero, n`ogochandeka kwane;
Nanyorokie boronge, Anyabere `komocha,
Nchenek` enkoro yane `Monene nchwo ase nde.

3
Ngwekane buna Yeso; Amatuko onsi pi,
Nintimoke as`are, Ninyw` amache amaya;
Nimbe n`obuya bwaye, Nyor` obwanchani bwaye, 
Ninyegene kegima `Monene nchwo ase nde.
