"Just As I Am"

Tindi na egetamero, Manying`ao `ngitekera, 
Gwantebirie nchich` as`or` Eng`ondi ya Nyasae, Nchiche.

2
Buna nde intaganyeti Gwetinyi `Omoy`on` ekebe, 
Manyingao `tinyi `ebibe Eng`ondi ya Nyasae, Nchiche.

3
Buna nde nkorutwarutwa, Nechitang`u tang`u chionsi, 
`Segi ime bwoba `sik `Eng`ondi ya Nyasae, Nchiche.

4
Nd`Omotaka omouko, Kogweni` ebirengererio, 
Nakwo nganetie as`or` Eng`ondi ya Nyasae, Nchiche.

5
Buna nde Aye nong`ire, Ondosi `onyaber `onsibie, 
Negenir`eira yao Eng`ondi ya Nyasae, Nchiche.

6
Buna nde Bwanchani bwao, Bwatagoire Ker`etoto, 
Bono mb`oyoo kegim` Eng`ondi ya Nyasae, Nchiche.
