75. N' Ijwi Ry' Umwungeri Numva

1
N' ijwi ry' Umwungeri numva, Ritabariza mw' ishyamba; 
Kw'intama ze zazimiye; Mu bushyo niho zavuye.

Gusubiramo:
Nimuze, Nimuze, Tuzikurikire mw'ishyamba! 
Nimuze, Nimuze, Tuzigarurire Yesu!

2
Dutabar' uwo mwungeri, Dushakan' izo nzimizi! 
Tuzigarure mubushyo, Niho zikirir' imbeho!

3
Ziratakira mw' ishyamba, Zigeze habi mu manga. 
Umwam'arababwir' ati: Munshakir' intama zanjye!
