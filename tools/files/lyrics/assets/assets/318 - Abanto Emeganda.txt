318 - ABANTO EMEGANDA
"Ten Thousand Times Ten Thousand"


1
Abanto emeganda, Ne chianga chindabu, Barina ase amare, Kogenda Igoro; Bakorir`okorwana, N`ebibe bi`ens`eye, Ebisieri mbimanorwe, `Babui basoe.

2
Batera Aleluya! Ense na Igoro, Amariogi y`amakano, Arari`obobui; Rituko ri`omogoko `S`ebitongwa bionsi, Emechando n`amakweri, Biaerire bono.

3
Rituk`erio abaya, Nabaumerane, Abakwete n`abasani, Tibagotigana; Amais` onsi ng`arabe, Amarig` asire, Chintakana nachinyore, Abaibori babo.

4
Reta ogotereka, Ngwakweret` ababe, Kora Korika ebuku, Inchwo eri` ogambe; Ika ay` Omonene, `Banto mbaganyete, Oroki` ekemanyererio, Mogambi Omotoria.
