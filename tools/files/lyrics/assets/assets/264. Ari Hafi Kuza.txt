264. Ari Hafi Kuza

1.
Ari hafi kuza, Nahimbazwe Yesu, Azakorany' abatoni be, 
Bajyane mw' ijuru, Turebe mw' ijuru, twishime araje!
aje mu cyubahiro cye, Mucunguzi wacu.
 
2.
Ari hafi kuza, Nahimbazwe Yesu, Imibabaro yacu yose, 
Ntizibukw' ukundi, Yes' azakuraho, Urupfu burundu, 
Azaza tuban' iteka, Mucunguzi wacu. 

3.
Ari hafi kuza, Nahimbazwe Yesu, Azaz' atungutse ku bicu, 
Ntidutinye rwose, Twubure amaso, Nguriya araje!
Aje kujyana njye nawe, Mucunguzi wacu.
