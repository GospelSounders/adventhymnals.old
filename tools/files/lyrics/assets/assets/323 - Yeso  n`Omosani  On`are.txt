323 - YESO N 'OMOSANI ON'ARE
"The Lily Of The Valley"


1
Yeso  n`Omosani  on`are,  Nere  bionsi  ase  nde; `S`enkoro  yane  nabuete  bonsi;    Nig`are bun `egesicha  kie  rigok`ase  nde;  `Kinganetie nsibigwe  mb`omogima. `S`  obor`andemia, `s`emechand  `ambisia; Antebia  motwek` amakong`u onsi.

Chorus
Ner`egesicha  ekiya, Ng`enang`eni endabu; `S` enkoro yane nabuete bonsi.

2
Er` ondusiri`  ogwechanda,  N`obororo  bwane; Nere  obotamer`  as`  amatemwa.    Natugutire bionsi  pi, Nony` emebwekano  Korw` enkoro er` or`omorendi,  one. `Nse  yonsi  yanyangire, Na  saitani  antema; Konde  na  Yeso  nimb` omobui.

3
Yaya  ere  tagontiga,  Gose Kombwatoka  Ko mwegenete  na  komokorera  Ongiteire  n`omorero, Tinkwoboa  kende; `Nyigotirie  n`endagera  ya Igoro, Ninso`as  obonene,  Ndor`obosio  bwaye; `Se  chindoche  chiomogoko  chire.
