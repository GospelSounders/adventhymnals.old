1. Murinzi We, Vuz' Impanda

1.
Murinzi, Jy' uvuz' impanda, Uhan' umuntu wese. 
Ng' uzumv' izo nkuru nziza, Yihan' akizw' ibyaha.

Gusubiramo:
Murinzi we vuz' impanda! 
Yivuze cyane hose (hose). 
Menyesha bos' ubutumwa, 
Imbohe zibohorwe.

2.
Yivugirize mu mpinga, Mw' ishyamba mu gisiza; 
Ndetse no mu nyanja hose, Bameny' ako gakiza.

3.
Yivugirize mu nzira, Mu rwiherero hose.
Dat' arabategereje, Nta cy' ataringanije.

4.
Uyivugiriz' indembe, Nazo zikizw' ibyaha. 
Yes' arabatumir' ati: Nimuze mbaboneze.
