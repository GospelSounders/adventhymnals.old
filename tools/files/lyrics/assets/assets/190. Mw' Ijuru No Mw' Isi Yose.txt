190. Mw' Ijuru No Mw' Isi Yose

1.
Mw' ijuru no mw' isi yose, Nta zina nk' irya Yesu; 
Ntaryo nkunda ku mutima, Nk' iry' uwaducunguye.

Gusubiramo:
N' izina (n' izina) rya Yesu (rya Yesu),
Niryo ritwiringiza; N' izina (n' izina)
Ry' umukiza, Riduhesh'amahirwe.

2.
Nihagir' umunt' ushimwa, Akarutishw' abandi,
N' abarush'icyubahiro, Yes' aramuhebaza.

3.
Abamaraika byose. Bitw' amazina meza, 
Ariko nta zina ryabo,Ribasha kunnezeza.

4.
Yes' ankiz' ibyago byose, Kandi niw' unkomeza; 
N' iyonagiz' intege nke, Anyonger' imbaraga.
