352 - 'MONENE ITUKO RIAERIRE
"The Day Thou Gavest"


1
`Monene_ituko riaerire, 
Obotuko bwaikire;
Natokobake n`ogotera,
`Motimok`osesenirie.

2
Twairanirie buya`as`ore, 
`Kanisa yao terairi;
Nig`erendete`asens`engima, 
`Botuko n`omobaso bo.

3
Igor`as`ebisinka bionsi, 
Boira, bwakia kera rituko;
`Riogi ri`ogosaba ndiereti, 
Nonya neriogokobaka.

4
Borwoti bwao mbokoera,
Buna amanda y`ens`eye;
Mbokine rioka goika Kare, 
Bana bao bair`omwando.
