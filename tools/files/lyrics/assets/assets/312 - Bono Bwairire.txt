312 - BONO BWAIRIRE
"Now The Day Is Over"


1
Bono bwairire Riagorobire;
Tobiswe naende `Mosunte_ime.

2
Bono natorore Ching`enang`eni;
Ebitongwa bionsi Nkorara bire.

3
Renda abarwaire N`abakoromwa;
`S`amaber`amange Tiga tomiamie.

4
Ase obotuko `Malaik`ao,
Bache batorende Gatwararire.

5
Erio agwo mambia Gatokoboka,
Tore abachenu `S`obosio bwao.
