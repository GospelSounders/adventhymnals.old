152. Ngwino Yesu

1.
Ngwino Yes' uvugane, N' umutima wanjye: Maz' umpumuriz' uti:
Ntabwo nagusize. Nyugurur' umutima, Nihutire kumva, 
Ijwi ryawe ry'impuhwe, Nyuzuz' ibyishimo.

Gusubiramo:
Mbwiz' ijwi ryongorera, Mbwiran'urukundo; 
Ut' uzanesh' iteka, Ub' umudendezo. 
Vugana nanjy'iteka, Mbwir' ubudahwema;
Reka numv' uk' umbwira ngo:sinaguhanye.

2.
Yobora abana bawe, Uvugane nabo; Ubuzuz' ibyishimo,
Bigekugusenga. Mwami, Bitange rwose, Bakwiyegurire; 
Tebuts' ubwamibwawe, Twibanire nawe.

3.
Ngwin' uvugane natwe, Uduhishurire iby' imigambi yawe, 
N'inshingano yacu. Duhe kuguhimbaza, Tukubahirize, 
Tujye tunezererwagukor' iby' ushaka.
