366 - NATOGENDE INTWE TWENSI
"Help Me Finf A Place"

1
Natogende intwe twensi as` emeremo yaye,
Natokore nomogoko, ne chinguru chionsi, 
`banto bonsi baganyete, as` ogokonywa gwaito, 
Natobatang `ane bono, enchera y` Omonene.

Chorus
Nchwo togend`, as` omogondo, as` emeremo yaye, 
Nonya `meke, gos` emenge, ninyekore nomokia, 
`Banto bonsi, nimbakonye, nobwanchani, nobuya,
Nchwo `Monene, nkonye bono, nkore emeremo yane.

2
Natogende, aborokia, as` enyomba ya Nyasae, 
Toborokie abanto bonsi, chingencho chia Nyasae,
Titokwa Moyo, as` ense tiga tosabe mono,
Erio natong`use abange, ase Omokonyi Oito.

3
Natokonye Abakristo, as` emeremo yaye,
Kabe ang`e koyeimokia, `mesigo `merito, 
Tob`ang`e gochiimokia `gurube chindito,
Natoike seito bori, as` Obonda bwa Yeso.
