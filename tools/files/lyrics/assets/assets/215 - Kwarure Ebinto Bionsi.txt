215 - KWARURE EBINTO BIONSI EKENG'WANSO?

"Is Your All On The Alter"


1
Kwaganirie omorembe, n`okwegena, Gwasabire as`ogweitia? 
Kotogotimoka; gos`osesenigwe, Goika bono bweru`ekeng`wanso.

Chorus
Kwarure ebinto bionsi ekeng`wanso? Omoika ngokoraa Are?
Norasesenigwe, onyor`omorembe, Koramo` omobere n`enkoro.

2
Nobe n`Omonene `se Ringana riaye, Obe n`omoremb `oisaneke;
Korer`ogochika, Ob`omochenu pi, Oru`ebiao bionsi ekeng`wanso.

3
Titokomany` eki Omonen `atoeire, As`ebisi`ebi twasabire.  Mebere, chinkoro, goik `abigambere, Bionsi torue as`ekeng`wanso.

4
Mbori gotebek` obwanchani-atoeire, Chinkoro chiaito na chigoke, 
`Buya bw`ogosanga tosange Nere, Gatwarure bionsi_ekeng`wanso.
