146 - IGWA ERIOGI RIA YESO
"Hark The Voice Of Jesus"


1
Igwa Eriogi Ria Yeso, Rikorangeria iga, `Ing`o
bwanchire kogenda, Korem` omogond` one?"
Botambe nkorangeri` are, `Ng`eria yaye nenene;
Ing`o bwanchire atebe, "Naond` aiga, ntoma".

2
Onye tokwambok` enyancha, goiker` abasiru 
Mbare bataraonchoka, ang` as`ore bakonye 
`Takwane buna Paulo, gos` abamalaika 
Randi` obwanchani bwa Yeso, buna abakwerete.

3
Onye tokob` Omorendi, `S` orwaki rwa Zayoni
Korag` enchera ya igoro na kwerua ase bonsi
As`ogosaba n`enibo, nokore aya Igoro, 
`Mwegenwa buna Aroni, akonyet` Omobani.

4
Kabant` abange bagokwa, Yeso okorangeirie
Toigwek`aye kogoteba: "Kende nkeri ndakore".
Bwata emeremo eye, Yekor` as` omogoko; 
Korarangerigwe teba; "Naond` aiga ntoma".
