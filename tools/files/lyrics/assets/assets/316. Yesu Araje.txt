316. Yesu Araje

1.
Yes' araje nimubyamamaze, Aje kujyan' abo yacunguye, 
Azanywe no kwimik' ingoma ye, Yesu agiye kuza. 

Gusubiramo:
Yes' agiye kugaruka vuba, 
Yesu agiye kuza (agiye kuza), 
Nimubyamamaz' ahantu hose, Agiye kugaruka.
 
2.
Yes' araj' abapfuye bazuke, Maz' abatoni be baterane, 
Bahurire nawe mu kirere, Yesu agiye kuza. 

3.
Yes' azanywe no gukiz' abera, Azanywe no gutang' amahoro, 
Imibabaro yos' izashira, Yesu agiye kuza.
 
4
Yes' araje nk' uko yabivuze, Ni bande batowe bamwizeye, 
Bamutegereje bari maso, Yesu agiye kuza.
