006 - Tata twagocheire

1
Tata twagocheire, Intwe twaremeirwe;
Nay`obotamero, Twasoroire.
`Nse`ger`omosunte, Eker`otario;
Kab`Omoremi`oito, Tat`o`bonsi.

Chorus
Tata twagocheire, totoombia
`Gw`ogosaba gwaito ntw`abareu,

2
Totorie Korw`as` ababisa n`obobe;
Nay`obotimok`as` okogania.
`Se chitang`utang`u n`obwor`otoremie;
`Se chinguru chiao otokong`ie.

3
Toe `buya bwao tob`abao bori;
Tami`amagor`aito korw`ebibe.
Twasoroire torae goika tobue;
Tomeny`amo nay`eng`umbu_engiya.
