121. Nzasingiz' Urukundo Rwa Yesu

1.
Nzasingiz' urukundo rwa Yesu, Wambambiwe; 
Yemeyekumanuka, Azanywe no kumpfira.

Gusubiramo:
Nzasingiz' urukundo rwe; 
Mpimbazeyaramfiriye; 
Ashaka kumbeshaho; 
Nzahorammuhimbaza.

2.
Yesu yambabarijwe, Ntararushya mbabara;
Yahoze ansabira,Ntarameny' iyo bijya.

3.
Urukundo rw' Imana, Mbega ngo rurankiza! 
Ibyaha byanjyebyose bikantamururwaho!

4.
Nta cyiza nakoreye, Umwami wankunz' atyo; 
None mmazekwemezwa, Mwami nguhaye byose.
