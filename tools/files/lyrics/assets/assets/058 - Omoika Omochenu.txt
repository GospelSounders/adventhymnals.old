058 - OMOIKA OMOCHENU
"Holy Spirit Faithful Guide"


1
Omoika Omochenu, Omorai ` Mwegenwa; 
Tobwate okoboko, Abator` abatari;
Toche as`ore toigwe, Eriogi ri`obwororo,
`Motari, nchwo ase nde, Ningoikie Igoro.

2
Naye or`Omosani, `Gokonywa kore  ang`e;
Tototiga emechando, N`ase omosunt`ime; 
K`emerind` ekogusa, Chinkoro chikwoboa, 
Monyerere "Motari, Inchuo ningotang`ane."

3
`Meremo gekoera, ogany`ogotimoka;
Totari n` okogania, Tatiga Igoro oka;
Titori n`okogania, Manyinga a  Yes`oka, 
Monyerere "Motari, Notimoke Igoro."
