163 - OMONENE NDIGETI' EKE
"One Thing Of Thee Lord I Desire"

1
Omonene ndigetie eke, Kiagera nakomocheire,
As` omorero n`amache, Onsibie mono kegima.

Chorus
Onsibie ime naisiko; Gos` as`omorer` onsibie,
`S` encher` ende yonsi nsibie; `Bibe bikwe ime yane.

2
Koranyoroki` oborori, Ninyare kogoka mono,
Kondabe n` enkor` enchenu, mbuy` ase nde, mbuy` ase nde.

3
Bun` enkor` ey` er`enchenu, mbe n`oborori obonge;
Naki as`_obogima bwao, Ndor` ebinto ebichenu.

4
Omonene natemire, gotanga ay`ar`amabe;
Korende tinkonyara pi, gwechena nche omonyene.
