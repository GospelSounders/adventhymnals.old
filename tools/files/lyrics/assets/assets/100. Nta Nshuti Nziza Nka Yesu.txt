100. Nta Nshuti Nziza Nka Yesu

1.
Nta nshuti nziza nka Yesu, Atsemb' ibyaha byacu.
N' amahirwe kumwizera, Kukw' atwuzuza na Se. 
Iyo tubabaye rwose, Dufit' ishavu ryinshi, 
Adusohoza ku Mana, Yumv' amaganya yacu.

2.
Nitugir' ibyago byinshi, Tugatotezwa cyane, 
Byoye kuduter' ubwoba, Tumwiringire rwose; 
Nta wundi wabidukiza, Abiduhemuraho. 
Yes' az' intege nke zacu, Jya wisung' Uwiteka.

3.
Ni tugir' ishavu ryinshi, Tukababara rwose, 
Yesu niw' uturokora; Komez' umwizigire. 
Nibakugira ruvumwa, Wisung' Umwami Yesu. 
Ntazagir' ubw' aguhana, Jy' uhor' umwizigiye.
