58. Jy' Uganza Mwami w' Iteka

1.
Jy' uganza Mwami w' iteka, Bose bakuramye!
Nimuzan' ikambary' ubwami bwe, Ab' Umwami wa bose!
Nimuzan' ikamba ry' ubwami bwe, Kw' ari we Mwami wa bose.

2.
Batowe mwe b' Israeli, Mwa nkehwe mwe nkeya,
Muramy'ubakirish' imbabazi, Ab' Umwami wa bose! 
Muramy' ubakirish' imbabazikw' ari we Mwami wa bose.

3.
Amoko yose yo hasi, N' imiryango yose, 
Bose bajye bamwubaha cyane! Ab' Umwami wa bose. 
Bose bajye bamwubaha cyane! Kw' ari wemwami wa bose.

4.
Muze tumuririmbire, Tumupfukamire, 
Tumusingize no kumushima, Ab' Umwami wa bose. 
Tumusingize no kumushima, Kw' ari we Mwami wa bose.
