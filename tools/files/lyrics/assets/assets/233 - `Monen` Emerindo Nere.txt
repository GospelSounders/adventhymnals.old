233 - 'MONEN' EMERINDO NERE
"Master The Tempest Is Raging"

1
`Monen` emerindo nere! Nig` ekogusa mono! `Mare _a igoro amwamire, Boigamo mbori ang`e; `Gokirete togosira? Ng`aki orarare! Kera etageka gekogosa, Gototindeka nyancha.

Chorus
"Mbeo n`emerindo mbinyigwe, `morembe, Neba endamwamu y`enyancha, Gos`ebirecha, gose monto onde, Mache takomera bwato as` Omonene bw`enyancha n`ense are, Ng`a bigwer` okogania kwane, `Morembe, `Morembe Ng`a bigwer` okogania kwane Omorembe".

2

3
Yes`obwoba bwasirire, `Morind` okanyekire, `Mobaso ororekanire, im` as` enkoro yane; Ganya Omoboria one, Tontiga nche bweka; Ntimoke as`ore n`omogoko, As` engegu y`ebisio.
