218 - O YESO NARIRE EIRA
"O Jesus I Have Promised"


1
Yeso narir` Eira, Kogokorera, `Kabe nainche`, Botambe, `Monen` omosani. Tinkoiroka esegi, Korabe ase nde, Tinkorw` as` enchera, `ker`orantang`ane.

2
Nkoigwe ang`e ase nde, Ense ng`ang`e ere, Tindi korora buya, "Mariogi agontema", Babisa ng`ang`e bare, Bang`etananire, O Yeso inchwo ang`e, Ntange korw`ebibe.

3
`Yeso kwarir`eir `as `abakogotunya` Agw`or` as` obonene, Mosomb`oo nabe, Yeso narir` Eira, Kogokorer` Aye, Ng`obuya `nkobwatie, `Monen` omosani.
