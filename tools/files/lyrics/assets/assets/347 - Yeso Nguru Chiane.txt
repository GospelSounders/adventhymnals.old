347 - YESO NGURU CHIANE
"Jesus My Strength"


1
Yeso nguru chiane, 
Nko` ogwenchanda kwane;
Nkwegen`as `obokanyeku,
``Gw`ogosaba kwane.

2
Ing`enkor`engiya, 
Erio nyeange nche;
Tintara chinchera chimbe, 
Na tingoki`amabe.

3
Ing`enkor`endemu,
`S`emechando yonsi;
Nyoteneni` Omosalaba,
`Nyorende bokong` u.

4
Ing`ogokoiroka, 
N`eriso eriongo;
Gokorigereria Aye,
K`ebibe bir`ange.

5
Ong`e na Omoika, 
Ore ang`e rioka;
`Y`orendet`ekero gionsi, 
Ase ogosaba.
