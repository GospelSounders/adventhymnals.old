325. Impanda Iravuze

1.
Impanda iravuze, Nimwumve, Nimwumve, 
Ivugira mw' ijuru, Yesu araje, 
Wow' uhunikira, Ngah' umva, Ngah' umva, 
Kanguka, Ubyuke, Umwam' araje.
 
2.
Ese ntimwumv' impanda, Nimwumve, Nimwumve, 
Umugen' arayumva, Yes' ari hafi, 
N' ibyishimo byinshi, Nimwumve, Nimwumve, 
Araririmb' ati: Yes' ari hafi.
 
3.
Impanda iravuze, Nimwumve, Nimwumve, 
Nimubyuke bwakeye, Umukw' araje, 
N' ibyishimo byinshi, Nimwumve, Nimwumve, 
Ngaho itegure, Dore araje.
