269 - TITORI NA OBOMENYO
"We Have No Abiding City Here"


1
Titori na obomenyo; Omochie tori aiga: Toremigw` as` eng`an` eye, Togany` omochie ogocha, Togany` omochie ogocha.

2
Titori na obomenyo; Torigi` omochie orache: Zayoni nka y`Omonene, Okomeka, miaka yonsi, Okomeka, miaka yonsi.

3
Obomenyo bw`omorembe; `Batorigw` batimoke; Onye nabwate chimbaba Nkoiruruka narenge, Ing`iruruke ntimoke.

4
Omoy`one kira igo! Omonene omanyire; Konda` nkor`obuya bwaye, Ner` ogonchorer` engaki, Ey`ogotimoka kwane.
