270 - BAGENDI NG'AI MOGOCHIA?
"Wither Pilgrim Are You Going?"


1
Bagendi ng`ai mogochia, Nechinyimbo koboko? Na togende orogendo, `As`ogochika Kwaye. Ebitunwa n`amagoko, Nkogenda tore nka bwoye, Nkogenda tore nka Bwoye, Ase ense engiya.

2
Bagendi nki morigetie, As`enseria engiya? Yanga ndabu ne chigutwa, korwa as`Omotoria. Tonyw`amache y`obogima, Tomeny` amo na Nyasae, Natomeny` amo botambe, Ase ense engiya.

3
Bagendi togende nainwe, Gochi` ens` eria engiya? Inchwo bono mwancheirwe, nchwo, Mosoe `mogand` oito. Inchwo, nchwo, timototiga, Krist` oganyir` atoire. `Ganyir` atoire Bwoye, As` ens` eria engiya.
