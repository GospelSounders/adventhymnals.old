160. Icyampa Nkab' Uwawe

1.
Icyampa nkab' uwawe, Yesu Mwami wanjye;
Ne kononwa n' iby'isi, Nkwikurikirire.

Gusubiramo:
Icyampa nkab' uwawe,
Nitanze maramaje.
Jye ntacyo maze, Ntacyo, Ntacyo; 
Niwe mpay' ikuzo.

2.
Icyampa nkab' uwawe, Yesu Mwami wanjye. 
Nkoresh' uk'ushaka, Njye nkubahiriza.

3.
Icyampa nkab' uwawe, Yesu Mwami wanjye; 
Ntandukanya n' iby'isi, Umboneze rwose.

4.
Icyampa nkab' uwawe, Yesu Mwami wanjye; 
Mb' uwaw' uko ndikose, Nta kintu kinziga.
