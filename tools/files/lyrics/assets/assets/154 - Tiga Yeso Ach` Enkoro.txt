"Let Jesus Come Into Your Heart"

Kwarosire n` egurub` endito, Tiga Yeso ach` enkoro; 
Koganeti obogima oboyia, Tiga Yeso ach` enkoro.

Chorus
Tiga chitang`utang`u, Bono takomokana;
Bono igor` enkoro, Yeso ache enkoro.

3
Kore namatemwa otanyare, Tiga Yeso ach` enkoro; 
Kore nokogani` ens` etabue, Tiga Yeso ach` enkoro.

4
Gotegeneti abasani bao, Tiga Yeso ach` enkoro; 
Morore Mosani ki arabe, Tiga Yeso ach` enkoro.

5
Koratere amo nababui, Tiga Yeso ach` enkoro; 
Korasoe as` obotimoko, Tiga Yeso ach` enkoro.

6
Ntige chitang`utang`u, Bono tinkomokana;
Naigoire enkoro, Yeso ache nkoro yane.
