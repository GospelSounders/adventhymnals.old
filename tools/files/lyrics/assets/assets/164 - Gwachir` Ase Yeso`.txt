164 - GWACHIR' ASE YESO?
"Have You Been To Jesus?

1
Gwachir` ase Yes` as`ogosibigwa?
Gwasibigwe n`amanying` aye;
Mbwegenete obuya bwaye bono?
Gwasibigw` as` amanying` aye.

Chorus
`Manyinga y`eng`ondi `Gwasibekir` as` amanyinga?
Ngobo chiao tichiri na kemoa?
Gwasibigw` as` amanying` aye?

2
Ngotar` ore botambe n`Omotoria,
Gwasibigwe n` amanying` aye?
Ngotimok` ore as` oyobambetwe,
Gwasibigw` as` amanying`aye?

3
Chingobo chiao nchirab` Ere kagocha,
chichenek` as`amanying` aye?
Mbwebeke ang`e ase obomenyo,
N`osibigw` as` amanying` aye?

4
Rusia chingobo chire na ebibe,
Osibigw` as` amanying` Aye?
Ensoko ner` egosibia enkoro,
Osibek` as` amanying` Aye.
