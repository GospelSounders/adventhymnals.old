259 - NTOUMERAN' ARIA ROCHE
"Shall We Gather At The River"


1
Ntoumeran` aria roche, `S` Amalaik` amenyete, Morind` omokenonoku, Okorw` ase Nyasae.

Chorus
Toumerane twensi roche, As` agay` oroch` oruya mono; Toumerane n`abaye bonsi, `S` oroche rwa Nyasae.

2
`S` engegu y` oroche roria, Rw`amache akomesa; Totere na gosasima, Botamb` as` omogoko.

3
Gator` ang`e goika roche, Totige chigurube; `S` obuya natotorigwe, Aru` eyanga n`egutwa.

4
Bwango ntoik` aria roche, Tokore orogendo; Chinkoro chigoke mono, Totere n`omorembe.
