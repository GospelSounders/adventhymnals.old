163. Yes' Arahamagara Ati

1.
Yes' arahamagar' ati: Ni nd' unkorera none? 
Isarurarirageze, Nind' uribusarure? 
Umv' ukw' aduhamagara; Kand'azagororera. 
Umusubiza wes' ati: Ndi hano Mwami ntuma.

2.
N' utabasha kujya kure, Ngo wambuke n' inyanja; 
N' ino har'abapagani, Wagarurira Yesu.
N' utabasha gushyanuka, Ngo wigishe nkaPaulo, 
Wakogez' iby' urukundo rw' Umukiza wabambwe.

3.
Wabura kub' umurinzi, Ku nkike z' i Sioni,
Wafashish'amashengesho, No gutur' amaturo; 
Ukayobora n' abandi, Mu nzir' ijyamw' ijuru, 
Nk' ukw' Aroni yakomeje amaboko ya Mose.

4.
Abantu bari mu kaga; Yes' aragutabaza.
Ntukagir' ubw' uvug'uti: Ntacyo nakora rwose. 
Ishimire gukoreshwa, Hitam' umurimo we;
Maz' usubize bwang' uti: Ndi hano, Mwami ntuma.
