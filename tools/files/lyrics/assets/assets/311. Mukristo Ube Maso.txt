311. Mukristo Ube Maso

1.
Mukristo ube maso, Nturuhuke na hato, 
Akaga kari hafi, Usenge!

2.
Ababisha b' Imana bajya bakubikira, 
Baguciriy' ibico, Usenge!

3.
Kenyer' intwaro zawe ku manywa na n' ijoro, 
Dor' umwanz' arakaze, Ba maso!

4.
Umv' abatsinze kera iyo ntambar' urwana, 
Barakubwira bati: Usenge!

5.
Umva Umwami wawe, Yesu wagupfiriye, 
Niwe wategets' ati: Wirinde!

6.
Bik' iryo jambo ryiza, Mu mutima wemera, 
Kandi unabikore usenga.
