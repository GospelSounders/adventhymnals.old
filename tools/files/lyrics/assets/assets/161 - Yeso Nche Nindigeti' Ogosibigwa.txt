161 - YESO NCHE NINDIGETI' OGOSIBIGWA
"Lord Jesus, I Long To Be Perfectly Whole"


1
Yeso nche nindigeti` ogosibigwa. Nindigetie ay` omenye ase nde;
Okeiyerie keria kegwekinia, Onsibie bono imbe omorabu.

Chorus
`Morabu pi, nimb` omorabu, Aye koransibie nimb` omorabu.

2
Aye Yeso ondigererie bono, Erio mbe ekeng`wanso kere moyo;
Narure bionsi as` ore, n`omoyo, Onsibie bono imbe omorabu.

3
`Monen` one inche nagosoroire, Ninkoganyete as` amagoro ao;
Onsibie bono n`amanyinga oka, Onsibie bono imbe omorabu.

4
`Monene nigo nkoganyete aye, Nyasae ontonger` omoy` omuya;
Abakogochera tori kobanga, Onsibie bono imbe omorabu.
