31. Mukiza Niwe Mucyo Waka

1.
Mukiza we! Niwe mucyo waka. 
Ntabw' umwijim' uhangar' ah'uri, 
Nyamururahw' ibinyoshya, 
Bituma nanduranya nawe.

2.
Mukiza mu gihe njya kuryama, 
Ngo nduhuk' imirimo nakoze,
Umpe kugutekereza, 
Ngo nsinzire ndi kumwe nawe.

3.
Umpagarik' umunsi n' ijoro; 
Ntabwo nabaho tutari kumwe;
Ungumane mu makuba,
Kuko ntinya gupf' utahari.

4.
Nubwo nkanguk' ungume bugufi;
Naho ndibujye gukora hose,
Kugez' ubwo nzaz' iwawe, 
Meny' urukundo rwawe rwose.
