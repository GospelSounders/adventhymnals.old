170. Mwam' Imana Yanjye

1.
Mwam' Imana yanjye, Ni wow' umbeshaho;
Ubuntu bwawe n' isokonkesh' ibyiza byose. 
Ubuntu bwawe n' isoko nkesh' amahirwe yose.

2.
Ni wowe wandemye, Nta wakundutira; 
Ineza yawe ni nyinshi,Ntacyo nayinganya; 
Ineza yawe ni nyinshi; Ntacyo nayigeraho.

3.
Mwami, Natur' iki, Ko byos' ar ' ibyawe? 
Kerets' umutim'ushima, Wo kuguhimbaza;
Kerets' umutim' nshima, Wo kukuramya, Mwami.

4.
Umpe gushikama, Kubw' ubuntu bwawe; 
Nkweguriy' ibyanjyebyose, Mbay' uwawe rwose; 
Nkweguriy' ibyanjye byose, Mb' uwaw'akaramata.
