047 - YESO OMOTORIA BETHLEHEMU
"Jesus My Saviour To Bethlehem Came"


1
Yes` Omotoria Bethlehemu, `S`obweri bw`ororo n`obosoku;
Nakumirie buna antoririe, Agacha kondigia.
Agacha kondigia, Agacha kondigia 
Nakumirie buna antoririe, Agacha kondigia.

2
Yes` Omotoria ase Kalwari, Agaakan` esir` akamboria;
Naichanire ng`aki yabete bo, Ere agankwera 
Ere agankwera, Ere agankwera
Naichanire ng`aki yabete bo, Ere akandigia.

3
Yes` Omotori` one korwa kare, `Kandigia konare gotayaya 
As` obwororo agansorora, Er` akandangeria 
Er` akandangeria, Er` akandangeria 
As` obwororo agansorora, Er` akandangeria.

4
`Motori`one nache korw` Igoro, Bun`eira yaye egoteba 
o nimorore ere kagocha, Gochicha konyoyia 
Gochicha konyoyia,  Gochicha konyoyia 
o nimorore ere kagocha, Gochicha konyoyia.
