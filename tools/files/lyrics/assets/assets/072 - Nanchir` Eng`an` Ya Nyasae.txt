072 - NANCHIR' ENG'ANA YA NYASAE
"I Love The Sacred Book"


1
Nanchir` eng`an` ya Nyasae, 
Kende tigekoribua
Rikonyoroki` obomenyo,
Rintebia ntame korw` abe.

2
`Mang`an` amay` anyorokia, 
`Monene, one ntarochi
Korw` as`ore negeire,
`Mogok` okoretwa nere.

3
Nobe ribaga Riay` aa, 
Ontebi` obwanchani Bwaye,
Ningosome n`okwegena,
Nsang` omogok` o Igoro.

4
`Me yao nao nkonyora, 
`mwando bw`Omotongi one
`S` enibo y`obong`aini ere,
Goichori` ebirengererio.

5
Karu` amasase arabi` ense,
Andae im` omosunte
Morek` enkoro emwamu, 
Tinsira korwa as`ore.
