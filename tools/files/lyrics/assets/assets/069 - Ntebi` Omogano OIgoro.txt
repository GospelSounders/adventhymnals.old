069 - NTEBI'OMOGANO OIGORO
"Tell Me The Old, Old Story"


1
Ntebi` omogano OIgoro, Otakororeka; 
OYeso n`obonene, n`obwanchani bwaye 
Ntebie na obwororo, buna as` omwana;
Nch` omworo omorosu, otakonye kende.

Chorus
Ntebi` omogan` okare, Ntebie mogan` okare
Ntebi` amang`an`a Yeso, Nobwanchani bwaye.

2
Ntebi` omogano ng`ora, Nario ndayomanye;
Koborigw` okonene, kogwenia chinkoro
Oyontebie botambe, Nigo nkweba bwango ;
Mang`an`ayio nyamanye, Nyanche ekeene.

3
Ntebererie boronge, Neriogi rigiya;
Narenge monyabibe, Yeso agantoria
Onyorokie Enjili, Gotokonya buya;
Kobek` omonto buya, Kero ki`emechando.

4
Ntebi` Enjili kegima, `Mang`an` amaronge;
Mogoko bw`ens` ong`ura, Bogima bw`enkoro
Erio` ens` enkiamokere, kondaike Igoro; 
Ntebi` Enjili kegima, Ya Yes` Omotoria.
