117. Mwami Ndumv' Ijwi Ryawe

1.
Mwami ndumv' ijwi ryawe, Rindarika ngo nze, 
Nuhagizwe yamaraso, Wamviriye kera.

Gusubiramo:
Mukiza ndaje, Ubu nj' ah' uri,
Nyejeshaya maraso, Wamviriye kera.

2.
Nubwo nje nahindanye, Ntunyirengagize; 
Ahubw' umbonezerwose, Mb' umweru waka de.

3.
Yesu ni w' unkomeza, Niw' umpa gutsinda;
Ng' urukundo n'amahoro binsabemo rwose.

4.
Mwese nimwishimire! Ya maras' akiza, 
Yatwujuje na Rurema, Ngotub' abana be.
