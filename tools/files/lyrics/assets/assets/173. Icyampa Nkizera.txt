173. Icyampa Nkizera

1.
Icyampa nkizera rwose, Ubutadohoka;
Sintinyishwe n'ubukene, Cyangw' ibyago byose.

2.
Sinivovote, mbabajwe, N' igihano cyawe; 
Ahubwo, nimbamu kaga, Nkunambeho Mwami.

3.
Mpabwe gushikama cyane, Umugaru n' ugwa; 
Neguhindishw' umushitsi, N' ibyago bitera.

4.
Ne gukangishwa n' ab' isi, N' igitsure cyabo;
N' ubwobangir' urw' amenyo, Noye kubyitaho.

5.
Mwami, Ben' uko kwizera, Niko ngutezeho;
Niringiyeko nzahabwa kwibera mw' ijuru.
