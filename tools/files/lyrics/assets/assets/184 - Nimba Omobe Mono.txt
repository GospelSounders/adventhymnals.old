184 - NIMBA OMOBE MONO
"Chif Of Sinners"

1
Nimba omobe mono, Yes`ere nankwerete;
Agakwa inkonyeke, Agakwa imbe moyo,
Buna rit` as`omote, Imbe nere botambe.

2
Obwanchani bwa Yeso, Botambe gochia igoro
Bwaetania chindiba, Boreo korwa kare,
`Bwanchani bwanyorete, `Kero nche nasirete.

3
Nimba omobe mono, Kristo mbionsi ase nde;
Omanyir` Okogania, Biane bionsi nebiaye,
Nandende korwa ense, Ongacheir` Obogima.
