255. Mbes' Aho Tuzahurira Ku Cyambu?

1.
Mbes' aho tuzahurira ku cyambu cya rwa ruzi, 
Ahahor' umucy' iteka, Ntihab' umubabaro?

Gusubiramo:
Mbes' aho tuzahurira ku cyambu cya rwa ruzi?
Tuzabonanirayo tuhanezererw' iteka?

2
Tuzahurira ku cyamb' urugendo rurangiye?
mbes' aho tuzahurirayo, Maze twiruhutse?

3
Mbes' aho twese tuzahurira mu mudugudu, 
Wubatswe n' Uhoraho, Urimbishijwe yasipi?

4
Abacunguwe bose, Bazaririmb' indirimbo, 
Kand' ibiremwa byose bizasingiz' Uhoraho. 
5
Mbes' aho tuzabonana n' abo twakundanaga, 
Maze twumv' amajwi yabo tubareba mu maso?

6
Mbese tuzabonana na Krist' Umukiza wacu, 
Aje kujyan' intore ze mu bwami buhoraho?
