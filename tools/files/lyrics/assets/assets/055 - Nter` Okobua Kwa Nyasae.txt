055 - NTER' OKOBUA KWA NYASAE
"I Sing The Mighty Power"

1
Nter` okobua kwa Nyasae, Gwatong` ebitunwa, 
Kwaara chinyancha chionsi, kwaagacha Igoro; 
Bong`aini bwaye bwachika, Risase kobara; 
Motienyi, ching`enang`eni, nkoigwera bire.

2
Nter` obuya bw`Omonene, Bwaret` endagera; 
`Kabitonga nering`ana, kabikor` ebiya,
`Monen` a`makumia ao, Aichir` ense yonsi! 
Nonya nas`ense ngotacha, N`erioba igoro!

3
`Mote tori ense aa, otagokobaka;
`Mare aichora n`omwaga, Gokwayachikire;
Bionsi bire moyo obo nay`okobirenda;
Ense teri torebise, Nyasaeotarore.
