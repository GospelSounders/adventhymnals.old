292 - NKWAROSIRE?
" Art Thou Weary?

1
Nkwarosire. gwaichanire nagochandeka? "Nchw`ateba, Eri`aye otimoke."

2
Naki ndamomany`erio nch`asar`oy`okondaa? "Nare amaot`as`ebimo biaye."

3
Ner`egutw`Omogambi abekeirw`Omotwe? "Maene negutwa, Ko neyamagwa."

4
Komonyora nagotunya nki ndanyoreo? "Obororo, `meremo namaiga."

5
Kondabwaterere nki ndanyor`Omoerio? "Bokong`u mboere ng`ete Yordani."

6
Ndamobori` ang`ire mbe oyoye nanyange? "Takwanga igoro nense nchieta."

7
Korigia, gotunya, korenda, nasesenie? "Abachenu mwensi iraneri `ee."
