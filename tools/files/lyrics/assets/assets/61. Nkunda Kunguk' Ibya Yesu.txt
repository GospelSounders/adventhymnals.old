61. Nkunda Kunguk' Ibya Yesu

1.
Nkunda kwunguk' ibya Yesu, Kwerekan' imbabazi ze;
Kwunguk'ubwenge bw' ukuri, N' urukundo rw' umukiza.

Gusubiramo:
Nunguk' ibya Yesu, Nunguk' ibya Yesu.
Kwunguk' ubwenge bw' ukuri,
N' urukundo rw' Umukiza.

2.
Nshishikarir' ibya Yesu, Mmeny iby' ashaka binkwiye. 
MwukaWera mfuturira! Mbwir' uko Yesu yankunze!

3.
Nunguk' ijambo rya Yesu, Mbane n' Umwam' antoneshe; 
Numvir'iby' ambwira byose, Mpuze na we muyoboke.

4.
Meny' iby' ingoma ya Yesu, Menye n' ubwiza bwe bwose. 
Menyenukw agiye kwima, Ni we mwami w' amahoro.
