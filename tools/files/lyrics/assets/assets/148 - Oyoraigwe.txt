148 - OYORAIGWE
"Whosoever Heareth"

1
"Oyoraigwe" aake eriogi!
`Tom` amang`an` amaya ense yonsi;
Ens` eye yonsi banyore ring`ana;
"Oyorigetie, nache!"

Chorus
"Oyorigetie, Oyorigetie"
`Bitunwa n`amagoko rirandigwe
Ntat` omwanchi korangeri` omosiru
"Oyorigetie, nache!"

2
"Onde bwensi" ogocha tatebana;
Bon` egesieri ngiasie tig` asoe
Yeso ner` Ekeene n`er`enchera; 
"Oyorigetie nache!"

3
`Yorigetie" ache, nero eira;
"Yorigetie" `remererie botambe
"Oyorigetie" Bogima bwa kare;
"Oyorigetie, nache!"
