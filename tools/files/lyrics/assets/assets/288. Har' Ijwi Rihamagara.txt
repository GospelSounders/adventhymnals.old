288. Har' Ijwi Rihamagara

1.
S. na A.:
Har' ijwi rihamagar' abarushye, Mubamurikire
Hahirw' abatabaza ngoa bakizwe, Mubamurikire
T. na B.:
Har' ijwi rihamagar' abarushye, Mubamu-rikire
Hahirw' abatabaza ngo bakizwe, Mubamu-rikire

Gusubiramo:
S.:
Nimubamurikishirize umucyo w'ubutumwa, 
Bamenyeshe ko Yes' ariwe wabapfiriye bose.
A.,T. na B.:
Nimubamu… kishirize … cyobu… . butumwa, 
Bamenyeshe ko Ye… riwe …pfi… ye bose (bose).

2.
S. na A.:
Hahirw' abatabariza gukizwa, Mubamurikire, 
Tuzan'amaturo ku musaraba, Ngo bamurikirwe.
T. na B.:
Hahirw' abatabariza gukizwa, Mubamu-rikire, 
Tuzan' amaturo ku musaraba, Ngo bamu-rikirwe.

3.
S. na A.:
Ubuntu bwa Yesu bugwire hose, Mubamurikire, 
Kand'Umwuka wa Yes' ukwire hose, Mubamurikire,
T. na B.:
Ubuntu bwa Yesu bugwire hose, Mubamu-rikire
Kand'Umwuka wa Yes' ukwire hose, Mubamu-rikire.

4.
S. na A.:
Twe gucogora gukora neza, Tubamurikire, 
Twitegurekuzahabw' ikamba, Tubamurikire.
T. na B.:
Twe gucogora gukora neza, Tubamu-rikire, 
Twitegure kuzahabw' ikamba, Tubamu-rikire.
