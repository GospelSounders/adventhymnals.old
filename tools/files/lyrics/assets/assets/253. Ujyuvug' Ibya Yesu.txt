253. Ujyuvug' Ibya Yesu

1.
ujy' uvug' ibya Yesu, Inshuti y' ukuri, 
Ngwin' uduhumurish' ibyo yagukoreye. 

Gusubiramo:
Vuga Yesu gusa, bizakuyobor' iteka, 
Iy' ubivug' uririmba, Kandi unasenga. 

2.
ujy' uvug' ibya Yes' uzumv' ubabariwe, 
No kubw' ubuntu bwe tuzagera mw' ijuru. 

3.
Sinabura kuvug' iby' umusaraba we, 
Nkund' uwo Mukiza, Kuko yanyitangiye. 

4.
Uvug' ibya Yesu, Ntupfush' igih' ubusa, 
Kutita ku nshingano, Bitesh' agaciro. 

5.
ujy' uvug' ibya Yesu, N' ubwo wacogora, 
Va mu ntege nke zawe, Umwiyegurire.
