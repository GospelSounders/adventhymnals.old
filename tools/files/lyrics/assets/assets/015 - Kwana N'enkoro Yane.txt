015 - KWANA N'ENKORO YANE
"Speak to My Soul"

1
Kwana nenkoro yane,
Ontebi` amangana Nyiwe enkoro ime,
"Tori aye bweka"; Igor` enkoro yane,
Nyigwe riogi riao; Erio ngotogie aye, Nchenge ime yao.

Chorus
Omonyerere ngora
N`obwanchani bwao;
Mbe omobui bori otari n`ebibe;
Kwana nainch` Omonene
N`Obwanchani bwao;
Omonyerere ng`ora "Tori aye bweka".

2
Kwana n`abana bao, Enchera enchenu;
Baichorie Omogoko, Barende banabe,
Baru` Obogim` as`ore bonda bwao boche;
Omonene oito nchuo,karansa na intwe.

3
Okwane buna kare gwakwanete ense;
Nindigetie nkomanye, Erio ntar` as`ore; 
Nyorokia ngosik` aye nkobake botambe;
Mbwatie ayorigetie,
`Matuko nde moyo.
