107 - OMONYAMWANDO NAKI OGOSUNDOKA?
        "Heir Of The Kingdom"


1
Omonyamwando nakiogosundoka?
Naki_okorara kogwaikire?
Imoka bweboyi` ebinto bi`esegi;
Matuko nkoer` ar` ayerera.

2
Omonyamwando naki ogotebana?
Naki ogotiga eng`eria yao Eboyie
Omotoria ngocha are;
`Morototer` amare igoro.

3
`Bisaku ebinene nkorwana bire; 
Bianyegerigwe na gokw` omoyo 
Tegerera neriogi rie chimbunde;
Omonyamwando tagotebana.

4
Tagoteban` as` omong`uso bw` ense! 
Buya bw` ense nigo bogosira 
Butora chingatare chia omobe;
Omonyamwando onchoka tama.

5
China eris` origererie igoro; 
`Bonene bw`Omogambi kagocha;
Ngokiamoka bore korw` ebitunwa; 
`Banyamwando chenga na gotera.
