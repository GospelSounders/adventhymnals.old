273. Yesu Ntabwo Yakwanga

1.
Yesu ntabwo yakwanga, n' ubw' ur' umunyabyaha, 
Niwe nshuti ihebuje, Ntazanga kukwakira, 
Imana yatanze Yesu, Kugirango ducungurwe, 
N' ubwo wamuhemukiye, Ntazanga kukwakira.
 
2.
N' ubw' ujy' umugayisha, Ntazakwirengagiza, 
Niwe nzira n' umucyo, Ntazanga kukwakira, 
Azagusendahw' ibyaha, maz' usigar' utunganye, 
Azagukiz' urupfu, Ntabw' azakwirengagiza.

3.
Ngaho ngwin' utiganda, Ntazanga kukwakira, 
Gana ku musaraba, Ntazanga kukwakira, 
Numara gusanga Yesu, Ntuzasubira mu byaha, 
Ngwino uhabw' ubugingo, Butagir' iherezo.
