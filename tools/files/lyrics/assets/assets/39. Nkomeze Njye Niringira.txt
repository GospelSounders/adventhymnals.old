39. Nkomeze Njye Niringira

1.
Nkomeze njye niringira, Amaraso y' Umukiza 
Sinagir' undi nigana, Keretse Yesu wenyine.

Gusubiramo:
Nikomeze ku Rutare,
N' ahw' ahandi n'umusenyi;
N' ahw' ahandi n' umusenyi.

2.
Umwijima n' umpumisha, Niringir' imbabazi ze.
Mu makuba no mu byago, Ntagir' ubw' anzibukira.

3.
Mbonezwa n' amaraso ye, Ampa kumukurikira.
Niyo nababaye rwose, Aramfasha ngakomera.

4.
N' aza kw' ijwi ry' ikondera, Azansanga mwiteguye;
Nambaye gukiranuka, Nta nenge nzaba ngifite.
