302 - ER'OYORAREMERI 'EMECHANDO
"He Who Would Valiant Be"


1
Er`oyoraremereri`  emechando, Agenderere  gotuny` Omotoria; Kegocha  tikeri  keramoonchore,   `Tig`eira yaye kob`Omotari.

2
Abakomorosia  nayar`amake, Bagwetutukania  `R`anyora  `nguru; Takobugwa  rinde,  Narwanigwa  naki,   Nekony` ekiagera Ar` Omotari. 

3
Ay`otorwanerera  n`Omoika,  Twamanyire  omwando  N`obogima; Obobe mbotame, Tinkwobo` ay` abanto, Botuko na mobaso mb`omotari.
