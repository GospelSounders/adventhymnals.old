230 - K'EMECHANDO YENSE EGOCHA
"He Will Hide Me"


1
K` Emechando y`ens` egocha, 
`Segi nyancha na ense; 
Nindigi as` obotamero, 
`Se kiriri kia Nyasae.

Chorus
Nambise nche, Nambise nche, 
Ase ntanyor` emechando,
Nambise nche, Nambise nche, 
`Se kiriri kia Nyasae.

2
Karandeter` emechando, 
Nisemerie Igoro; 
`S` obwanchani, nandongeyie, 
Takworoki` endamwamu.

3
Ababisa bakondigia, 
Saitani_ondigetie; 
Yeso aonchor` amabe, 
Kob` omogok` omonge.

4
Kobogori` omosalaba, 
Ndor` emechando y` ense; 
Yes` orendet` Omoy` one, 
Tagontiga nche nsire.
