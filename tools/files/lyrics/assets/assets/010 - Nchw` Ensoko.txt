010 - NCHW` ENSOKO
"334 Come Thou Fount"


1
Nchw` Ensoko ya Ker`egesio, Ninter`obuya bwao, 
Maber`amang`ataere, Agere tokobake;
Nyegerie kogosasima, Nyorok`obuya bwao,
`Gosemeria Kw`Obonene, Nkor`eichorw`obwanchani.

2
Nateneniri `Ebenesa*, kwandendire goik`aa, 
Nasemeirie as`obuya bwao, ning`ike inka 
Yes`akandigi`omosiru, Konareng`are nere,
Gonkonya korw`emechando, Gaiter`amanying`aye.

3
Botambe nd`omonyasira, `s`obuya bwa Nyasae, 
`Buya buna chingatare, bombwaterani` as`ore;
Mbetereirie ntayaye, ntige Nyasae nyanchete,
Ir`enkoro yane tata, yegach`agwo igoro.
