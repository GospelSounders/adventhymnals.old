262. Nyir' Impuhwe Nyinshi

1.
Nyir' impuhwe ninshi cyane, Iman' ibabarira, 
Kwibagirw' ibyaha ikababarir' uwagomye.
 
Gusubiramo:
S na A:
Ndaje Yesu, Ndakwitabye, 
Ukondi kos' unyemere, 
Ndaje Yesu, Ndakwitabye, 
Mukiz' unyakire.
T na B:
Ndaje Yesu, Ndakwitabye, 
Ukondi ko. . unyemere, 
Ndaje Yesu, Ndakwitabye, 
Mukiz' unyakire.

2.
Nari kure y' uwo nkunda, Mbabaj' umutima, 
Kukonamwibagihw' aba, N' uwanjye yamfiriye.
 
3.
Yesu ndakwiyeguriye, Ndashaka kubaturwa, 
Icyahacyose cyambase, Maze ngikire rwose. 

4.
Haleluya yanduhuye, Ubu sinshidikanya, 
Ndekerez' iyomw' ijuru, Nejejwe no kubayo.
