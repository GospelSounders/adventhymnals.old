032 - MBAE N'OBOSERA BWA YESO
"Let THe Beauty Of Jesus"


1
Mbe n`obosera bwa Yeso ime yane,
`Mabera n`obochenu bwaye bwonsi;
Omoik` Omochenu Anchene kegima,
Goika Yes`arorekane ime yane.

2
Mbe nobosera bwa Yeso ime yane,
`Buya bwaye bororekan` asende;
Tig`amang`an` ane Nebikorwa biane,
Bioroki` obosera bwaye Yeso.

3
`Ker`orogendo rwane rwaerire,
Ntare na Yeso botamb` as`obuya;
Nkoimokigwa igoro `S`enka y`omorembe, 
Neb` omogok`one komenya Nere.
