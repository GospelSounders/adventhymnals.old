326 - MBWAT' OMORISIA
"I Have A Shepherd"


1
Mbwat` Omorisia Oyonyanchete; Ansesenia nche Ntanyare kwera; `S`Omosalab` agachandek` agakwa; Erio menye as`obwanchani bwaye.

Chorus
Gotunya Yeso kera rituko; Kende nkegonsaria kare nainche; `Mosunte gose omobaso; Yes`omorisi` one nere bionsi.

2
`Borisi` obonge aru` ase tore; Mache aeta ng`ora ase` nde, `Buya n`amabera bintunya nchera; Tinkoremerwa kare nainche.

3
Kondatayaye nsire enchera; Ere nang`use` ng`iranie nchera, Nonya nobokong`u Nche tinkwoboa; Morisi` Omuya Nandende rioka.

4
Merem` eere Rogendo boigo; Ere nandae gochia Bwone nka, Agw`ase ndamenye `Mogoko bori; Nabanchwa ase Amagor` Aye.
