331. Mwuka Wera

1.
Aha hantu har' amahoro, Kandi hari n' Umwaka Wera w'Uhoraho, 
Kandi n' Umuntu wese uhari, Afit' umunezero mwinshibihebuje. 
Gusubiramo:
Mwuka Wera We, We numa nziza, 
Ubane natwe, Utwuzuz' urukundo, 
Turaguhimbaza kuk'uduhira, 
Turataha tudashidikanya yuko duhiriwe twese.
