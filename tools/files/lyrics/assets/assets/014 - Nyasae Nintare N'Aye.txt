014 - NYASAE NINTARE N'AYE
"O Let Me Walk With Thee"

1
Nyasae nintare Naye,
Bun` Enoku atarete;
Mbwate okoboko kwane,
Inkwane n`Aye boronge;
Nintaror` enchera buya;
Yeso, ntare amo Naye.

2
Tingotara inche bweka,
Mwaga nomotindi bori;
Amatemwa abuchire,
Ababisa nabange bo;
Kiria emerindo nyancha,
Omonene! ntare Naye.

3
Korambwate okoboko,
Ense tekombera kende;
Nintare nde omoremu,
Goika ng`ik` Omoerio;
Ndore omochie Zayoni,
Omotoria, ntare Naye.
