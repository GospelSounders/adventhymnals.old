105. Ashobora Gukiza

1.
Ashobora gukiza! Mubyamamaze hose;
Ashobora gukiz' umwumvirawese, 
Akamwarura mu murir' ugurumana. 
Yes' ashobora gukiza ushakawese.
Gusubiramo:
Ashobora kugukiza, No kukurokor' ubu;
Mwene Data n' umwizera, Azagukiza.

2.
Ashobora gukiza! Mbese n' ukuri koko?
Ahw' afit' imbarag'ibasha kunkiza? 
Mbwira, Nyamuna mbwira, Mbese Kristo wishwe,
yashobora kumbohora, Akandokora?

3.
Ashobora gukiza! Mugenzi we komera! 
N' uyoboka Yesuazakurokora. 
Ntugatinye Satani, Jy' ugundira Yesu;
Reka kujy'ushidikanya, Jy' umwiringira.
