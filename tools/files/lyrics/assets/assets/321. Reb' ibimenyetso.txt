321. Reb' ibimenyetso

1.
Reb' ibimenyetso by' urugendo, Reb' ibimenyetso birashira, 
By' imyak' ishize by' ubwami bune, Twe turi hehe, Reb' urugendo.
 
Gusubiramo:
Reb' ibimenyetso by' ubuhanuzi, 
By' ibihe byashize by' ubwami bune, 
Reb' ibimenyetso by' ubuhanuzi, 
Byose byarangiye. 

2.
Abasiriya bategets' isi, Haz' Abamedi n' Abaperesi, 
Abagiriki batway' iyi si, Haz' Abaroma, Twe turi hehe?

3.
Ko turi mu bihe by' intege nke, Iby' ibumba n' icyuma byavanzwe, 
Nuko tuzareb' uko bizaba Yes' agarutse, kwim' ingoma ye.
