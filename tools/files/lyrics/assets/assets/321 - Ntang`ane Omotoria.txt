321 - NTANG'ANE OMOTORIA
"Jesus, Saviour, Pilot Me"


1
Ntang`ane Omotoria. `Merindo y` obogima; Neruma bosio bwane, `Bitare nabintuba, Bimbise Korw`as`Ore, Ntang`ane Omotoria.

2
Nokirie emerindo, Buna ng`ina Omwana `Merindo nekoigwere. Korayetebie `Kira, `Omogambi bw` enyancha, Ntang `ane Omotoria.

3
Ngoika ang`e n`engegu, `s`emerind` ekoruma; sende n`omotimoko, Ko nyeegeget` as`ore, Ninkoigwe Kogontebia. "Toiroka ninkorae."
