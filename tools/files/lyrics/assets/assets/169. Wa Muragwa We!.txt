169. Wa Muragwa We!

1.
Wa muragwa we, mbese k' uhunikira? 
K' ucogoy' ugiyegusohora?
Kanguka bwangu, shak' intwaro zawe;
Wizarira, dorebyatinze rwose!

2.
Amahanga yose yasubiranyemo; 
Bose baradagadwa bihebye.
Umv'ukw' amagar' ahindira hose;
Wa muragwa we, wigumya gutinda!

3.
Ntugwabizwe n' iby' iyi si biguhenda; 
Reb' ubwiza bwabyoburashira.
C' ingoy' Umwanzi yakubohesheje;
Wa muragwa we, zicikehobwangu!

4.
Nyamuneka, ntugir' imitim' ibiri; 
Ba mas' Umwam'atagutungura; 
Dor' umucy' utangiye gutangaza;
Muragwa we, vuz'impundu, wishime!
