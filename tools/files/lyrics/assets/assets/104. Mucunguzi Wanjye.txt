104. Mucunguzi Wanjye

1.
Mucunguzi wanjye, Wumv' uko ntaka; Ngwin' unyirokorere. 
Umutima wanjy' ugoswe n' ibyago; Ngwin' unyirokorere.

Gusubiramo:
Nahabiye kure, Nd' akahebwe;
Nahabiye kure y' iwacu. 
Nyakira none, Maz' ungez' iwacu; 
Ngwin' unyirokorere.

2.
Mwami, Singira shinge na rugero; Ngwin' unyirokorere.
Kwitabwaho nawe nibyo byanyura; Ngwin' unyirokorere.

3.
Dore ndi jyenyine, Nabay' impabe; Ngwin' unyirokorere. 
Nubuy' amaso ngo mbeshweho nawe; Ngwin' unyirokorere.

4.
Ntuhinyur' umutim' ushenjaguwe; Ngwin' unyirokorere. 
Nyamunek' ita ku gusenga, Kwanjye, Ngwin' unyirokorere.
