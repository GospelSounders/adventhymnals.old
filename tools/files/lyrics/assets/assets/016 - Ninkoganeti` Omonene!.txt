016 - NINKOGANETI' OMONENE!
"Blesses Lord, How Much I Need Thee"

1
Ninkoganeti` Omonene! Igo inde n`ebibe;
Mbwate okoboko ndae, Erio nyare korora.

Chorus
Botambe, botambe,
Ninkoganeti` Omonene!
Botambe, botambe,
Omotoria ondende.

2
Mbekere oboitongo, Ebibe biambuire;
Nyoroki` oboreu bwane, Ing`a chinguru chiao.

3
Tinkoiroka as`ore, A ye nondae buya;
Tara na inch` Omotoria, Nay` obogima bwane.

4
Kende gionsi nagechicha, `Mogoko n`obororo;
Omoyo nobe as`ore, Naye orantimokie.
