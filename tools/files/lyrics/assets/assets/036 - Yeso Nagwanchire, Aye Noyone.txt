036 - YESO NAGWANCHIRE AYE NOYONE
"My Jesus I Love Thee"


1
Yeso nagwanchire, Aye noyone;
Natigir` ebibe nchiche as`ore,
`Moboria one, naye ogontoria;
Onye nagwanchete, Rirorio mbono.

2
Nagwanchire buna Aye kwanyanchete;
`Kabogori` ebibe aria kalwari,
Ngwanch`as`okobek`engatay`amagwa;
Onye nagwanchete rirorio mbono.

3
Ngwanch`as`obogima, n`as`amakweri;
Naende ngotogie, buna nde moyo,
Nintebe komberete ang`e gokwa;
Onye nagwanchete, Rirorio mbono.

4
`Bomenyo bw`omogok` otakoera;
Ngosasime botambe agwaoigoro,
Nintere n`egutw` ekomekameka;
Onye nagwanchete, Riririo mbono.
