166. Ayi Rutare Rw' Imana

1.
Ayi, Rutare rw' Imana, Reka nguhungireho! 
Kubw'imbabazi zawe, Kera warammenewe, 
Non' ubu nkwihishemo,Umujinya w' Imana.

2.
Ibyo twakora byose, N' umwete wacu wose, 
N' agahindagasaze, N' amarie adashira, 
Byose ntibyashobora, Kudukurahw'ibyaha.

3.
Yes' byo mfite byose, Nta mumaro bigira. 
Sinabashakwikiza, Ndagushakah' ubuntu, 
Unyuhagir' imbyiro, Mutabazi negupfa.

4.
Nuko nkiri muzima, Mwami, Nguhungireho.
No ku munsiw' imanza, Ninger' imbere yawe, 
Ayi Rutare rw' Imana,Uzandeke nkwisunge!
