41. Yes' Abe Hamwe Nawe Hose

1.
Yes' abe hamwe nawe hose! Akugir' inama nziza! 
Agucyure mubushyo bwe! Yes' abe hamwe nawe hose!

Gusubiramo:
Kugez' ubwo (ubwo) tuzabonana,
Ubwotuzabana na Yesu (na Yesu).
Kugez' ubwo (ubwo) tuzabonana,
Yes'abe hamwe nawe hose.

2.
Yes' abe hamwe nawe hose, Akurinde mu makuba! 
Akwongere man'iteka! Yes' abe hamwe nawe hose!

3.
Yes' abe hamwe nawe hose! Ubuyerezwa n' ibyaha, 
Agukingir'ukuboko! Yes' abe hamwe nawe hose!

4.
Yes' abe hamwe nawe hose! Jy' uguman' urukundo rwe! 
Tsind'urupf' imbere yawe! Yes' abe hamwe nawe hose!
