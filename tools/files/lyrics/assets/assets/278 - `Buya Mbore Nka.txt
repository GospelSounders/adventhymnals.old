278 - 'BUYA MBORE NKA"
THer Is Beauty All Around"


1
`Buya mbore nka k`obwachani boreo, `Mogoko` oigweke k`obwanchani bore nka; `Morembe n`omoigoto, N`omogoko bibeo, Ng`akiere buya k`obwanchani bore nka.

Chorus
`Bwanchani, bore nka, Ng`aki_ere buya k`obwanchani bore nka.

2
`Mogoko nyomba k`obwanchani boreo, Moko tegocha, k`obwanchani bore nka; Bisicha biam`as`ense, s`emegond` emiya, Bogima bwagokigwa, `Bwanchani nyomba.

3
Igor`egoka k`obwanchani bore nka, Ens`eichorw`obwanchani ko bore nyomba; Agansoko gatera, Igoro ner`eraba, k`obwanchani bore nka, Yes` agokigwa.

4
Yeso nkore mb`oyoo bwanchani boche, Nsang` ekeng`wanso kiao, `Rio twanchane nka; Ntimoke korw`obobe, Tindengereri `ebibe, `S`obuya nseseneke, Bwanchani nyomba.
