86. Muramy' Umwami

1.
Muramy' Umwami waremye byose, 
Nimuhimbaze mwes' urukundorwe, 
N' umuvunyi wacu niw' Uhoraho, 
Agoswe n' ibyiza birabagirana.

2.
Mumwamamaze! Nyir' imbabazi! 
Yihunz' umucyo, Akwiriye hose,
Ibicu bikoranywa, N' icyusa cye,
Acyahish' abant' urusaku rw' inkuba.

3.
Agir' ubuntu bukwira hose,
Bwuj' ikirere, Buba no mu mucyo,
Butemba mu misozi n' ibisiza, 
Nibw' ikime n' imvura biturukamo.

4
Tur' igitaka, Tworoshye twese. 
Turakwizera kuk' udahemuka;
Imbabazi zawe n' akamenyero. 
Mubyey' ugir' ibambe, Mukiza mwiza.
