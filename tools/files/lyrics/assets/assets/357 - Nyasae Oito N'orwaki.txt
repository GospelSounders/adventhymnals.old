357 - NYASAE OITO N'ORWAKI
"A Safe Stronghold Our God Is Still"


1
Nyasae oito norwaki, Nguba nekerwanero,
Natokonye korw` obobe obwo bwatobuire;
Omogambi bw` obobe Oimokereirie igoro,
Bong`ainereri` obonene `bekire `kero eke;
As` ens` etari mosani.

2
Ntokonyara kwerwanera, Bwango nigo toraitwe,
`Monto nar` ogotokonya, Nyasae bwek` omorure;
Mwaboria ning`o `yo? n`kristo Yes` akorokwa;
Omwan` o Nyasae; Bweka, onde tari, Orabue `Se `segi.

3
Nebirecha bikaer` enseye, `Ganyete gotosiria,
Titwayabeka `mayo ime, Titogotobua `ntwe;
`Mogambi `gosania. Naba bun` arabe,
Takonyara nonya, Nakio erikeire; "Ring`ana" narimoite.

4
Biblia se chinguru chiabo, Tageka tekoera,
`Nki`nkeba goikebue, Nyasae arigete bweka;
Nachitour` obogima, Nibo, bana, Nyomba,
Ng`eria yabo nenke; Bionsi nabisire o Nyasae otigare.
