302. Umunsi W' Umuzuko

1.
Umunsi w' umuzuko, Umuns' utangaje, 
Hazab' umunezero, Tuzabon' Imana, 
Abera bazakangurwa, Bazamurwe mu bicu, 
Baririmb' indirimbo, Nziza yo kunesha. 

2
Tuzezwa imitima, Tuzabona Yesu, 
Tuzabana n' Imana mu mucy' utazima, 
Tuzahora duhimbaza, Dushim' Imana yacu, 
Yo yatuneshereje, Iduh' agakiza.
 
3.
Wa juru we ishime, Wa si we ririmba, 
Abanambye ku Mana, Baranesherejwe, 
Ibindi biremwa nabyo, Byishime binezerwe, 
Umwami wacu Yesu, Nasingizw' iteka.
