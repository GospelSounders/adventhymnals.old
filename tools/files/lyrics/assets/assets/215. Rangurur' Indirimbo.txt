215. Rangurur' Indirimbo

1
Rangurur' indirimbo y' ibyishimo n' umunezero, 
Wikwiheba shir' agahinda, Ririmban' ibyishimo. 
Ririmbir' Uwaturinze, Waduhuje n' inshuti, 
Uwatuyoboy' inzira, Itugeza mw' ijuru. 
Gusubiramo:
Ririmba (ririmba), Ririmba (ririmba), 
Ririmban' ibyishimo, 
Ririmba (ririmba), Ririmba (ririmba), 
N' ibyishimo byinshi. 
2
Turirimbe dutway' amabendera tunezerewe, 
Turirimba hoziana z' umunezero w' iteka. 
Tuvug' ibyabaye kera, By' urukundo rw' Imana. 
3
Turagushima Data Wera, Kubw' imbabazi zawe, 
Uko duteraniye hamwe, Turagushima cyane. 
Turagushima Mukiza, Kuko waducunguye, 
Uduhe Umwuka Wera, Atwegerez' Imana.
