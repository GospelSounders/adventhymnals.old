140. Numvise Yesu Ambwira Ati

1.
Numvise Yes' ambwir' ati: Ngwino nkuruhure.
Zan'ibikurushya byose, Mbikwikorerere. 
Musanga, mez' uko nari ndi,ndushye, mfite n' ishavu; 
Amber' uburuhukiro, Maz' aranezeza.

2.
Numvise Yes' ambwir' ati: Ndaguher' ubuntu,
Amaz'azakubeshaho, Unyw' uram' iteka.
Nuko nsanga Yesu maz' ampa ya maziy' ubugingo,
Nshir' inyota, Ndahembuka; Nzahorana nawe.

3.
Numvise Yes' ambwir' ati: Ni jye Mucyo w' isi.
N' undeb'uramurikirwa, Ntuzarindagira.
Maze muhanz' amaso koko, Mbon' ar'izuba ryanjye; 
Rizamvir' iminsi yose y' ubugingo bwanjye.
