270. Nyobora Mwami Wanjye

1.
Nyobora Mwami wanjye (Mwami wanjye), 
Ne kurorongotana (mbane nawe), 
Mb' amahor' unyoboye (unyoboye), 
Iyo nkuri bugufi (nkuri bugufi)

Gusubiramo:
Mukiza we, Mpabura nahabiye kure, 
Unyambutse mw' isumo (mw' isumo), 
Mwami ngendane nawe (nawe).
 
2.
Iyo ngeze mu kaga (ncits' intege), 
umber' ubuhungiro (buhungiro), 
Nyakiran' imbabazi (imbabazi), 
Nezerwa turi kumwe (turi kumwe), 

3.
Ntabara Mwami mwiza (Mwami mwiza), 
Mu mibabaro yanjye (yanjye yose), 
Kugez' ubwo nzager' aho (nzager' aho), 
Ntazarir' ukundi (ntazarir' ukundi).
