227 - YESO NAKIRE?
"Does Jesus Care?"

1
Yeso nakir` as` obororo, Ntagoke gose gotera? `Gurub`endito birengererio, Orogendo rokondosia.

Chorus
Nkondend` ar`amaene_igo Oigure emechando, Mobaso ndosa Botuko ng`iroka Omotoria ndenda.

2
Yeso nakir` as`omosunte, Konde nobwoba obonge; Omobas`ore botuko bocha, Ere nkomber`are ang`e.

3
Yeso nakir` as`amatemwa, Ekero nche nasinyirwe; Boror` obonge ntatimogeti, Marig` akonagoiteka.

4
Yeso nakire kamakweri, Agwatanana abanchwa; Omoy`oichana ang`e n`ogokwa, Tagokira atandore.
