252. Nimucane Amatabaza

1.
Nimucan' amatabaza, Umwijim' ubay' icyago, 
Hazab' umwijim' ukaze, Umwam' ataragaruka.
 
Gusubiramo:
mubonez' amatabaza, 
Kandi nimwubah' Imana, 
Kuza kw' Umwami kuri hafi, 
Mubonez' amatabaza. 

2.
Abant' ibihumbi byinshi, Bahinyur' ubu butumwa, 
Natwe tubushikamyeho, Dutegerez' Umukiza.
 
3.
Ijambo rye n' itabaza, Ntituzigera tuyoba, 
N' ubw' amakub' ari hose, Ntabw' Iman' izaduhana. 

4.
Dukor' imirimo myiza, Twe guh' ab' iy' is' urwaho, 
Tuzabiherw' imigisha, Nidukomeza kwizera.
