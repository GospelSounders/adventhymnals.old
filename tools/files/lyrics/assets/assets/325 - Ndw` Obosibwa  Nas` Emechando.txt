325 - NDW' OBOSIBWA NAS' EMECHANDO
"Jesus I Come"


1
Ndw` obosibwa  nas` emechando  Nche  nachire Yer` as` ore, S` obosibore  nomogoko  Yeso  nagocheire; Ndw` as` Oborwaire `Rio  ngwenigwe  Ndw` obotaka `Rio  ntenetakigwe Ntig`ebibe Nchiche  as` ore, Yeso nagocheire. 

2
Ndw`as`obosoku  bw`ogosinywa  Nche  nachire Yes`as`ore, Nyor`Obuya  bw`omosalaba,  Yeso nagocheire; Ndw`as`obororo  ombogorie,
Ntimoke nemorindo  yense, Ndw`as`obokong`u  ntere  ngoke, Yeso nagocheire. 

3
Ndw` as` obotindi  Nogwekaga  Nche  nachire Yes` as` ore, Erio  nkor` okogania  Kwao,  Yeso nagocheire; Nyetige  meny` as` obwanchani, Nyimoke ntig` ogokw` omoyo, Nyiruruke  buna  riruma  Yeso nagocheire.

4
Ndwe  as`obwoba  bw`amakweri,  Nche  nachire Yes` asore, `S`omogok`  oborabu  bwa  nka  Yeso nagocheire; Ntam`obosareku  ntatebe  Nyor` omoremb` ase  riicho, Ndor` obosio  bwa` obosera  Yeso  nagocheire.
