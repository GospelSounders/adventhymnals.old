330 - BORABU NDAE
"Lead, Kindly Light"

1
Borabu ndae as` omoichano, Ndae rioka, Obotuko, ng`arende korwa nka, Ndae rioka, Ndend` amagoro; Timboreti korora egeka, Kogoro okomo kong`isane.

2
Tinanarete iga, gosaba ng`a, Ay`ondae Nigo nanchete gochor` ayane, Bono ndae; Ngancha omogoko ko inde omwoba, Nkaenena, Toinyor` ayaetire.

3
Chinguru chiao chiansesenirie, Nchindae rioka As`amanani nas` emerindo, Goika bokie Kobwakire, `Malaika bagoka, `Banyanchete ko bansireirie ake.
