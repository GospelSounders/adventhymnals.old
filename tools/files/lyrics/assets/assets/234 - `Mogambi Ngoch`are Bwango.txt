234 - MOGAMBI NGOCH'ARE BWANGO
"The Coming King"

1
`Mogambi ngoch`are bwango,
`Ri`okweret` abanyabibe,
Nache kwoyia abachenu, Abaire Igoro.

Chorus
Ngoch` Are, ngoch` Are,
Ogocha kwaye ng`ang`e kore;
O! Nachiche, O! Nachiche,
Nachiche gototoria.

2
Twaror` ebimanyererio,
Bikworoki` ogocha kwaye;
Matuko bwango naere,
Natogende _Igoro.

3
Amatuk` onsi nesegi,
Titori n` ogotimoka;
Goik` Omotoria asirie,
`Bibe n`amakweri.

4
Ens` eyio ya_Igoro nenyia,
Toraikaranse botambe;
Toboyigwe Ogotakwa,
Tomenye botambe.
