98. Ndagushaka Mukiza We

1.
Ndagushaka Mukiza we! Ntabwo mbasha kwigenza.
Ndifuza k'umpora hafi, Gumy' unyikomereze.

Gusubiramo:
Sinzagir' icyo ntinya, 
Umbwiriz' iby'ushaka. 
Ntacyambuza kuz' ah' uri, 
Ngukurikir' ah' ujya.

2.
Ndagushaka Mukiza we! Kuko noroshye cyane. 
Niw' umbwir'ibinkomeza, Nta wundi mbitezeho.

3.
Ndagushaka Mukiza we! Aho ndi mur' iy' isi. 
Mu muvumbi no kuzuba, Mu bimbabaza byose.

4.
Ndagushaka Mukiza we! Mbwiriz' ibitunganye. 
Ungeze kuri Yor'dani,Umpe no kuyambuka.
