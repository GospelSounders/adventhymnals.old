36. Ni Wowe Nizeye

1.
Ni wowe nizeye, Ntama y' i Kalwari, 
We mukiza! Ngusab'unyumvire,
Nkiz' ibicumuro, Mperey' ubu Mwami,
Nkuyoboke.

2.
Nyuzuz' imbabazi, Ngir' inama nziza,
Nter' umwete; Kukowamfiriye, 
Nkwerek' urukundo, Rudatuba rwaka,
Nk' umuriro.

3.
Ni ngotwa n' ibyaha, Nkagira n' ishavu, 
Unyobore. Nkura mumwijima,
Nsembahw' amaganya, Kandi ntundekere,
Kure yawe.
