112(2) - 'MONENE NGOIKA RI?
"How Long, O Lord Our Saviour"

1
`Monene ngoika ri, Orateban` Aye?
Emioyo yarosire Koganya ochiche.
`Kero ekio orache, Nakebare mono,
`Borabu bwao mbobar` as` abanto bao?

2
`Motoria ngoika ri, Oratig` abao?
Gwatebanire mono, `Mbake baganyete.
Abakori emeremo, Borobire mono,
Abake bagangayete, `S` ogokorotota.

3
Tura abanto bao, `S` okobachanchima,
Abachenu batebe, "Motoria ng`ang`are." 
Chitaya chiaito nchioke, Tweboyie
bokong`u Tokoganye kegima, Tokoror`
obosio.
