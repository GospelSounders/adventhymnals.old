225. Narebye Yesu

1.
Narebye Yes' igihe nari ndembejwe n' ibyaha, 
Narinabuz' uko ngira, Maz' arampumuriza.

Gusubiramo:
S.:
Ye, ni koko, Nareby' Umwami Yesu; 
Arambona, Nzabana naw' iteka.
A.,T. na B.:
Ye, ni koko, Nanjye nareby' 
Umwami Yesu, Maz' arambona, 
Nanjye rwose, Nzabana naw'iteka.

2.
Nabony' Umwami Ye' ambambiwe ku musaraba, 
Nizerayukw' ibyaha byanjye bibambanywe nawe. 

3.
Nabony' Umwami Yesu, Azukany' icyubahiro, 
Niringir'imbabazi ze, N' ibyahanuwe kera. 

4.
Yandebany' impuhwe, Umutima wanjy' uranyurwa, 
Maz'anyiher' ikimenyetso cy' Umwuka We Wera. 

5.
Tuzabana n' Umwami Yesu Krist' ibihe byose, 
Mutezehobyose, Mbe nk' umwana wiringira se.
