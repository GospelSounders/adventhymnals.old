083 - NIMBWATE OMOSANI
"I Have A Friend So Precious"


1
Nimbwate Omosani, Nimwanchete mono; 
Nigo anyanchete mono, Anchete kegima; 
Tinkoba moyo ataiyo, Mbuya kare ang`e,
Erio twamenya amo: Ere nainche.

2
Kero kende narosa, Ere namanyete;
Kagontebia mweegeke, Nanyor` ogokonywa;
Nere okondaa nchera, Tinyara kogochwa;
Erio twatara amo, Ere nainche.

3
Nigo nkomworoki` onsi; Omogoko one; 
Naende obororo bo, Nony` Omoichano; 
Antebia nkore amaya, Buna nche ndateme. 
Erio twakwana amo, Ere nainche.

4
Namanyet` okogania, Morete oyomo;
Onchikire ntebie
`bande, Ring`ana rigiya,
Ntebe obwanchani bwaye, `Kiageret` agakwa.
Twakora emeremo, Ere nainche.
