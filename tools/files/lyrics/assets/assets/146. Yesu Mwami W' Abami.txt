146. Yesu Mwami W' Abami

1.
Yes' Umwami w' abami, Ngwin' ube muri jye! 
He kub' ari jy'uvuga, Ujy' umvugiramo. 
Unsabe mu mutima, Ngengwe nawe rwose, 
Maz'abajya bandeba, Bajye bakubona.

2.
Ndakwiyeguriy' ubu, Ez' ibyaha byanjye; 
Ngaragareh' ubwizabwawe buhebuje. 
Ngir' umutim' utuje, Ne guteraganwa; 
Niyoroshyenganduke, Ngo nkoreshwe nawe.

3.
Ingingo zanjye zose, Zijye zikumvira; 
Uzikoreshereze,Zikubahirize. 
Ne kujya nivovota, Ngo mpore ku nkeke. 
Y' ibimbaho,nishama, Nta mahoro mfite.

4.
Ni wowe ntegereje, Ungenz' uk' ushaka. 
Ubwanjye nditeguye,Simfit' ikinziga. 
Yes' Umwami w' abami, Ngwin' ube muri jye! 
Maz'ibyo nkora byose, Mbikoreshwe nawe.
