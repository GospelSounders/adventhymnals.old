"Away In A Manger"

Ari` as obweri borere mbwareo, 
Omwana oyo Yeso nao araire,
Ching`enang`eni chikainomoreka, 
Ase Yes`arareret` obonyansi.

2
Chiombe chikwana chiatura Omwana, 
Ko Omwana Yeso tari korera;
Nche ningwanchete Yeso ndigererie,
Mbesieyeso goika mambia bokie,

3
Mbere ang`Omonene nasabire, 
Ombwate, Onyanche nagosabire;
Osesenie abana orendete,
Nario toragwener` agwo igoro.
