113 - ONG' OKWEGEN'OKONENE
     "O For A Faith"

1
Ong` okwegen`okonene, Babisa mbamiga; 
Gotaiguswe `S`obotaka, nonya nemechando, 
Nonya n`emechando.

2
Ogotakwemurungania, `kero ki`emechando;
Korende as` emechando nobororo 
Kwegeme Nyasae.

3
Okwegena gokobara, Komwag` okogusa; 
Nakoba ase aabe, tikwoboa kende, 
Korem` Omosunte.

4
Gotagoswe n`ab`ens`eye, Nagosekwa bori 
Ogotabugwe n`ebibe, gosirigwe bori
N`ebiya bi`ens`eye.

5
`Monene ng`a okwegena, Erio kende ngecha
Ninyor` Omogok` omuya nonya nde as`ense 
Bw`enka ya igoro.
