248. Mutima Wanjye Ucururuke

1.
Mutima wanjye ucururuke, Nugir' ibiguter' impagarara, 
Wiringire Ishobora byose, N' imigambi yayo myiza. 
2.

Mutima wanjy' iby' ari byo byose, N' ubw' ibihe byagenda bihinduka, 
Imbabazi zayo ntizishira, Zihorahw' ibihe byose. 

3.
Mutima wanjye uremerewe, N' ubwo wab' utew' ubwoba n' urupfu, 
Ni wiheba wisung' Uwiteka, Agukingurir' ijuru. 

4.
Mutima wanjy' uhereye kera, Ntiwigeze ubur' umudendezo, 
Isung' Imana izakwakira, Wemere kugengwa na yo.
 
5.
Ubugingo bwiza buboneye, Bushishikarira gukor' ibyiza, 
Mukiza mfasha ngo mbugereho, Ump' umutim' utunganye.
