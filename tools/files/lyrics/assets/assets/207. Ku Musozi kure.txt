207. Ku Musozi kure

1.
Ku musozi kure har' umusaraba, 
Ubabaj' ukojej' isoni, 
Ndawukunda cyane kuko wabambweho, 
Uwacunguy' abanyabyaha. 

Gusubiramo:
Nzogeza uwo musaraba (musaraba), 
Kugez' ubwo nzat' umuruho, 
Nzikomeza ku musaraba (musaraba), 
Kugez' ubwo nzambikw' ikamba. 

2.
Mbeg' umusarab' ab' isi bahinyuye, 
Ng' uranyur' umutima wanjye!
Umwana w' intama yarets' ikuzo rye, 
awikorer' ajy' i Kalvari.
 
3.
Umusaraba wavushij' amaraso, 
Ya Yesu umwana w' Imana, 
Kuri wo nih' Umukiza yatangiye, 
Maz' ubwo mbabarirw' ibyaha.
 
4.
Kubw' umusaraba, Nzab' umunyakuri, 
Nihanganir' isoni zawo, 
Kugez' ubw' uzanjyan' iwacu mw' ijuru, 
Tunezeranw' iteka ryose.
