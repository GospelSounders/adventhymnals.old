316 - ABANG'INA BAKARETA ABANA
"When Mothers Of Salem"

1
Ker`abang`ina baretera Yes`abana, `Borokigwa`as`obotindi bakabaseria;
Yes`akarora bagotama`agateba n`obwororo, "Aban`abake nabach`ase nde."

2
`Nimbaire mbabogori` as`egekuba kiane, Mb`Omorisia bwe ching`ondi tagochiseria; Kobarang`e chinkoro chiabo mbameny` as` Obonene, "Aban` abake nabach`ase nde."

3
`Motori `Omuya Okoarigani`abana, Mbar` abange batamanyeti rieta riaye; `Motori` Omuya toigwe gotogosaba otebe, `Aban` abake nabach`ase nde.

4
Bwango tig`abanto b`ebisaku nechinse, Bach`ase Yeso batugute chisanamu; Nabarab` erio bakomanye Nyasae bw` obwanchani, Beger` Omotoria bach` as` ore.
