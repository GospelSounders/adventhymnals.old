082 - TEKOB 'EGETUNWA IGORO
"It May Not Be On The Mountain Height"


1
Tekob` egetunwa Igoro, Gose ng`umbu y`enyancha,
Gose tai oroari ime, Nyasa` Aranganerie;
Gakorangeria ng`or` obwororo, Nintamany` enchera,
Korambwat` okoboko ningende, As` origetie ngende.

Chorus
Ningende as` origetie ngende,
Bitunwa, `birubo, `nyancha;
Nintebe bun` aganetie ntebe,
Nimbe buna aganetie.

2
Nare amang`ana obwanchani, Yes` arigetie ntebe,
Gose nab` omonto` `as` ebibe, `Yongwenerete ndigie; 
`Motoria korab` Omorai one, `Nchera neb` embe,
Ninkwan` amang`an` ao amaya, Bun` origetie nintebe.

3. 
Ase nare ens`achayire, ko rigesa ndireo, 
`Se ndakore emeremo ya Yeso obambetwe; 
Kondegene as` oborai bwao, `Manyete nonyanchete, 
Ninkore obwanchani bwao obuya, Nimbe buna origetie.
