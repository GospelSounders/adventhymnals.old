348 - 'NG'ANA Y'OMONENE
"Lord Thy Word Abideth"


1
`Ng`ana y`Omonene, `Torae botambe,
Oyoriegenete, Nanyor`Omogoko.

2
`Babisa mbabao, Ntonyore chinguru,
`Ng`ana ri`okoremia, `Ret`ogotoreka.

3
K`emerind`egocha, N`aimar`atotuba,
`Ng`ana riao ribe, Eng`ay`as`enchera.

4
Ng`oranyare kwera, `Mogok`ore naki,
Oyotwanyorire, Korw`ase ring`ana?

5
`Ng`ana y`amabera, As`abare moyo,
Rikorw`oboremu, Ase abagokwa.

6
Gatwamanyire bo, Obochenu bwaye;
Togwanch`Omonene, Tobe naye rioka.
