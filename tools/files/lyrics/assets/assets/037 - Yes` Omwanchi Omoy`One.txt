037 - YES'OMWANCHIO MOY'ONE
"Jesus Lover Of My Soul"


1
Yes` omwanchi omoy`one, Ancha menye as`ore,
Merindo nemenene, nomwaga nomotindi.
Nyebise Omotoria, Goik` emechando eere,
Rokoer` orogendo As`aya Kristo ng`ira.

2
Tindi n`ogokonyeka, Tig` aye Omonene;
Tontiga inche bweka, obe ang`e botambe,
Negenire as`ore, Nyor`ogokony` as`ore
Otube omotw`one, Na orobaba rwao.

3
Kwang`isanire aye, Nas`ore bire bionsi
Abororo n`ababe, Nkobakony` ore bonsi 
Omuy` ore Nyasae. Inche tindi n`obuya 
Oyore n`obochenu, Nabwatirwe n`obobe.

4
`Buy`obonge nas`ore, korusi` ebibe bionsi 
As`ainache amaya, Norigetie onsibie
Kwabeire ase tore, Ensoko y`obogima
Obe nue asende, Emiaka na emiaka.
