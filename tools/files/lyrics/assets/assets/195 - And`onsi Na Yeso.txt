195 - AND'ONSI NA YESO
"Anywhere With Jesus"


1
And`onsi na Yeso nigo ndagende,
Karantang`an` and`onsi as`ens`eye;
Mogoko tori Monene ataiyo, 
And`onsi na Yeso tindi nche bweka.

Chorus
And`onsi, and`onsi tindi kwoboa,
And`onsi na Yeso nigo ndagende.

2
And`onsi na Yeso tindi nche bweka,
Basani mbantiga Ere noyone;
Karanda` ase chinchera chinkong`u, 
And`onsi nimosasime` Omotoria.

3
And`onsi na Yeso ningende ndare,
`Mosunte nimoinyore Omonene;
Tingosira korwa enchera yaye, 
And`onsi na Yeso nimenye n`Ere.
