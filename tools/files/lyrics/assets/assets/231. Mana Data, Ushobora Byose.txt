231. Mana Data, Ushobora Byose

1.
Mana Data, Ushobora byose, Duhe Mwami, Kurabagirana, 
Tukuririmbire tugushima, Kukw' ariw' ushobora byose.
 
2.
Watuyoboy' ibihe byashize, Tuyobore no mur' iki gihe, 
Dukingire imyambi y' umwanzi, Tuyoboreshe ijambo ryawe.
 
3.
Intambara ziraduc' intege, Tuber' ingabo idukingira, 
Duhe kwizer' imbaraga yawe, Dukeney' ubuntu bwawe Mwami. 

4.
Duhembure mur' urugendo, Tuyobore amanywa n' ijoro, 
Duhe kuguma bugufi bwawe, Guhimbazwa n' ibyawe iteka.
