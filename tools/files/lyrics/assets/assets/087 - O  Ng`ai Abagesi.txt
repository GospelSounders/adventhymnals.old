087 - O NGA'I ABAGESI
"O Where Are The Reapers"


1
Ng`ai abagesi bakogesa, Abant` abegeni
korwa ense? `Merem` ekorek` as` ekeene,
Onde tatimoka goika eere.

Chorus
Ng`ai bagesi bare bache, Basang` obonen`
enka y`erigesa `Ng` oratokonye,
kobareta,Bant` abegeni korwa ense.

2
Gend` as`ebinchera mobarigie, Babe
mbange k`abaya mbareo Barigie nchera
timotig`onde, Basangererie kogenda `goro.

3
Rigesa riarabire And`onsi, `Nse bono
neganyete rigesa Bagesi mbake,
merem`emenge, Abange mbasire rikonyorwa.

4
Bono nchwo n`emeyio mwanyabanto,
Mogese embusuro engiya Kor` omoki`
Omonene nachiche, Twegokie ner` as` enka yaye.
