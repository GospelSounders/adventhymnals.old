212. Mb' Uwawe, Mwami

1.
Mb' uwawe Mwami mbane nawe, 
Ni wow' umbumba, Jye nd' ibumba, 
Ngenz' uk' ushaka mere nkawe
Mwam' ubu ndakwiyeguriye. 

2.
Mb' uwawe Mwami mbane nawe, 
Ndondora Mwam' ungerageze, 
Unyeze nere nk' urubura, 
Njye ngusenga niyoroheje.
 
3.
Mb' uwawe Mwami mbane nawe, 
Ubu ndagusab' untabare, 
Wowe Nyir' imbaraga zose, 
Nkorah' umvure we Mukiza.
 
4.
Mb' uwawe Mwami mbane nawe, 
Nkomeza kuko nteraganwa, 
Nyuzuz' Umwuka wawe Wera, 
Ngo bose bambonemo Yesu.
