"Halleluya! We Shall Rise"


1
`S`emambia yokobokigwa, getureri nkebuge,
Ntoboke (Halleluya) Ntoboke. `Bachenu mbachenge gocha,
`mariga takobao, Ntoboke (Halleluya! `emambi eyio Natoboke) Ntoboke.

Chorus
(Ntoboke) Halleluya (Ntoboke) Amen

Ntoboke (Halleluya) Ntoboke.

3

Ntoboke (Halleluya) Ntoboke.
Morore Motori `omwanchwa, Bwerwete agankwera,

4
`Semambia yokobokigwa, Ntoumeran` amare,
Ntoboke (Halleluya) Natoboke.
Ntoirwe as`obonene` s`enk`engiy`ekomesa,
Ntoboke (Halleluya! Mambi`eyio Natoboke) Ntoboke.
