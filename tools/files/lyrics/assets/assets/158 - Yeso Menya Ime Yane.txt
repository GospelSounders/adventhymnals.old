158 - YESO MENYA IME YANE
"Live Out Thy Life Within Me"


1
Yeso menya ime yane, `Ye `mogambi omuya; 
Obe amairaneri`, as` okogania kwane; 
Yeso menya ime yane, Kor` ayorigetie; 
Nche nimboorokie, Obonene bwao.

2
Nayerure ekaru, Yasibigwe bori, 
Tig` oborabu bwao bomese korwa ime; 
Ense yonsi ekire, nonya nomobere, 
Eri `omosomb` oo atar` as` ore rioka.

3
Ebimo biaye bionsi, Biagw` eriogi riao, 
Mbire ang`e bionsi pi, Gokor` emeremo yao; 
Gose tibiri korosa, tibiri kogania, 
Nony` eubi mbibwati, biarurue pi as` ore.

4
Nenyango gekong`uswa, Teri gwanchereria, 
`Ganyet`eki_oratebe, `Ker` orayerangerie; 
Yeso menya_ime yane `ye `mogambi_omuya, 
Obe amairaneri` as` okogania kwane.
