265. Ndakwifuza Yesu Mwiza

1.
Ndifuza Yesu mwiza, Ndagukennye cyane, 
Nd' umushyitsi n' umwimukira, Singira gakondo, 
Ndifuz' urukundo, Rwawe rumpumuriza, 
Runyobora ngakomera cyane, Sintezuke. 

2.
Ndifuz' umutima wa Yesu ngo nduhuke, 
Ng' untere kumutur' imibabaro yanjye yose, 
Ndifuz' Umwuka Wera, Ngw' ambwir' icyo ndi cyo, 
arusheho kunyereka Umwana w' Intama. 

3.
Ndakwifuza Yesu, Nshaka kuzakubona, 
Ugoswe n' umukororombya ku ntebe ya cyami, 
nzahora nezeranywe n' abo wacunguye, 
Nzahora ngusingiz' iteka, Ndi kumwe nawe.
