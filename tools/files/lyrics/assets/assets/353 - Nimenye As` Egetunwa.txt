353 - NIMENYE AS'EGETUNWA
"I am Dwelling On The Mountain"


1
Nimenye as` egetunwa, As` omobaso `muya;
`Mochie bw`obuya obonge, Aase ay`omogambi;
Ens` ere n` omoik` omuya, Ebisicha bikwama,
Ase engegu y`ensoko, bitari kwoma rinde.

2
Natonywe `mache `chinsoko, Ense `nyia toramenye;
Amache ay`obogima Tonywe toisaneke;
Titokoba na erang`o, Gose `gwechanda konde
Gatwanyorire emonga, Etari gosareka.

3
Moinyore `mosalaba, `Monene abambetwe,
Ase emechando y`ense, Atworokie enchera;
Monene `torangeirie, Yoimokie `mosalaba,
Nakoroseirie `nchera, Oraike Igoro.
