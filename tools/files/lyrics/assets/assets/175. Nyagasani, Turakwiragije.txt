175. Nyagasani, Turakwiragije

1.
Nyagasani, Turakwiragije, Turagukennye cyane; 
Turagire mubwatsi butoshye, Turakwiyeguriye; 
Nyagasani, Nyagasani, Niwewaducunguye; 
Nyagasani, Nyagasani, Niwe waducunguye.

2.
Ubu tur' abawe rwose Mwami, Tureban' imbabazi; 
Turinde, Tur'umukumbi wawe, Twe guteshuk' inzira;
Nyagasani, Nyagasani, Nyamunekatwumvire; 
Nyagasani, Nyagasani, Nyamuneka twumvire.

3.
Wasezeranye k' uzatwakira, N' ubwo tur' abangizi;
Udufitiy'imbabazi nyinshi, N' ubuntu buhebuje; 
Nyagasani, Nyagasani,Turakugarukira; 
Nyagasani, Nyagasani, Turakugarukira.
