165. Ndagukunda, Mwami

1.
Ndagukunda, Mwami; Uzi ko ngukunda; 
Ndagukunda Yesu,Bihebuje rwose. 
Ndagukunda Yesu, Naw' urabizi; 
Imirimo yanjy'izabyerekana.

2.
Nishimye, Nishimye; Rwose bitavugwa. 
Ibyishimo byanjye, Ntawabirondora.
Ndareb' ubutunzi buri mw' ijuru,
Nzagabirwa nawe kubw'ubuntu bwawe.

3.
Yesu Mwami wanjye, Niwe nkesha byose; 
Ar' ugucungurwa, Arin' ubugingo. 
Nzakogez' iteka, Nkuririmbire, 
Kuk' ubuntu bwawebwandemy' umutima.

4.
Naguhwanya na nde, Mucunguzi wanjye?
Uhoran' ineza, Ntugir'impinduka. 
Nzajya nguhimbaza, Nkubahirize;
Umutima wanjye, Wuzuy'ibyishimo.
