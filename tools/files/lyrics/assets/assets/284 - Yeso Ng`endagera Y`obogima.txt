284 - YESO NG'ENDAGERA Y'OBOGIMA
"Break Thou The Bread Of Life"

1
Yeso ng`endagera Y`obogima,
Buna gwakorete ang`e` nyancha;
As`amarik`ao ninkorigie,
Nkoro nekoganetie, `Ring`ana!"

2
Seseni`ekeene namanyire,
Buna gwaseseneti `omogati;
`Bosomba bosire n`ebisibo,
Nyore omorembe ase bionsi.

3
`Mang`an`ao Moika n`obogima,
Ko tindi na nguru chi`okoigwera;
Naye ogokonywa n`obogima,
Nganetie nyigwer`amang`an`ao
