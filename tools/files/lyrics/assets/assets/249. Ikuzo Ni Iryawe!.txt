249. Ikuzo Ni Iryawe!

1
Ikuzo n' iryawe Data wa Twese, Nari nahabiye kure yawe, 
Wangejeje mu mucyo w' itangaza, Umvana mu bubata ndakira. 
Gusubiramo:
Mbeg' urukundo ruhebuje!
Nababariwe, N' inkuru nziza kuri twe. 
2
Mbeg' urukundo rutarondoreka! Rwatumye Yesu aza kunshaka. 
Nar' umuhamya sinari nkwiye, Gukira ngukesha Yesu gusa. 
3
akir' uyu mutima wanjye Mwami, Nkweguriy' imibereho yanjye, 
Wowe wanyitangiye ukankiza, Wanyejeshej' amaraso yawe. 
4
Mana yanjye nkingira ndakwinginze, Sinashobora kwirwanirira, 
Kugira ngo nzanezererw' iteka, Twishimye, Dusingiza Umwami.
