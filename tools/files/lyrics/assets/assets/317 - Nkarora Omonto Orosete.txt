317 - NKARORA OMONTO OROSETE
      "I Saw One Weary"


1
Nkarora  ase  enchera,  Omont` oyom` orosete, Akabwati`  Omosalaba  Bun` omogendi  omuya; Orosire,  nechingiki  chichire  obosio  bwaye, "Ng`ai  kwarusi` Omokia"?  agantebia "Okwegena". 

2
Na oyomo  nkamorora  Ne ritimo  na  enguba, Akarwana  nechinguru  Eri`anyore  egutwa;  Nonya orosire  mono;  Tachieti  kwemurungania, "Ng`ai kwarusi` omokia?" Agantebia "Okwegena." 

3
Nar` oyom`  otiget` abasani  Bobosae  bwaye, `Gatig`  esiko  n`emegoko  Atar`as`obokong`u, Nony`amatemw` areo  `Koroki`omosasok`  obosio, "Ng`ai kwarusi`omokia?" Agantebia "Okwegena."

4
Bun` abaeti as` orogendo  As`ense`eye yebibe, Gochaywa, `bokong`u,  nogosikwa, Nonya nas`embera, Goika  Omogambi ache `Toire  ntw`abasakore nka, `Nki  kiabaeire Omokia`?  Neke  gioka,  "Okwegena"
