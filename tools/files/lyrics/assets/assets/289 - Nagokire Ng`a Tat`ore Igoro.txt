289 - NAGOKIRE NG'A TAT'ORE IGORO
"I am So Glad"

1
Nagokire ng`a Tat`ore igoro, `Teb`obwanchani bway`as`ebuku; `Makumi` amange narorire ime, Kobu`onsi Yeso nanyanchete.

Chorus
Nagokire Yes`Anyanchete, Nanyanchete, nanyanchete; Nagokire Yes` Anyanchete, Nanyanchete mono.

2
Nonya nkomweb`erio natayaya, `Nyanchete rioka nonya ngosira; Nimotamer`er`oyonyanchete, Gankoinyora bun`er`ankwerete.

3
Onye kore gotera ndatere, `Kero namororir`Omotoria; Nakw`oko goika kare na kare,``N`ogokumia ki Yes` Anyanchete.
