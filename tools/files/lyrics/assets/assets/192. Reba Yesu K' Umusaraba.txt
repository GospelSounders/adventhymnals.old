192. Reba Yesu K' Umusaraba

1.
Reba Yesu ku musaraba, Reb' uko twatumy' agirirwa;
Huguk'araguhamagara, Ngwin' umusange.

Gusubiramo:
Duhimbaz' izina rye, Duhimbaz' izina rye,
Yaducunguj' amaraso ye, Yes' ahimbazwe!

2.
Yesu yambatuye mu byaha, Atura mu mutima wanjye; 
Niw'uwuzanamw ihumure; Yes' ahimbazwe!

3.
Niwe Sokw' inyezahw ibyaha; Narayiyuhagije ndera; 
Nzi yukw'ineza y' izaramba; Yes' ahimbazwe!

4.
Muze kw' isoko y' agakiza; Maze muyiyuhagiremo;
Irabezahw'ibyaha byose; Yes' ahimbazwe!
