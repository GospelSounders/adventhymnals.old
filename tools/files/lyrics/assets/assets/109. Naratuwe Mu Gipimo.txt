109. Naratuwe Mu Gipimo

1.
Umucamanza n' agera, Akarondor' ibyacu, 
Tuzamera nk'izahabu, Cyangwa s' inkamb' itabwa?

Gusubiramo:
Naratuwe mu gipimo,
Nsangwa ntashyitse;
Naratuwe n' ijambo rye,
Nsangwa ntashyitse.

2.
Mbese tuzumv' ishimwe ngo: Nuko, Wakoze neza; 
Cyangw' itekaryo gupfa ngo: mwasanzwe mudashyitse?

3.
Nimutyo twite kw' ijwi rye, Igihe kikiriho. 
Turekegutindiganya, Tumaramaze rwose.
