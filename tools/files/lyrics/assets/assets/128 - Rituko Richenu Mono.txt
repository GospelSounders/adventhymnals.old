128 - RITUKO RICHENU MONO
"Delightful Day, Best Gift Of Heaven"


1
Rituko richenu mono, Riatongetwe na Nyasae, Totimoke
rituko riaye. Natosesenigwe mono.

2
Twatimokir` Esabato, Twagokire gosasima; N`ogotera
tomobake, Ase obwanchani bwaye.

3
Tobake Nyasae mono, Ase Yeso Omotoria; Ase amanyinga
aye, Twanyorire okwabera.

4
`Kina ki`ebibe nogokwa, Atokweret` Omotoria; Tobwat`
amachiko Aye, Titosari` amang`an` Aye.
