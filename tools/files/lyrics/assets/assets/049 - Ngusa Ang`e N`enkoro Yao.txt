049 - NG'USA ANG'E N'ENKORO YAO
"Nearer Still Nearer"



1
Ngusa ang`e n`enkoro Yao; 
Ngusa `Motoria aye omuya; 
Mbeke as`egekuba kiao, 
Onkenge ontimokie as`ore 
Onkenge ontimokie as`ore.

2
Ngusa ang`e, tindi na kende;
Ndaretere Yeso Omogambi;
Tatig` omoy` one omobe`
Nsibie bono n`amanyinga ao` 
Nsibie bono n`amanying`a ao.

3
Ng`usa ang`e, imbe oyoo; 
Natigir` ebibe n`okomocha;
`Mogoko bw`ense, n`ogwetogia
Ing`a Yeso, oria onkwerete, 
Ing`a Yeso, oria onkwerete.

4
Ng`usa ang`e kond` omogima;
Ntenene as` egetare kiane; 
Ase emiaka eria yonsi, 
Ong`use ang`e n`Aye `Motoria, 
Ong`use ang`e n`Aye `Motoria.
