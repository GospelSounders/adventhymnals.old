246 - YESU NGOCHICH'ARE
"He's Coming Once Again"

1
Yeso ngochich` are, kwoyi` abanto baye, Abachenu baye bonsi, Baikarans` as`are; Rigereria_Igoro, Goka na gotera, Nache n`obonene bwaye, Yeso Omogambi.

2
Ense netengeche igoro neete, Omobe ng`ai-orabe ase rituk`erio?
Botamero mboiyo, nonya n`oboigamo, `Ndamwamu erorekane, Kamaber` aerire.

3
Amais` ay`agwoka, Nasambe ababe, Onde tagwator`erieta, ria Yes`eker`ekio; Y`okogecha, taiyo, As` Abakrist` erio, Ogosaba n`ebirero Nabibe bos`erio.
