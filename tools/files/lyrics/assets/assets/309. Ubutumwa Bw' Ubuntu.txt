309. Ubutumwa Bw' Ubuntu

1.
Ubutumwa b' ubuntu bwanyuz' umutima, 
Imana yadutangiy' Umwana wayo. 

Gusubiramo:
Ng' umwizera wes' adapf' 
Ahubw' ahabw' ubugingo, 
Ahubw' ahabw' ubugingo bw' iteka.
 
2.
Nkuko Mose yahanits' inzoka ku giti, 
Niko Yesu yamanitswe ngo tubeho. 

3.
Numvise yuk' ukor' ibyaha wes' azapfa, 
None ndahabutse kubw' ijambo ryawe.
 
4.
Yesu ntabwo yaje kuducirahw' iteka, 
Ahubwo yaj' atuzaniy' agakiza.
 
5.
Mwami ndakwinginz' ongera kwizera kwanjye, 
Nakir' iby' ubutumwa bwawe bumbwira.
