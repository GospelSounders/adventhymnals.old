354 - OYO, NERE, NGOIK'ARE
      "Lo He Comes"


1
Oyo nere, ngoik` are, Oria orwet` ense;
`Malaika baimokobe, Koirana naende;
Haleluya! Rituko riaye ng`ang`e.
Haleluya! Rituko riaye ng`ang`e.

2
Intwe twensi, tomorore, Kagoika n`amare;
`Banto babe bairoke, Mbamokorete bobe;
Nabarer, Bakomorora ense;
Nabarere, Bakomorora ense.

3
Rituko erio agocha, Toumeran` Igoro;
Na barabwo babokire, Nbwo nabaonchoke;
`Banto `bande, nogokwa baganyete;
`Banto `bande, nogokwa baganyete.

4
Amakweri, n`oborwaire, Nabiere kegima;
Obwanchani n`omogoko, Nabomenye as`ense;
Haleluya! Tonyore omorembe;
Haleluya! Tonyore omorembe.

5
Intwe twensi natomenye, As`obosio bwao;
Chinguru chionsi nobwate, Obutore ekina;
Nchuo `Monene! Intwe abanto bao!
Nchuo `Monene! Intwe abanto bao!
