327. Nkunda Igitabo Cyiza

1.
Nkund' igitabo cyera cy' Imana, Nicyo kindutir' ibindi byose, 
Kinyigish' ibyiza byo mw' ijuru, Nkameny' inzira y' agakiza.
 
2.
Gitabo cy' ingenzi muri wowe, Mpabon' ubwiza bw' Umwami wanjye, 
Ibyanditswe byawe binyigisha, Iby' umunezero nzagira. 

3.
Ariko igihe nkiri mw' isi, Umbwir' iby' urukundo rw' Imana, 
Nkarushaho gusobanukirwa, N' umunezero wo mw' ijuru.
 
4.
Ni wowe werekan' imigambi, Yose ya Yes' Umukiza wanjye, 
Muri wowe niho har' ubwenge, N' ubuhanga butarondorwa. 

5.
Ur' umucy' umurikir' ab' isi, Ngo bave mu mwijima w' ibyaha, 
Ni wow' umurikir' imitima, Y' abahabye bagahabuka.
