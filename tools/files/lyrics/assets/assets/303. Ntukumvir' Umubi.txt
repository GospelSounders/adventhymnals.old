303. Ntukumvir' Umubi

1.
Ntukumvir' umubi ujy' agushuka, 
Umwumviye wese ab' acumuye, 
Wamutsinda none kubw' Umukiza, 
Byagufash' ubundi kujy' umunesha. 

Gusubiramo:
Saba Yes' agufashe, Kand' aguhumurize, 
Yemeye kukurinda, Azatum' Utsinda. 

2.
Wirind' ubucuti bw' abantu babi, 
Bakunda kukoshya gukor' ibibi, 
Ntukavug' ijambo ritey' isoni, 
Ujye wubaha izina ry' Imana. 

3.
Ujy' ugira neza, Ufash' abandi, 
Wirinde kubeshya, Ahubw' ujy' uba, 
Inyangamugayo kukw' iby' aribyo, 
Bikundwa na Yesu, Uzagufasha. 

4.
Unesha Satani yandikiwe ko, 
Azahabw' ikamba n' Umukiza we, 
Tugir' intege nke, Ariko Yesu, 
Niwe Mutabazi, Udushoboza.
