144 - NKAIGWA YESO OGOKWANA
"I heard The Voice Of Jesus Say"


1
Nkaigwa Yeso ogokwana "Ninchwo otimoke; 
Ikaransa kwarosire, Bweegek` ase nde." 
Inkamochiera ndosete, Nde n`omoichano! 
Akang`a obotimoko, Ere ongokirie.

2
Nkaigwa Yeso ogokwana, "Nigo nkorua bosa; 
Amache ya Obogima, Yanywe obe moyo".
Ngacha ase Yeso nkanywa `Nsoko y`obogima;
Erang`o yan` egasira, Nigo nche nde moyo.

3
Nkaigwa Yeso ogokwana, "Ninche Omobaso; 
Ondigererie Orabe, Orabe botambe". 
Nkamorigereria Yeso `kang` eng`enang`eni 
Nintare as`oborabu, Nkore orogendo.
