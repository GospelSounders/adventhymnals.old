304. Nzabana N' Umwami

1.
Nzabana n' Umwam' ibihe bidashira, 
Iryo jambo rivuga ngo: Nzatur' ibudapfa,
 
Gusubiramo:
Iwacu, Iwacu, 
Ngeze hafi yaho.
 
2.
Mu rugo rw' Umwami ndahararikira, 
Kwizera niko kunyerek' amarembo yaho, 
N' ubwo njya ncogora s' ukutahashaka, 
Iyo gakondo y' abera ni Yerusalemu.
 
3.
Njya ngotwa n' ibyaha, Nkumva ndacogoye, 
Nka bur' aho mfata nka ya numa yo mu nkuge, 
Ibicu byeyutse, Umukororombya, 
W' ihumure urasira mu mutima wanjye.
 
4.
Nkund' izo ndirimbo zihora mw' ijuru, 
Izo mw' isi yacu ni nk' indimi z' i Babeli, 
Harihw' icyo nkunda, Nukw' abazazuka, 
Bazongera kubana na Yesu, Yee, Amina.
