216. Nshikamye Ku Mazerano

1.
Nshikamye ku masezerano meza, Y' Umukiza wanjye, Mporaningiza
Nzahora musingiza, Ndanguruye, Nshikamye kumasezerano. 
Nta buhungiro nta bwugamo, Bw' umuzajoro nk'uyu. 
Mbese ko tugiye gupfa, Nawe wiryamiye?
Dor'umuraba waby' icyago, Tugiye kurengerwa. 

Gusubiramo:
S.:
Nshikamye ku masezerano, Y'Umucunguzi wanjye, 
Nshikamye ku masezerano, Y'Umucunguzi.
A.,T. na B.:
Nshikamye ku masezerano, 
Masezerano y' Umucunguzi wanjye, 
Nshikamye kumasezerano, Y' Umucunguzi. 
Masezerano y' Umucunguzi;

2.
Nshikamye ku masezerano meza, N' ubwo naba mbuz' epfo naruguru, 
Ijambo ry' Imana rimpa gutsinda, Nshikamye kumasezerano. 

3.
Nshikamye ku masezerano meza, Kuko nejeshejw' amaras' akiza,
Niy' atuma mpora mu mudendezo, Nshikamye ku masezerano. 

4.
Nshikamye ku masezerano meza, Nkomezwa n' umuruna w'urukundo, 
Mpora neshesh' inkota y' Umwuka we, Nshikamye kumasezerano. 

5.
Nshikamye ku masezerano meza, Numv' Umwuka we Wer'ampamagara, 
Ngo ngume mu Mukiza nkesha byose, Nshikamye kumasezerano.
