94. Nkunda Guterana Kw' Isabato

1.
Nkunda guterana kw' Isabato,
Kwig' ijambo ry' Imana.
Teraniro ryiza ry' abigishwa,
Undutira byose.

Gusubiramo:
Huriro (huriro) rishimwa (rishimwa),
Huriro (huriro) rishirnwa (rishimwa).
Teraniro ryiza ry' abigishwa,
Undutira byose.

2.
Niho namenyey' iby' ubugingo, 
Mbon' uburyo nihana,
Nitoraniriz' ibitunganye,
Nibyo binnezeza.

3.
Aho niho Yesu yantoreye, 
Ambwiz' ijwi rye ryiza.
Ubwo nibwo namwitoraniye, 
Mw' ihuriro ryiza.
