64. S' Uhagarik' Ik' Umutima?

1
S' uhagarikiy' ik' umutima? Bibwire Yesu! Bibwire Yesu!
Ubabajwe no kubur' ituza? Ihererane Yesu!

Gusubiramo:
Bibwire Yesu, Bibwire Yesu!
Niwe nshutiy' ukuri;
Ntugir' ind' inshut' ihwanye nawe, 
Jy' ubana nawecyane.

2
Mbes' ub' urabogoz' amarira? Bibwire Yesu! Bibwire Yesu!
Utotezwa n' ibyaha wahishe? Ihererane Yesu!

3
Ujy' utinya guterwa n' ibyago? Bibwire Yesu! Bibwire Yesu!
Mbes' ub' uraganyishwa n' iby' ejo? Ihererane Yesu!

4
Mbes' ujy ugir' ubwoba bwo gupfa? Bibwire Yesu! BibwireYesu!
Mbes' urashak' ingoma ya Kristo? Ihererane Yesu!
