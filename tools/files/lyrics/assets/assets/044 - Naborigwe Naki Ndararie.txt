044 - NABORIGWE NAKI NDARARIE
"Redeemed , How I Love To Proclaim It"



1
Naborigwe naki ndararie! As` amanyinga y` engondi,
Koborigw` as` amaber` aye, Nabeir` omwan` oye bori.

Chorus
Koborigwa! As` amanyinga y` eng`ondi
Koborigwa! Nabeir` omwan` oye bori.

2
Koborigwa ngok` ase Yeso, Kobua buna ndakwane;
Oborabe bw` okoba kwaye, Nabomenye nainche bori.

3
Ndengereri` Omoboria one, Morengererie botambe;
Nter` ekiagera tingokira, Eganga neb` obwanchani.

4
Nindore as` obuya bwaye, `Ngokiri` as` amachik` aye;
`Yokondenda ker` ase ndabe, Kong` amatera botuko.

5
Inche nagacheirwe egutwa, Yabekirwe ang`e igoro;
`Kero toraikeranigwe, Erio as` are nimbeo.
