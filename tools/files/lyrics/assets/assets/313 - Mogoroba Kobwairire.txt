313 - MOGOROBA KOBWAIRIRE
"At Even When The Sun Was Set"


1
Mogoroba kobwairire, Abarwaire bachire, Ang`e naye Omonene, Basabire obakonye.

2
Naintwe bono mogoroba, Twachire n`obob`obonge, Nonya titokorochi aye, Twamanyire nao ore.

3
Omotoria seri`obwoba, `Bande nabarwaire bare, `Bande mbaragwancha buya, Abande babosiririe,

4
`Nde tari nogotimoka, Taiy`otakoreti ebibe, `Bakogokorera buya Beigwa mbanyabibe bare.

5
`Koboko kwao nkwa nguru, Ng`ana yao tekogwa bosa, Igwa bono mogoroba, Ase obuya togwenie.
