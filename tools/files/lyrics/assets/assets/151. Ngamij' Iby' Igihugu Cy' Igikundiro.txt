151. Ngamij' Iby' Igihugu Cy' Igikundiro

1.
Ngamij' iby' igihugu cy' igikundiro; Nzageray' izubarirenze. 
Ariko s' ikamba Yes' azamperayo, Azaritakahw inyenyeri?

Gusubiramo:
Mbes' ikamba ryanjye Yes' azamperayo,
Azaritakahw inyenyeri (inyenyeri)? 
Ni ngir' amahirwe, Nkabana naYesu, 
Azaritakahw' inyenyeri (inyenyeri)?

2.
Reka nkore, nsenge, Mbiheshejwe na we, Mugarurir' abazimiye.
N' agaruka kuri wa munsi w' ubwiza, Nzatakwehw' inyenyeri nyinshi.

3.
Mbeg' umunezer' uhebuje nzagira, Ni mwerek' abo nakijije,
Mfite n' ibyishimo bitavugwa, Mbonye inyenyeri kw' ikamba ryanjye.
