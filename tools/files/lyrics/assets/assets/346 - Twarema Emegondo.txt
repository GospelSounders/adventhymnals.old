346 - TWAREMA EMEGONDO
"We Plough The Fields"


1
Twarema emegondo, Na koyebusura,
Nyasae orw`embura, N`oborendi obuya;
Areta riberera, Areta `mbokendu,
`Mbe`engiya n`omobaso, N`embura y`okwamia

Chorus
Ebiegw`ebiya, Nigoro bikorwa,
Mbuya, Mbuy` as`Omonene, `s`obwanchani bwaye.

Risimeka, rigesa, `Bogima, `ndagera;
Ntobwati ebitorarwe, As`obwancham obo, 
Ko ekioganeti`Aye, Ne chinkoro chiato.

3
Titokoba `abande, Bun`Er`atoeire! 
Tobakony`abanto, Kogenda Igoro;
Torw`esadaka yaito, Yangoyi `abotomwa,
Bwa Kristo im`as`ense, N`ogotoreka bo.
