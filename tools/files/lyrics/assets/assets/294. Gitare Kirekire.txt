294. Gitare Kirekire

1.
Gitare kirekire njya mpungiraho, 
Ni wow' umutima wanjye wifuza, 
Nd' umunyantege nke nshaka kukwisunga, 
Wowe Gitare cyanjye, Nkwihishemo.
 
Gusubiramo:
Nkwihishemo, Nkwihishemo, 
Wowe Gitare cyanjye, Nkwihishemo. 

2.
Ngiz' imibabaro ku manywa y' ihangu, 
Nizingiweho n' ibigeragezo, 
Ndengewe n' imiraba yiyungikanya, 
Wowe Gitare cyanjye, Nkwihishemo. 

3.
Umwanz' anyibasiye ndi ku rugamba, 
Ni wowe mpungiraho nkaruhuka, 
Iyo nibasiwe n' ibigeragezo, 
Niwe nihishamo, Gitare cyanjye.
