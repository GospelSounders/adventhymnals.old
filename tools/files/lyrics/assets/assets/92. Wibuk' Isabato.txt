92. Wibuk' Isabato

1.
Wibuk' Isabato, Umwami yahiriye.
N' umuns' urut' iyindi, N'umuns' unezeza. 
Turuhukamw' imirimo, Tubwirwamw' iby' ijuru.
Niwomucy' umurika, Uva ku Mwam' Imana.

Gusubiramo:
Utuzanir' amahoro, Sabato nziza rwose.
Utuzanir' amahoro, Sabato nziza.

2.
Jya wez' Isabato, Maz' uramy' uwo Mwami,
Wabwiy' intumwa z'ati: Ndi nyir' ubugingo. 
Niba twicisha bugufi, Dukorer' umukiza,
Tuzajyanwa kw' isoko, Y' amazi y' ubugingo.

3.
Twishime twezwemo, Turirimbire Yesu, 
Inshuti yacu nziza,Tumutekereze. 
Mukiza mwiz' udukunda, Isezerano ryawe,
Ng' uzaba muritwebwe, Niryo ritunezaza.
